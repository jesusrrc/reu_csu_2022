Use of cookies.


As do most websites, our websites use cookies. Our apps may also use cookies. A cookie is a small data file that can be sent to your browser or other device to be stored automatically on your computer or mobile device. Cookies are commonly used to track your visits to a site or your use of apps so you dont have to log in on every page and to analyze how you use the site or app. Among other things, this can allow website operators and app providers to serve you better. It may be possible to set your Internet browser or mobile device to reject cookies but this may affect your ability to use some parts of websites or Apps.


It is possible to switch off cookies by setting your browser preferences. Turning cookies of may result in a loss of functionality when using our website.
