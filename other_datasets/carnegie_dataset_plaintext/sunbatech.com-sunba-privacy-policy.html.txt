SUNBA TECHNOLOGY CO., LTD PRIVACY POLICY.


Your privacy is recognized as the most important for Sunba Technology Co., Ltd. Please read the terms and condition set forth below on how we will use your personal information collected on this website before submitting anything on this website. If you do not agree with any of the listed conditions, do not access or otherwise use this site or any information contained on this site. Your use of the site shall be deemed to be your consent to each of the terms set forth below.


COLLECTION AND USE OF INFORMATION (WEBSITE ACCESS).


You may be asked to provide a username, email, password and a name to UNIQUELY identify yourself in this website with the following practices: manage your account, use the shopping cart, enter the forum or communicate with Sunba Technology. This information is only used to provide access to the website in the above practices. Sunba will not sell, share, or rent your personal information to any third party or use your e-mail address for unsolicited mail unless requested by law or with your consent.


COLLECTION AND USE OF INFORMATION (SHOPPING).


Apart from your unique identifier on this website (a username, email, password and a name), to process transactions, sometimes additional information is requested by our 3rd party payment processing provider such as Paypal. Sunba Technology will NOT collect any payment information from you. You should also NOT disclose any payment information on this website other than to Paypal. However, the general region you come from (example: country or state, with NO specific address info) as well as your time of access (example: Tuesday 2: 30 pm) on this website may be collected and used for improving advertisement model.


COLLECTION AND USE OF INFORMATION (COMMUNICATION WITH SUNBA).


Except for your email address, Sunba will only be using the information you provided such as product reviews, feedback, comments and encouragements as objective information to help us develop, deliver, and improve our products, services, content, and advertising. We may also use the information that you provide to respond to your requests, support your use of Sunba products and services, or customize future shopping for you. If any relevant policy or terms and conditions are changed, your information may be used to get in time notification from Sunba.


COOKIE AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGY.


This website uses cookies to improve the user experience and gather information such as browser type and operating system, tracking the number of visitors to the website, and understanding how visitors use the website. Note personal information cannot be collected via cookies and other tracking technology; however, if you previously provided personally identifiable information, cookies may be tied to such information. If you want to disable cookies, you can do so by modifying the browser settings. Check with your browser provider to find out how to disable cookies.


DATA SAFETY COMMITMENTS.


The online services offered by Sunba and its affiliates use encryption on web pages where personal information is collected (for example, credit card and other payment information). To use these services, we recommend you use an SSL-enabled browser such as Internet Explorer, Firefox, Chrome, or Safari. Doing so protects the confidentiality of your personal information while its transmitted over the Internet. Internet data transmissions can always be exposed to a certain level of risk. Sunba is committed to protecting your privacy with administrative and technical measures against any data loss and theft. However, with the existing security technology, Sunba cannot guarantee that your personal information is under absolute security. You acknowledge that Sunba is not liable for any losses with any personal information revealed or stolen due to security issues not caused by Sunba.
