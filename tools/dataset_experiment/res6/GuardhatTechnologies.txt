we at guardhat inc (“company”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.
if you have any questions or concerns about our policy or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at  our products or services have been selected by your employer or by a company to which you provide services  to collect and manage data about your workplace activities.
in this document, for the sake of simplicity we use the term “employer” to refer to the organization that elected to use our products and services and who has directed us to provide them to you, whether or not that organization is your actual employer (for example, if you are an independent contractor, or if you work for a different company, but are performing services on site for an organization that uses our products and services, we refer to that organization as your “employer” even if, strictly speaking, it is not).
when you visit our website  use our products or services, or install our mobile applications, you trust us with your personal information.
we take your privacy very seriously.
in this privacy policy, we seek to explain to you in the clearest way possible what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.
we hope you take some time to read through it carefully, as it is important.
this privacy policy applies to all information collected through our website, our products and services and our mobile applications (we refer to them collectively in this privacy policy as the "services").

1.
what information do we collect.
in short.
we collect personal information that you or your employer provide to us, as well as information gathered through the use of our services.
if you contact us through our website or via other services (such as via email, messaging applications or social media), we will collect the information you provide through that communication (such as your username, email address, phone number or social media handle).
when you use our services, we will collect the information you or your employer supply to us in order to associate information about your use of our services with you, and the information gathered through the devices that you or your employer link with our services.
for example, from either you or your employer, we will collect your name or employee id number, and from the devices we will collect the information the particular device is designed to collect, which may include geolocation information, environmental information, activity tracking information, audiovisual information and information about health or safety incidents you experience.
in some instances, you or your employer can choose to link our devices with third party devices, enabling our device to collect information from the third party device.
the information gathered by the third party device is determined by the third party, not by us, and you should consult their privacy policies to understand what data they collect and how they use it.
when you install our mobile applications, you will be asked to provide logon information that your employer has provided us, in order to verify your identity and to associate the information we gather about you with the correct employer.
in addition, these applications collect information gathered from the sensors in the mobile device, such as geolocation information and information collected through motion sensors.
they are also equipped to collect messaging and audiovisual information recorded using the applications.
finally, the mobile applications can also collect information from devices that are linked to them, including devices we offer and certain third-party devices.
2.
how do we use your information.
in short.
we use your information to help you and your employer gather data about your activities and environment, to enable us to provide the services we offer (including to assess the how our services are used and to help us further develop our services over time) and to fulfill our legal obligations.
our services are designed to enable employers to monitor workplace safety and to gather analytics about workplace behavior.
we use the information gathered through our services for those purposes.
we also use your information for the purpose of fulfilling our contractual obligations to you and to your employer, and as needed to fulfill our legal obligations.
we use the information we collect or receive.
to send safety alerts.
we will use the information we gather through our services to send real-time safety alerts to the frontline worker and their supervisor and/or support personnel.
to analyze workplace data.
we will use the information we collect about you to help your employer analyze workplace practices and safety, including to gather information about specific incidents.
we also use and store this information in aggregated and anonymized form that is not associated with individual end users and does not include personal information.
to send you marketing and promotional communications.
we do not use personal information from guardhat offerings for marketing purposes, however, we and/or our third-party marketing partners will use the personal information you send to us via our website for our marketing purposes.
you can opt-out of our marketing emails at any time (see the "what are your privacy rights" below).
to send administrative information to you.
we will use your personal information to send you product, service and new feature information and/or information about changes to our terms, conditions, and policies.
to manage your account.
we will use your information to manage your account with us.
to request feedback.
we may, from time to time, use your information to request feedback and to contact you about your use of our services.
to respond to user inquiries and offer support to users.
we will use your information as needed to respond to your inquiries and solve any potential issues you might have with the use of our services.
to improve our services.
we will use your information to assess how our services are used, to troubleshoot service issues and to help us decide how to improve our services.
3.
will your information be shared with anyone.
in short.
we will share the information about you that we gather through the services with your employer, with our personnel who have a need to access such information, and with our third party service providers.
in addition, your employer may share this information with others, or may instruct us to do so.
beyond that, we only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services, to protect your rights, or to fulfill business obligations.
we will share the information we collect through our services with your employer, and, if your employer directs us to do so, we will also share this information with other third parties.
please contact your employer to understand how and when it will use this information, and when it may direct us to share that information with third parties.
we will provide access to your information to our personnel who have a need to access such information.
we use third-party data storage providers to store the data we collect through the services, so the information we collect about you will be shared with those third parties.
those third parties are not permitted to use identifiable information about you for any purpose other than providing services to us.
we may allow selected third parties to use tracking technology on the services, which will enable them to collect data about how you interact with the services over time.
this information may be used to, among other things, analyze and track data, determine the popularity of certain content and better understand online activity.
unless described in this policy, we do not share, sell, rent or trade any of your information with third parties for their promotional purposes.
in addition, we may process or share data in the following circumstances.
if you have given us specific consent to use your personal information in a specific purpose.
where we have entered into a contract with you, we will process your personal information to fulfill the terms of our contract.
we will disclose your information where we are legally required to do so in order to comply with applicable law, governmental requests, a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process, such as in response to a court order or a subpoena (including in response to public authorities to meet national security or law enforcement requirements).
we will disclose your information where we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding potential violations of our policies, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the safety of any person and illegal activities, or as evidence in litigation in which we are involved.
we may share or transfer your information in connection with, or during negotiations of, any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business to another company.
we may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit the our website.
these companies may use information about your use of the website and other websites contained in web cookies and other tracking technologies in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
we may share your information with our affiliates, in which case we will require those affiliates to honor this privacy policy.
affiliates include our parent company and any subsidiaries, joint venture partners or other companies that we control or that are under common control with us.
when we share your personal information as set out in this privacy policy, your personal information may be transferred to or from the united states or other locations outside of your state, province, country or other governmental jurisdiction where privacy laws may not be as protective as those in your jurisdiction.
we will take steps that are reasonably necessary to ensure that your personal information is treated securely and appropriately safeguarded in accordance with this privacy policy and applicable data protection or privacy laws.
please contact us at if you want further information on the specific mechanism used by us when transferring your personal information out of the eea/uk.
4.
how long do we keep your information.
in short.
we will keep your information for one year, unless otherwise directed by your employer or required by applicable law.
we retain information we collect as long as it is necessary and relevant to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy and to the extent permitted by applicable legal requirements.
as a general matter, we retain your information for up to one year after collecting it.
your employer can direct us to keep information for longer or shorter periods, and we will honor that direction unless legally required otherwise.
in addition, if we are required to retain your information for a longer period under applicable law, then we will do so.
when we no longer intend to retain your information, we will either delete or anonymize it, or, if this is not possible (for example, because your personal information has been stored in backup archives), then we will securely store your personal information and isolate it from any further processing until deletion is possible.
5.
how do we keep your information safe.
in short.
we aim to protect your personal information through a system of organizational and technical security measures.
we have implemented what we believe to be appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process, and we partner with security providers who alert us to any anomalies or security concerns related to our services.
our third-party service providers are required to implement security measures as well.
your employers are responsible for protecting the information we provide to them, so we suggest you contact them to understand how they seek to protect it.
please also remember that we cannot guarantee that the internet itself is 100% secure.
although we will do our best to protect your personal information, transmission of personal information to and from our services is at your own risk.
6.

we do not knowingly collect data from or market to children under 18 years of age.
our services are designed to be used by adults in their work environments.
we do not knowingly solicit data from or market to children under 18 years of age.

if you become aware of any data we have collected from children under age 18, please contact us at .

our website and software applications do not currently use cookies, but might do so in the future to better understand how our users interact with our content and improve user experience.

for more information on cookies generally, you can check .

8.
termination of accounts.
in short.
your employer can direct us to terminate your account at any time.
we will terminate your account and delete the information we have about you only at the direction of your employer.
if you would like your account terminated and/or the information we have about you deleted, you may ask your employer to direct us to do so.
9.
controls for do-not-track features.
in short.
we do not currently honor “do not track” functionalities.
when and if we begin using tracking cookies, we will honor industry-standardized anti-tracking functionalities, but doing so may affect our ability to provide our services, including the safety offerings.
most web browsers and some mobile operating systems and mobile applications include a do-not-track (“dnt”) feature or setting you can activate to signal your privacy preference not to have data about your online browsing activities monitored and collected.
no uniform technology standard for recognizing and implementing dnt signals has been finalized.
when and if we begin using tracking cookies, and if an anti-tracking functionalities become standardized in the industry, we will honor anti-tracking requests through those functionalities.
however, as tracking is an important element of some of our services and offerings, disabling that tracking may affect our ability to provide our services, including some of our safety offerings.

this policy describes our privacy practices with respect to the services, but does not apply to these third party products and services.
for information on their privacy practices, please consult with the third parties.
