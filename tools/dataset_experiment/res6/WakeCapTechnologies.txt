privacy policy.
1.
introduction.
1.
1 wakecap provides its website visitors with access to information, updates and certain products and services (the “services”) through this website (the “wakecap website”).
1.
2 this privacy policy details the wakecap practices in connection with the collection and use of information and the circumstances when wakecap may disclose such collected information.
1.
3 by visiting the wakecap website, you acknowledge that you accept the practices and policies as detailed in this privacy policy and you consent to the collection, use and sharing of your information by wakecap as detailed in this privacy policy.
1.
4 this privacy policy deals with the treatment of the personal information of website visitors to the wakecap website.
this privacy policy does not extend to the practices of companies or people that are not controlled or managed by wakecap.
2.
commitment.
2.
1 wakecap is committed to creating a safe and secure online environment and safeguarding the privacy of its website visitors.
3.
collecting information.
3.
1 website visitors may access the wakecap website while remaining anonymous and without disclosing any personal information.
3.
2 during a visit to the wakecap website, wakecap may collect, store and use the following kinds of personal information from website visitors, including but not limited to.
(a) information about your computer and about your visits to and use of the wakecap website (including your ip address, geographical location, browser type and version, operating system, referral source, length of visit, page views and website navigation paths).
(b) information that you provide to wakecap for the purpose of subscribing to our email notifications and/or newsletters (including your name and email address).
(c) information that you provide to wakecap when using the services on the wakecap website, or information that is generated in the course of the use of those services.
(d) information contained in or relating to any communication that you send to wakecap or send through the wakecap website.
(e) any other personal information that you choose to send to wakecap.
4.
using personal information.
4.
1 personal information submitted to wakecap through the wakecap website will be used for the purposes specified in this privacy policy or on the relevant pages of the wakecap website.
4.
2 wakecap may use your personal information to.
(a) contact you.
(b) administer the wakecap website and business.
(c) enable your use of the services available on the wakecap website.
(d) send you non-marketing commercial communications.
(e) send you email notifications that you have specifically requested.
(f) send you the wakecap email newsletter, if you have requested it (such circulation can be terminated at any time at your request and notification to wakecap).
(g) provide statistical information only to third parties about wakecap users.
(h) address any queries and/or complaints made by or about you relating to the wakecap website.
(i) maintain wakecap website security and prevent fraud.
4.
3 wakecap will not, without your express consent, supply your personal information to any third party for the purpose of their or any other third party’s direct marketing.
5.
disclosing personal information.
5.
1 wakecap may disclose your personal information to any of its employees, officers, insurers, professional advisers, agents, suppliers or subcontractors insofar as reasonably necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy policy.
5.
2 wakecap may disclose your personal information to any wakecap affiliate (which shall mean for the purposes of this privacy policy any individual or corporation, partnership, trust, incorporated or unincorporated association, joint venture, limited liability company, or joint stock company that, directly or indirectly through one or more intermediaries, controls or is controlled by or is under common control with such individual or corporation, partnership, trust, incorporated or unincorporated association, joint venture, limited liability company, or joint stock company) insofar as reasonably necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy policy.
5.
3 wakecap may disclose your personal information.
(a) to the extent that wakecap is required to do so by law.
(b) in connection with any ongoing or prospective legal proceedings.
(c) in order to establish, exercise, protect or defend the legal rights (including providing information to others for the purposes of fraud prevention and reducing credit risk) of wakecap.
(d) to the purchaser (or prospective purchaser) of any business or asset that wakecap are (or are contemplating) selling.
5.
4 except as provided in this privacy policy, wakecap will not provide your personal information to third parties.
6.

6.

and.

10.
your rights.
10.
1 you may instruct wakecap to provide you with any personal information wakecap may hold about you.

11.
updating information.
11.
1 please let wakecap know if the personal information that wakecap holds about you needs to be corrected or updated.
12.
cookies.
12.
1 the wakecap website uses cookies.
12.
2 a cookie is a file containing an identifier that is sent by a web server to a web browser and is stored by the browser.
the identifier is then sent back to the server each time the browser requests a page from the server.
12.
3 cookies do not typically contain any personal information that may personally identify a user, but personal information that wakecap stores about you may be linked to the information stored in and obtained from cookies.
12.
4 most browsers allow you to refuse to accept cookies.
13.
child users.
13.
1 protecting the privacy of young children is especially important to wakecap.
wakecap does not knowingly collect or maintain personal information from persons under 13 years for age (“child users”).
the wakecap website is not directed to child users.
if wakecap becomes aware of personal information that has been collected from a child user without verified parental consent, wakecap will delete such information.
