privacy policy.
secure meters respects the personal information data entrusted to us and is fully committed to protecting and respecting your privacy and confidentiality through lawful, fair and transparent practices, complying with the requirements of the general data protection regulation (gdpr) and applicable data protection laws whereever it does business.
what personal information or data is collected from you and how we use it.
in order to keep our website relevant, easy to use, up-to-date and to develop a better user experience we use google analytics services to help us understand how people use our site.
the information, we collect through google analytics is anonymous and does not contain personally identifiable information.
since, we use google analytics services to collect information, google analytics may be collecting cookies from our website to provide us information services.
we suggest you visit to google analytics privacy policy  to find out more information about their cookie use.
when you visit our website  , you may give us information about you by filling in various forms.
the information you give us may include your name, address, email address and phone number.
we will use this information for lawful purposes and ensure your privacy is maintained as mandated by the gdpr.
we have implemented appropriate security measures to safegaurd your information against loss, misuse or any alteration of the information you have provided to us on .
our website  may contain links to external websites.
if you follow a link to any external websites, please note that these websites have their own privacy or cookie policies and that we do not accept any responsibility or liability for these policies.
please check these policies before you submit any personal data to these websites.
we may revise this privacy policy from time to time, in our sole discretion, and the most current version will always be available on our website.
we encourage you to review this privacy policy regularly for any changes.
