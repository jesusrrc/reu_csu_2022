privacy policy.
this notice is made available to data subjects if switch automation is collecting or processing their personal information.
all employees/contractors of the switch automation group must bring this to the data subject’s attention and ensure their consent to the processing of their data is secured.
we at switch automation are committed to protecting your privacy.
this privacy notice applies to our website, platform and our subscription service and governs our data collection, processing and usage practices.
it also describes your choices regarding use, access and correction of your personal information.
if you do not agree with the data practices described in this privacy notice, you should not use our website, platform or the subscription service.
about switch automation.
switch automation was founded with an ambitious goal to improve the way we operate buildings in order to make a lasting positive impact on the environment.
we are headquartered in denver, colorado and provide a global iot platform for smart buildings.
our vision is to enable all buildings to be smarter and more sustainable, by creating technology that brings people and planet to the center of building operations.

information we collect.
on our website.
when you visit our website, you can browse the site without providing any information to us.
if you request a demonstration or register for our subscription service, we collect navigational information and request that you provide us personal information about yourself.
personal information.
when we say, “personal information”, we mean information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person (‘data subject‘).
if you can’t be identified (for example, when personal information has been aggregated and anonymized) then this notice doesn’t apply.
the personal information we collect is any information that you provide to us that identifies you personally including contact information of your name, e-mail address, company name and phone number.
we also collect your title, job function and level and the industry your company is in.
personal information also includes “navigational information” where such information can directly or indirectly identify an individual.
navigational information means information about your computer and your visits to our website such as your ip address and geographical location.
for our customers and partners.
“customers” means companies and individuals who have a direct or an indirect relationship with us to use our subscription service.
our “partners” are companies that use our platform to deliver services to their customers.
we must collect and store personal information in order to provide you with our subscription service and access to our platform.
we are committed to ensuring that the information we collect and use is appropriate for this purpose, and does not constitute an invasion of your privacy.
we will never disclose any of your details without your consent.
we may from time to time contact you for consent on matters relating to marketing purposes.
log files in our platform.
when you use our subscription service or access our platform, we automatically collect some navigational information including your ip address and we keep records of the number of logins you make.
this information is used by switch automation for security purposes and for the operations of our platform.
information about children.
our website is not intended for or targeted at children under 16 years of age.
no one under age 16 may provide any information to the website.
we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.
if you are under 16, do not use or provide any information on our website.
if we learn we have collected or received personal information from a child under 16, we will delete that information.
if you believe that we have collected information about a child under 16, please contact us at so that we may delete the information.

your rights as a data subject.
at any point while we are in possession of, or processing your personal information, you, the data subject, have the following rights.
right of access – you have the right to request a copy of the information that we hold about you.
right of rectification – you have the right to correct data that we hold about you that is inaccurate or incomplete.
right to be forgotten – in certain circumstances you have the right to ask for the data we hold about you to be erased from our records.
right to restriction of processing – where certain conditions apply, you have the right to restrict the processing.
right of portability – you have the right to have the data we hold about you transferred to another organisation.
right to object – you have the right to object to certain types of processing such as direct marketing.
right to object to automated processing, including profiling – you have the right to be subject to the legal effects of automated processing or profiling.
right to judicial review – in the event that we refuse your request under rights of access, we will provide you with a reason as to why.
