privacy policy.
this privacy policy describes the privacy practices of project frog for using their website, projectfrog.
com, and other websites owned or controlled by project frog.
this privacy policy applies only to data collected by all the websites owned or controlled by project frog and will notify you of the following.
​.
changes to this privacy policy.
if we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will notify you by revising the "last updated" date at the top of this privacy policy and, in some cases, we may provide you with additional notice, such as sending you an email notification or posting a statement to our website.
if there are material changes to this privacy policy, we will notify you more directly by email or a post on the website prior to the change taking effect.
if you do not agree with any of the changes and do not want your information to be subject to the revised privacy policy, you will need to stop using our services and deactivate with us.
your use of frog services after the posting of such changes will constitute your consent to those changes.
information collection, use, and sharing.
we are the sole owners of all the information collected on this site.
we only have access to/collect information that you willingly give us via email or other ways.
we will use your information to communicate with you regarding the reason you contacted us.
we will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization, other than as necessary to fulfill your request, e.
g.
to ship an order.
unless you ask us not to, we may contact you via email in the future to inform you about new products or services, or any change to this privacy policy.

registration.
in order to use some of our services, a user completes the registration/contact form.
a user is required to give certain information and that information will be used in the future to contact the user about the products/services on our site in which the user has expressed interest.
membership/subscription orders.
we request information from you on our membership/subscription form.
to use our services, you must provide contact information and financial information.
this information is solely used for billing purposes.
if we have trouble processing a service request, we will use the provided information to contact you.
security.
we take precautions to protect your information.
your sensitive information submitted via the website is protected both online and offline.
we use encryption to protect your sensitive information transmitted online.
only employees who need sensitive information to perform a job are granted access to that personal information.
we keep the devices/servers in which personally identifiable information is stored, in a secure environment.
wherever we collect sensitive information (such as credit card data), that information is encrypted and securely transmitted to us.
you can verify this by looking for a lock icon in the address bar and looking for "https" at the beginning of the address of the web page.
cookies.
we use first-party and third-party cookies on this site.
a cookie is a small file stored on a site visitor's computer to improve your experience and identify repeat visitors to our site.
usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information on our site.
if you use and interact with our websites or emails, we automatically collect information about your device and your usage of our websites or emails through cookies or similar technologies.
​.
the cookies we use.
account-related cookies.
if you sign up for an account, we will use cookies for the management of the signing process and my account.
login-related cookies.
this prevents you from having to log in every single time you visit a new page.
these cookies are usually removed or cleared when you log out.
email-related cookies.
our website offers subscription services and these cookies will be used to remember if you are already registered.
they also show certain notifications to subscribed or unsubscribed users.
forms related cookies.
when you submit data through an online form cookies may be set to remember your user details for future communication.
analytics cookies.
our site uses certain third-party cookies, such as google analytics and they are trusted solutions for helping us to understand how we can improve your experience.
they collect data about how you interact with the website and our services over time.
