privacy policy.
ctrack anz is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful and safe online experience.
this statement of privacy applies to the ctrack anz web site and governs data collection and usage.
by using the ctrack anz website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.
collection of your personal information.
ctrack anz collects personally identifiable information, such as your e-mail address, name, home or work address or telephone number.
ctrack anz also collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you, such as your postcode, age, gender, preferences, interests and favourites.
there is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by ctrack anz.
this information can include.
your ip address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring web site addresses.
this information is used by ctrack anz for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the ctrack anz web site.
please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through ctrack anz public message boards, this information may be collected and used by others.
note.
ctrack anz does not read any of your private online communications.
ctrack anz encourages you to review the privacy statements of web sites you choose to link to from ctrack anz so that you can understand how those web sites collect, use and share your information.
ctrack anz is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on web sites outside of the ctrack anz and ctrack anz family of web sites.
use of your personal information.
ctrack anz collects and uses your personal information to operate the ctrack anz web site and deliver the services you have requested.
ctrack anz also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from ctrack anz and its affiliates.
ctrack anz may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.
ctrack anz does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties.
ctrack anz may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you.
in those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party.
in addition, ctrack anz may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries.
all such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to ctrack anz, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.
ctrack anz does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.
ctrack anz keeps track of the web sites and pages our customers visit within ctrack anz, in order to determine what ctrack anz services are the most popular.
this data is used to deliver customised content and advertising within ctrack anz to customers whose behaviour indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.
ctrack anz web sites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to.
(a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on ctrack anz or the site.
(b) protect and defend the rights or property of ctrack anz.
and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of ctrack anz, or the public.
use of cookies.
the ctrack anz web site use “cookies” to help you personalise your online experience.
a cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a web page server.
cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer.
cookies are uniquely assigned to you and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.
one of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time.
the purpose of a cookie is to tell the web server that you have returned to a specific page.
for example, if you personalise ctrack anz pages, or register with ctrack anz site or services, a cookie helps ctrack anz to recall your specific information on subsequent visits.
this simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on.
when you return to the same ctrack anz web site, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the ctrack anz features that you customised.
you have the ability to accept or decline cookies.
most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer.
if you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the ctrack sa services or web sites you visit.
security of your personal information.
ctrack anz secures your personal information from unauthorised access, use or disclosure.
ctrack anz secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorised access, use or disclosure.
when personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other web sites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the secure socket layer (ssl) protocol.
changes to this statement.
ctrack anz will occasionally update this statement of privacy to reflect company and customer feedback.
ctrack anz encourages you to periodically review this statement to be informed of how ctrack anz is protecting your information.
contact information.
ctrack anz welcomes your comments regarding this statement of privacy.
if you believe that ctrack anz has not adhered to this statement, please contact ctrack anz at [email protected] we will use commercially reasonable efforts to promptly determine and remedy the problem.
