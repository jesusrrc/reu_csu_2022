other information.

we may collect information that others provide about you when they use the sensee’s services, or obtain information from other sources and combine that with information we collect through sensee’s services.

for example, service providers may help us.

these providers have limited access to your information to perform these tasks on our behalf, and are contractually obligated to use it consistent with this privacy policy.
payments to sensee.
sensee may share your personal information to.
(i) take payment from you for your use of the services and(ii) facilitate your use of the sensee services.
safety and compliance with law.
sensee may disclose your information to courts, law enforcement or governmental authorities, or authorized third parties, if and to the extent we are required to do so by law or if such disclosure is reasonably necessary.
(i) to comply with legal process and to respond to claims asserted against sensee,(ii) to respond to verified requests relating to a criminal investigation or alleged or suspected illegal activity or any other activity that may expose us, you, or any other of our users to legal liability,(iii) to enforce and administer our terms of service or other agreements with our account holders,(iv) for fraud investigation and prevention, risk assessment, customer support, product development and debugging purposes, or(v) to protect the rights, property or personal safety of sensee, its employees, its other account holders, or members of the public.
we will attempt to notify you about these requests unless.
(i) providing notice is prohibited by the legal process itself, by court order we receive, or by applicable law, or (ii) we believe that providing notice would be futile, ineffective, create a risk of injury or bodily harm to an individual or group, or create or increase a risk of fraud upon sensee’s services, its other account holders and/or and the sensee technical platform (collectively, “risk scenarios”).
in instances where we comply with legal requests without notice for these reasons, we will attempt to notify you about the request after the fact if we determine in good faith that we are no longer legally prohibited from doing so and that no risk scenarios apply.
