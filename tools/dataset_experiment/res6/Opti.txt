opti digital offers technology solutions and consulting to publishers to help them monetize their digital media.
amongst the solutions offered by opti digital is an advertising marketplace that proposes smart advertising services to publishers and advertisers.
this service gathers advertising demand including demand from other advertising buyer partners (hereafter referred to as “opti digital ad marketplace” or “our services”  or “the services” in this document).
advertisers or their intermediaries (e.
g.
media agencies) display their advertising campaigns through opti digital advertising solutions on the websites of our publishers.
opti digital advertising technology collects and uses data to help make online advertising more relevant as well as for ad delivery and reporting purposes.
introduction.
opti digital sas (hereafter referred to as “opti digital” or “we”, “us”) recognizes the importance of privacy and is committed to providing transparency and control over the use of the data in online advertising.
opti digital has taken all necessary steps to comply with the provisions of the regulation (eu) 2016/679 of the european parliament and of the council dated april 27, 2016 and applicable as of may 25, 2018 (the “gdpr”).
opti digital, when applicable, also adheres to the fullest extent to the iab europe’s gdpr transparency & consent framework 2.
0.
opti digital is a french company and falls within the competence of the french privacy authority, the commission nationale informatique et libertés (cnil).
general scope of this privacy policy.
this privacy policy details opti digital’ data practices on how we collect, use, process and share the data we may collect through our advertising service “opti digital ad marketplace” as well as the ways in which individuals may exercise control over the use of information collected by us.
this privacy policy does not describe opti digital’ data practices relating to information opti digital collects through its website  and only applies to opti digital ad marketplace.
your use of the services is also governed by the collaboration agreement between opti digital and our publishers.
as used herein, “personal data” means any information that identifies, relates to, describes, is reasonably capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with a particular opti digital customer or consumer or their household.
this privacy policy may be updated.
if we make any material changes we will notify you by means of a notice on our website prior to the change becoming effective.

data use.
we use the information collected for several purposes, including those described below.
targeted advertising.
we use the data to filter ad delivery according to the user profile, such as user interests, location, device used or page content.
user frequency capping.
we use data to prevent displaying the same ad to the same user too many times.
traffic and user interactions analisis.
we use data to monitor and report the campaign delivery to advertisers and publishers and for internal business analysis.
ads delivery.
we may communicate part of the user profile to programmatic buyers so that they can deliver their advertising campaigns.
solutions maintenance and improvement.
we use data to improve our service.
customer service.
we may use data to resolve disputes or other issues related to opti digital ad marketplace to publishers and advertisers.
troubleshooting, debugging, error identification.
we may use data to fix issues that affect the functionality and performance of our service.
opti digital rights and property protection and complying with law.
we may use data to protect our rights and property and comply with our legal obligations, including to detect, investigate, and prevent fraud and other illegal activities and to enforce our agreements.
insights analysis.

data retention and security.
we retain the data linked to a user up to 12 months from the date of collection.
we may also store aggregated and non-personal data for a longer period than 12 months for financial and statistical reasons.
opti digital cookie is kept up to 365 days after the last user interaction with our services.
opti digital uses generally accepted industry security standards to protect data.
your personal information will be retained in accordance with our data retention policy which is designed to retain data for as long as needed for us to comply with our contractual obligations, employment obligations, and other business purposes, including global legal obligations to retain data.
we may retain some information from closed accounts so that we can comply with law, prevent fraud, assist with investigations, resolve disputes, analyse or troubleshoot programs, enforce our collaboration agreement with our publishers, or take other actions permitted by law.
we have implemented reasonable security measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, exfiltration, theft, loss, misuse, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
please be aware that despite our best efforts, no data security measures can guarantee security.
you should take steps to protect against unauthorized access to your password, phone, and computer by, among other things, signing off after using a shared computer, choosing a robust password that nobody else knows or can easily guess, and keeping your log-in and password private.
we are not responsible for any lost, stolen, or compromised passwords or for any activity on your account via unauthorized password activity.
