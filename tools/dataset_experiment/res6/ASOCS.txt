right to restrict processing – you have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

what are cookies.
cookies are text files that a web server places on your computer when you visit a web page.
the cookies are used by the website to enable certain functionalities, like keeping you logged in, or remembering your settings, and displaying information relevant to you.
what types of cookies do we use.
we use several types of cookies, each containing different information.
anonymous analytics cookies, containing anonymous aggregate statistics and analysis.
registration cookies, which let us know when you are logged-in, so that features requiring log-in are available to you.
third party non advertising-related cookies, that allow us to integrate the website with other services, like linkedin or facebook.
each cookie has a time of expiry set, which can be up to 365 days.
how do we use cookies.
we use cookies mainly to influence the content that is presented to visitors on our websites and for analytics.
how to manage your cookies.
you can set your browser not to accept cookies, and our websites may instruct you how to remove cookies from your browser.
however, some of our websites’ features may not function as a result.
