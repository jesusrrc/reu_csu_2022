About Apostera.
Apostera is an automotive firm that specializes in a wide range of autonomous driving technologies such as ADAS, Lidar and automotive augmented reality.
On February 10th, 2022, Apostera was acquired by Harman International Industries.
The terms of the transaction were not disclosed.
