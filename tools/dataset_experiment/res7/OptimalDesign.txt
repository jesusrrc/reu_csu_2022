Cookies.
Cookies (or browser cookies).
A cookie is a small file placed on a visitor’s computer hard drive.
Cookies help us understand how the website is used.
This is purely for statistical information that helps us provide a better experience for all users of our website.
It also helps determine usage patterns and estimate our audience size.
We do not collect personal information automatically, but we may tie this information to personal information about you that you provide to us.
