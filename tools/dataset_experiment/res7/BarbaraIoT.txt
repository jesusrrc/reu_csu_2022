How do we collect your information.
As you browse this website you may encounter web forms for you to provide us with certain information.
For example, if you would like to receive information about our partner program, we ask for your name, email address and the company you work for.
Who has access to this information.
Only us.
We will not sell or rent your information to third parties and we will not share your information with third parties for commercial or marketing purposes.
Please note, however, that Barbara IoT may disclose your personal data in good faith and in the belief that such action is necessary with respect to the following.
Fulfilling a legal obligation.
To prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the service.
To protect the personal safety of service users or members of the public.
To protect against legal liability.
Data security.
The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
If we do suffer a data breach and discover it, we will always disclose it in accordance with established procedures.
Cookies.
Like many other websites, Barbara loT uses cookies.
Cookies' are small pieces of information sent by an organisation to your computer and stored on your hard drive to enable websites to recognise you when you visit them.
They collect statistical data about your browsing actions and patterns and do not identify you as an individual.
For example, we use cookies to store your language preference.
This helps us to improve our website and provide a better and more personalised service.
You can always configure your browser settings to stop receiving cookies if you wish.
