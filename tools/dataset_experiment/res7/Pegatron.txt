USE OF COOKIES.
In order to improve our Website, we use "cookies" to track your visit.
A cookie is small amount of data that is transferred to your browser by a web server and can only be read by the server that gave it to you.
It functions as your identification card, recording your passwords, purchases, and preferences.
It cannot be executed as code or deliver viruses.
Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies.
You can set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it.
Since our web pages require user authorization, cookies are not optional.
Users choosing not to accept cookies will probably not be able to access those pages.
We use cookies to track your visit to our Website and log the IP/Internet address of your computer.
