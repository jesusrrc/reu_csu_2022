Thank you for choosing to use Wingtra products.
We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.
If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at .
The primary purpose of collecting your data is to improve your experience with the WingtraOne drone, WingtraPilot and WingtraHub and to tailor our marketing offering according to your needs.
To that end, we only collect your data for clearly defined purposes.
We do not and will not sell your personal data to third parties.
This Privacy Statement (the ‘Statement’) explains the personal information we collect from you, either directly or indirectly, and how we will use it.
Personal information is any information that can be used to identify you or that we can link to you.
It applies to all products offered by Wingtra (‘Wingtra Product(s)’), including the WingtraOne drone and our software WingtraPilot and WingtraHub (‘Wingtra app’ or ‘app’) or services, for example the Wingtra website, as well as other offline interactions, such as visiting a Wingtra office or attending a Wingtra event (collectively referred to as ‘Services’).
Please read this Statement carefully to understand how Wingtra may collect, use, and share your personal information.
The terms ‘we’, ‘us’, or ‘Wingtra’ are each intended as reference to Wingtra AG.
The types of personal information we collect.
Information you provide to us.
We collect information you voluntarily provide to us, such as your name, phone number and email address.
for instance, when you want to learn more about Wingtra Products and Services or when you contact our support or sales team for assistance with your Wingtra Products.
Information we collect automatically.
We automatically collect information about you, about your use of Wingtra Products and Services, such as drone meta-data or information collected by using cookies and similar technologies when you use, access, or interact with us via our websites or app.
We may, for example, collect information about the type of device you use to access our websites, the operating system and version, your IP address, your general geographic location as indicated by your IP address, your browser type, the webpages you view on our websites, and whether and how you interact with content available on our websites.
Information obtained from other sources.
We may receive information about you from other sources, including third parties, such as partners with whom we offer co-branded services or engage in joint-marketing activities.
We may also receive information about you from social media platforms, for instance, when you interact with us on social media.
We protect data obtained from third parties according to the practices described in this Statement and we also apply any additional restrictions imposed by the source of data.
Information of children.
Wingtra does not target and is not intended to attract children under the age of eighteen.
Although visitors of all ages may navigate through our website or use our app, we do not knowingly collect or request personal information from those under the age of eighteen without parental consent.
If, following a notification from a parent or guardian or discovery by other means, a child under eighteen has been improperly registered on our site by using false information, we will cancel the child’s account and delete the child’s personal information from our records.

Customer interactions with Wingtra.
To make the information in this Statement easy to understand, we have highlighted different occasions when Wingtra could potentially collect personal information from customers.
The customer interactions with Wingtra are broken down into the following segments.
Learning about Wingtra.
As you gather more information about Wingtra, you may virtually or physically visit a site that we own.
Examples include browsing our website, calling our in-house sales representatives, physically visiting a Wingtra office or attending Wingtra-sponsored events.
To learn more about Wingtra Products, you may voluntarily provide personal information, such as your name and email address during these interactions.
We may also collect information about you online via cookies or similar technologies when you visit our websites.
We collect.
name, email address, phone number, company name, country, IP address, industry, other information you provide to us, and information provided by cookies or similar technology.
Why.
We use this information to share news about events and products offered by Wingtra.
Additionally, the IP address helps us to understand geographic information about our website’s visitors better so that we can improve our website for everyone.
It is our legitimate interest to process your personal data for these purposes.
Buying Wingtra products.
Once you have decided to buy a Wingtra Product, you may be asked to provide information so that we can complete your purchase.
We collect.
your name, company name, phone number, email address, delivery/shipping address, and other information you enter on our website.
Why.
We use this information to complete your transaction, carry out any follow up with you about your purchase, help with any delivery issues, handle returns, and other issues related to the purchase of Wingtra Products.
Processing your personal data for this purpose is necessary for the performance of the agreement we have with you.
Registering your Wingtra system.
When registering your Wingtra product, you will be asked to provide certain personal information, such as your e-mail address and product serial number, which will be associated with each other.
You will also be required to create one or more accounts with Wingtra itself and third party partners in order to access and use certain services such as Wingtra software, online support (Wingtra knowledge base) or to view the changelog and send product development suggestions (Canny).
We collect.
your name, email address, company name and the reseller name at which you purchased the drone, and other information you enter in our software or on our website.
Why.
We collect this information to help get your Wingtra Product working securely and to provide you support.
You will not be able to opt out from this data collection, sharing, and/or processing if you want to continue to use your Wingtra Products.
It is our legitimate interest to process your personal data for these purposes.
Using Wingtra products (‘drone meta-data’).
We collect information from your Wingtra Products in order to (1) provide product functionality such as synchronising flight plans with devices and user accounts, (2) provide predictive maintenance by detecting problems of Wingtra drones before failure occurs, (3) provide better support and to diagnose product issues faster, (4) improve the general reliability and security of the Wingtra Products, (5) understand how users use Wingtra products to guide our decisions when enhancing existing products or developing new products.
The processing of this information is in our legitimate interest as further set out below (under Why).
We collect.
Drone and software configuration.
This includes but is not limited to.
Plane ID, camera and PPK module serial numbers, software version, payload type and PPK status (active, non-active, failed).
Performance Information.
This includes, but is not limited to.
drone tippings, preflight check failures, flight data status warnings and all sorts of hardware and software warnings and failures, battery capacity, telemetry performance, type of landing (assisted, RTH, Auto, etc), speed and acceleration during landing, transition altitude.
Flight information.
This includes but is not limited to.
Number of flights, flight duration, flight date, area size, number of images, GSD, side and front overlap, altitude and wind speed, region and city.
Wingtra does not collect GPS coordinates of drones.
However, we obtain region and city locations of a drone via Google Firebase Analytics.
We do not collect.
Survey results.
We never collect exact flight plans, drone GPS coordinates, images, whether at full or reduced resolution or map data such as geo-tags, unless you submit such data separately via our file submission form in order to get support or consulting on data processing from us.
Why.
This data is absolutely necessary for your Wingtra Product or Service (including replacement notifications, safety checks of components, and synchronisation of flight plans between devices) to perform its basic functions in a secure way and you will not be able to opt out from this data collection, sharing, and/or processing if you want to continue to use your Wingtra Products.
Also we collect this information so that we can help ensure Wingtra Products are functioning properly and in a safe way, help predict potential problems with Wingtra Products, provide you with customer support, improve Wingtra Product’s reliability and security and determine what types of Product or feature improvements would please our customers most, which is our legitimate interest.
Getting help from Wingtra.
From time to time, you may decide to contact our Support for assistance with your Wingtra Products.
To be able to offer you the best service possible, we may collect certain data from you or your Wingtra Products.
We collect.
This may include contact information, such as your name, email address, phone number, social media identifiers (if you contact us using a social media channel), and the content of your chats and other communications with the Wingtra Support, information about your drone such as Plane ID, Software version, Camera serial number, Camera model, Camera mount serial number, PPK serial number.
In certain situations, the Support team will need additional data in order to help you solve your issue, such as log files, flight images and pictures or videos of the drone.
We will obtain these data only from you when you upload them via the support form or send it directly to our Support team.
We don’t capture log files or images ourselves without your consent.
Log files.
Log files contain all system data, including but not limited to.
the drone configuration, the drone exact position and trajectory, records of the behaviour and performance of drone components, system warnings and failures, in total more than 600+ parameters.
Log files do not contain any flight images or map data.
Flight images and geotags.
In case you need support regarding the quality or the post-processing of the images, our Support Team might require you to send images and geo-tags of your flights.
Pictures or videos of the drone.
This is necessary for our Support team to assess the state of the drone hardware in case of physical damage and/or to assess if the warranty applies in case of repair or return.
Why.
We collect this information to give you the best and most efficient customer support possible.
For instance, for customer enquiries and technical questions, we use the contact information to respond to the customer in their preferred channel (phone, email, social).
For technical problems, we use data in the log files and/or the flight images to effectively troubleshoot the issue.
For product returns and/or repairs, we use the contact information to ensure successful product receipt and/or delivery.
Using these types of data is necessary to fulfil the agreement we have with you.
Additionally we will use aggregated flight logs to improve Wingtra Product’s reliability and security and determine what types of Product or feature improvements would please our customers most, which is our legitimate interest.

Our use of cookies and other similar technologies.
We use cookies and similar technologies like pixels, tags, web beacons, and other identifiers to help us personalise our website for you, remember your preferences, understand how users are using our website or app, and help customise our marketing offerings.
Cookies.
A cookie is a small data file containing a string of characters that is sent to your computer when you visit a website.
When you visit the website again, the cookie allows that site to recognise your browser.
The length of time a cookie will stay on your computer or mobile device depends on whether it is a “persistent” or “session” cookie.
Wingtra uses both types of cookies.
Session cookies will only stay on your device until you stop browsing.
Persistent cookies stay on your computer or mobile device until they expire or are deleted.
We use the following types of cookies on our website.
Strictly necessary cookies.
These cookies are essential for you to browse our website and use its features.
Without these cookies, the layout of the website might not look as intended.
Performance cookies.
These cookies collect information about how you use our websites.
This data is used to help optimise our website and make it easier for you to navigate.
Functional cookies.
These cookies allow our websites to remember choices you make whilst browsing and personalise your experience.
We may store your geographic location in a cookie for instance, to ensure that we show you the website relevant to your area.
Third Party cookies.
Third party cookies are those placed by websites and/or parties other than Wingtra.
These cookies may be used on our website to improve our products or services or to help us provide more relevant advertising.
These cookies are subject to their respective privacy policies for these external services, for example, Facebook Data Use Policy.
Analytics cookies.
We use analytics cookies, like those offered by Google Analytics or Hubspot, to help us understand things like how long a visitor stays on our website, what pages they find most useful, and how they arrived at Wingtra.

To learn more about Google Analytics and your data, visit this Google webpage.

We use cookies, such as the one offered by Hubspot to tailor our marketing offering to our visitors and customers.
These cookies record the behaviour of users on the website, such as which page has been visited and when.
This allows us to send you more relevant offerings on our Products and Services whenever we think you may need it.

Although we do not currently employ technology that recognises DNT signals, we will only process your personal data in accordance with this Statement.
