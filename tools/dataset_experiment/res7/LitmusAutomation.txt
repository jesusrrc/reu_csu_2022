Litmus Web & Software Privacy Policy.
This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices for  email newsletter subscriptions, as well as registration for Litmus Automation’s Litmus Edge and Litmus Edge Manager software and resources.
This privacy policy applies solely to information collected by this web site, newsletter subscriptions, softwares, support documentation and API access.
It will notify you of the following.
1.
What personally identifiable information is collected from you through the web site, how it is used and with whom it may be shared.
2.
What choices are available to you regarding the use of your data.
3.
The update procedures in place to protect the use of your information.
Information Collection, Use, and Sharing.
We are the sole owners of the information collected on this site.
We only have access to/collect information that you voluntarily give us via email or other direct contact from you.
We will not sell or rent this information to anyone or an entity.
We will use your information to respond to you, regarding the reason you contacted us.
We will not share your information with any third party outside of our organization, other than as necessary to fulfill your request, e.
g.
to ship an order, subscribe to our newsletter, receive product information in digital format.
At times, we may analyze activity, such as email opens and product information downloads that you have accessed via email from our website.
The data may be cross referenced or compared to website traffic we gather on litmusautomation.
com by using Autopilot automation software.
We may also gather information on your customer journey through our website, demonstrating when, where and how you had success in interacting with our marketing collateral and access to core products.
Based on your consent, we may contact you via email in the future to tell you about our upcoming events, new products or services, or changes to this privacy policy.
The consented collection of your data is kept indefinitely, unless requested to no longer be kept through contacting us (review contact methods below).
All data collected and accessed through our website or newsletter subscriptions service (Mailchimp) is protected through two-factor authentication methods.
The Litmus Automation website may use cookies from time to time.
This information is only used for analytics purposes (Google Analytics).
Compliance to cookies is available at the bottom of your web browser when visiting litmusautomation.
com.
In the event of a data breach, Litmus will provide notice to all subscribers within 72 hours of data breach discovery.
We do not collect or process information to determine race or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious or philosophical beliefs, trade union memberships, genetic or biometric data, Health or mortality, sex life or sexual orientation.
Your Access to and Control Over Information.
You may opt out of any future contacts from us at any time.
You can do the following at any time by contacting us via the contact prompt on our website at .
– See what data we have about you, if any.
– Change/correct any data we have about you.
– Have us delete any data we have about you.
– Express any concern you have about our use of your data.
Updates.
Our Privacy Policy may change from and all updates will be posted on this page.
