Do-Not-Track.
If you wish to further prevent tracking of your online activity, you can set a preference in your browser that alerts websites you visit that you do not want them to collect certain information about you.
This is referred to as a Do-Not-Track (DNT) signal.
Please be aware that our websites may not recognize or take action in response to DNT signals from your web browser.
At present time, there is no universally accepted standard for what a company should do when a DNT signal is detected.
However, should a final standard be established, we will assess how to appropriately respond to these signals.

How We Protect Your Data.
We have taken all appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect Your Data from misuse, unauthorized or unlawful access or disclosure, loss, alteration, damage or destruction.

Personally identifiable information is stored behind a firewall and is not publicly accessible.
