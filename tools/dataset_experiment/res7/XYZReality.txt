COOKIES.
Our website uses cookies to distinguish you from other users of our website.
This helps us to provide you with a good experience when you browse our website and allows us to improve our site.
Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to your hard drive to store and sometimes track information about you.
Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but if you prefer, you can change your browser to prevent that and your help screen or manual will tell you how to do this.
However, you will not be able to take full advantage of our website if you do so.
Cookies are specific to the server that created them and cannot be accessed by other servers, which means they cannot be used to track your movements around the web.
Although they do identify a user’s computer, cookies do not personally identify users and passwords and credit card information are not stored in cookies.
