Privacy and Data Confidentiality.
Your privacy is important to us.
We will use strict information technology procedures to safeguard your data and prevent improper access.
Data is stored in our secure databases with access to only authorized individuals.
This includes all images too.
No data such as survey responses is stored on users phone.
Only data that is already being tracked on your  phone using phone sensors like GPS, barometer, step tracking (shared by user for the purpose of tracking) remains on the phone as this data is outside the tracker app itself and the tracker app does not have permission to delete this data from the user’s phone.
We follow all industry best practices for HIPAA compliance.
Data is sent from the devices to our servers in encrypted form using SSL encryption.
All images are encoded.
All database access is restricted to authorized individuals only.
We will not access your personal phone contacts, texts, emails, personal photos, or websites visited.
Your authorization to use and share your tracked data will expire when you opt out of the Application or uninstall the Application.
Security.
We are concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information.
We provide physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect information we process and maintain.

Please be aware that, although we endeavor provide reasonable security for information we process and maintain, no security system can prevent all potential security breaches.

We will retain User Provided data for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter.
