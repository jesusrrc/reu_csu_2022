What Tracking Information Does Tiertime Collect About Me Or My Devices.
Tiertime uses cookies, tracking pixels and similar technologies on our Websites to collect information that helps us provide our Products to you and to learn how our Products is used.
We also use these technologies to help deliver promotional messages and ads for relevant Tiertime products and services.
Cookies are small data files that a web site you visit may save on your hard drive.
They usually include an anonymous unique identifier.
A cookie cannot retrieve any other data from your hard drive or pass on computer viruses.
These cookies are necessary to enable you to move around our web site and use its features.
Without these cookies, we can’t provide services you have asked for, like processing an order in the web store.
We use other cookies to help recognize and remember you when you are logged into the Service so we can remember your settings and preferences, such as your language and region or your logged-in state.
These cookies also may help us provide services you have asked for, such as watching a video or commenting on our user forum.
These cookies do not track your browsing activity on non-Tiertime websites.
You may disable any of these functional cookies.
but if you do so, then various functions of the web site may be unavailable to you or may not work as intended.

Data Storage and Transfer.
Where Is My Data Stored.
When you use Tiertime Software on your computing device, such as by using one of our downloadable applications, Content you save will be stored locally on that device.
When you upload your data or information to Tiertime’s website, the information will be replicated on our servers, which are located in the cloud service that Tiertime chooses.

If you store information in or submit data to the Tiertime website or Tiertime Software and sync such Tiertime Software with the Tiertime service, you are confirming your consent to such information, including Personal Information and Content, being transmitted to, hosted and accessed in the cloud.
How Can I Access or Correct Information Tiertime Holds About Me.
If you wish to (i) access any Personal Information that we hold about you.
