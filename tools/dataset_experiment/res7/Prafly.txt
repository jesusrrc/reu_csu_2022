IF YOU ARE BELOW THE AGE OF 18.
If you are below the age of 18 years, you must always get permission from your parent or guardian before you use our Platforms or give us your Personal Information.
We may check that your parent or guardian has given you such permission, for example by asking you to provide us with your parent’s or guardian’s contact details so we can contact them.
We may also check that the contact details you have given us are correct and are the details of a person who is actually your parent or guardian.
If we are not able to verify such permission or if a parent or guardian confirms that you do not have their permission, you may not use our Platforms.
Further, Perifly may not sell liquor products to you if you are under the age of 18 regardless of any supervision by a parent or guardian.
You may be requested to provide proof of your age before receiving any liquor products ordered via our Platforms.
