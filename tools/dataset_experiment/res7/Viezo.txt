Data Privacy Policy of UAB “Nanoenergija”This Privacy Policy applies to all personal data that you provide us with or personal data that is collected and processed by us when you are using UAB “Nanoenergija” (Viezo) website .
Viezo respects your privacy and protects your personal data that is provided by you or collected by us as stated in this Privacy Policy.
This Privacy Policy explains why we collect your personal data and how we use it.
Contact UsYour personal data (name, surname, e-mail address, phone number, company name, country and question) is used and processed for the purpose of providing an offer to you.
Your personal data is used and processed on the basis of your consent which is expressed by filling in the form available at  (“Request Demo” section).
Data Storage PeriodWe process and store your personal data no longer than necessary for the purposes stated in this Privacy Policy.

Data TransferYour personal data may be provided to the third parties who process your personal data in order to help us administrate our website and protect your personal data.

Your personal data may also be provided to the authorities of law enforcement agencies, such as police or supervisory authorities, but only upon request and only when required by applicable law or in order to protect our rights or to ensure the security of our customers, employees and resources.
The Consequences of Failure to Provide Personal DataYou have the right to refuse to provide us with your personal data.

Personal Data ProtectionWe apply security and processing requirements set out in the Law on Legal Protection of Personal Data of the Republic of Lithuania and General Data Protection Regulation of the European Union.
Viezo takes appropriate technical and organisational measures in order to ensure personal data protection against unauthorised or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction or damage and against all other forms of unlawful processing.
