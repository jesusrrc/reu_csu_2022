About Sucheon.
Sucheon is a developer of noise recognition solutions to manufacture diagnostic devices and operation monitoring systems for machinery and equipment.
The product collects acoustic signals in the laboratory, workshop, plant, road, or other environments through sensor arrays, and uses Fourier transform, time-shift correlation analysis, spatial pattern analysis, cross-correlation power spectrum and neural network to analyze and process the noise.
