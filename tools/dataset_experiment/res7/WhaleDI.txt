Our website provides information about Blue Whale’s general business, work profile, service insights, or products etc.
We have created this privacy policy based on our dedication and commitment to protecting our client’s privacy.
The following statements will disclose our information gathering and dissemination practices for the Blue Whale Apps Website.
Collection of personal information.
At Blue Whales, we take the data protection very seriously and strictly adhere to the confidentiality of the information shared by every individual or client who is interested to do business with us.
By accessing our website you agree with our collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this privacy policy.
We may require information such as name, contact number, and email address, in order to transact business with you.
We will use this data to provide information requested by you for fulfilling business transactions or for other purposes set out in this notice.
We would like to give an assurance that we do not collect any sensitive information like political or religious or philosophical opinions, racial or ethnic origin or trade union membership, genetic or biometric data or data concerning health or sexual orientation.
GDPR Data Privacy Rules.
As per the GDPR, aka General Data Protection Regulation Act rolled out on May 25th, 2018, we have updated our privacy policy.
We would like to inform that all our survey tools are fully GDPR compliant.
At Blue Whales, we use all the latest techniques to collect personal data as well as confidential data and these insights are in compliance with the GDPR act and cyber-security.
The content which we will collect through this website, we will obtain through your explicit consent.
We will use the data collected to enhance our services, and better understand our customer’s needs and expectations.
This is also to ensure the security of our services and that they are functioning properly.
Purpose of Data Collection and Legal Requirements.
The sole purpose of data collection is to use it for marketing and sales reasons.
In case of any legal requirements, we may disclose any personally identifiable or non-personally identifiable information in accordance with the law or if we reasonably believe that such action is necessary in order to.
Fulfill a government request.

To defend or protect the legal rights or property of Blue Whale Web Solutions, Inc.
the Site or its users.
In an emergency, to protect the health and safety of the users of the Site or the general public.
Web Browser Cookies When you visit the Blue Whale Apps website, our site may use “cookies” to enhance user experience.
Cookies are basically small chunks of information stored on your hard-drive in order to track information about our users and for record-keeping purposes.
Be aware that cookies are not used to spy on you or invade your privacy or steal information.
The sole purpose of cookies is for easy navigation through the website and deliver specific content as per your interest and to prevent data reentering while you register yourself at each connection.
