Privacy at the HoloBuilder Help Center.
At HoloBuilder we aim to provide our customers a seamless support experience.
To achieve this, we use a tightly coupled combination of live chat and knowledge base by Intercom.
Intercom places a cookie on your computer.
The chat needs to be able to associate the message with you.
Thus, Intercom places a cookie stating with 'intercom-id' on your computer.
This is needed to provide proper support to you.
You can disable this cookie by disabling cookies in your browser or clear it.
However, this will inhibit the chat functionality.
For instruction on how to do that please refer to the documentation of your browser.
We do not track your behavior in the HoloBuilder Help Center.
