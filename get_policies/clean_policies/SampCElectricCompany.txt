last updated: june 30, 2020this privacy notice (“notice”) describes how the s&c electric company (“us,” “we,” “s&c”) collects and otherwise processes your personal data in connection with:
your use of our website (www.sandc.com) and customer portals;
our recruitment processes;
your communications with us through other channels, such as if you call our customer service center, interact with us at an event, or otherwise contact us; and
any other circumstances in which we refer you to this notice.
this notice does not apply to personal data that we collect in the context of your employment or other working relationship with s&c. if you are an employee or other worker of s&c, please refer to your human resources department. this notice does, however, contain disclosures related to our use of personal data pertaining to the family members, next of kin, beneficiaries and/or dependents of our current and former personnel in connection with the provision of benefits and similar services.if you have any questions or complaints about this notice or our information practices, please contact us using the information below.personal data we processdepending on how you interact with us, we may process the following categories of personal data about you:

direct identifiers, such as your first and last name, mailing address (including zip code), e-mail address, telephone and facsimile numbers, and date of birth.
commercial information, such as credit card number and related information.
communication information, such as the content of communications when you contact us via our hotline, an online form or enter a contest.
professional or employment-related information, such as employer, job title and department, resume/curriculum vitae, cover letter, background information, and work history and qualifications; we may also process the personal data of references.
internet activity information, such as ip address and other device identifiers.
other information you may provide us through your use of our website or services, or by otherwise interacting with us.

we collect personal data from:
direct interactions, such as when you enquire about our services, register for an event with us, provide us with information in connection with our services, participate in surveys or questionnaires, subscribe to a newsletter or in any way engage with us or our workers;
cookies and other data collection technologies, such as when you interact with our website or click on links on our emails;
through social media, such as if you link to social media platforms or use social media plug-ins, in which case we may (depending on your user privacy settings on that social media platform) automatically receive information about you from that social media platform;
third parties, such as customers, contractors, consultants and suppliers; and
security systems, such as when you visit our offices and we obtain closed circuit television (“cctv”) footage and other information through electronic means such as security/swipe card records.
s&c also automatically collects certain other data when you visit our website. this data is collected in aggregated form and includes the type of web browser and operating systems used.  this information cannot be used to identify you, but is useful for improving the user experience on our website.how we use your personal datas&c collects your personal data in order to operate our business, improve our services, and support your participation in the activities you select.  the personal data we collect may be used in the following ways:
