privacy policy
mercury systems, inc., 50 minuteman road, andover, ma 01810, respects your privacy and recognizes your need for appropriate protection and management of any personally identifiable information you share with us. this includes any information by which you can be personally identified, such as name, address, telephone number, email address, and ip address.  mercury has established this privacy policy so that you can understand how we intend to treat your information. under european/uk data privacy laws, mercury systems, inc. is a data controller.
to protect your privacy, we have adopted the following principles:
information we collect
in general, you can visit mercury's corporate website, www.mrcy.com, without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. we do track the internet address of the domains from which people visit us and analyze this data for trends and statistics, but the individual user remains anonymous.
some of our web pages utilize "cookies" so that we can better serve you with more tailored information when you return to our site. cookies are identifiers which a website can send to your browser to keep on your computer to facilitate your next visit to our site. you can set your browser to notify you when you are sent a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether or not to accept it. we honor “do not track” scripts sent to our website by the internet browser you use.  if you do not wish for us to track your web visits, you can turn that setting on in your web browser.
for more information see www.allaboutcookies.org. we do not share information obtained from cookies with third parties. we collect and analyze it for trends and statistics and also use it to improve our service to you. we currently use google's demographics and interests reporting features strictly for analytics purposes. you are able to opt out of this functionality by modifying your google ads settings.
your personal information
there are times when we may ask you to provide information, including some identifying you personally. it is our intent to inform you before we do so and to tell you what we intend to do with the information. you may agree to provide us with this information if you choose and you will be able to "opt out" of certain uses of the information where you have initially provided your consent. if you choose not to provide the information we request, you can still visit most of the mrcy.com web site, but you may be unable to access certain options, offers, and services. in most instances, we process your personal information based on your consent and we may also rely on our legitimate interests to process your personal information.  we take appropriate technical and organization security measures to protect your personal data. this website is not intended to be used by children (under the age of 16 or such lower age as determined by applicable law) and we do not intend to collect any personal information relating to children. please contact us if you become aware that any personal information relating to children has been provided to us.
sharing your personal information
we take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us from unauthorized access or disclosure. we share your personal information with our affiliates, advisors and other third parties for the purposes of our legitimate business interests and in accordance with our obligations to keep your personal information secure. we process your information in the u.s., where we are based, and you should be aware that the u.s. may have a lower standard of data privacy laws than in your country of residence. we do not intend to share the personal information you provide us with other third parties without your consent (except, of course, if a court orders us to do so or if we are requested to share your personal information with a governmental authority, a regulator or pursuant to litigation or an investigation).
rights
you may, under applicable laws including european and uk data privacy laws, have the right to access your personal information that we hold, to ensure it is accurate, to request that we delete and/or restrict the processing of your personal information in accordance with, and subject to, such data privacy laws. to enforce any of these rights, you can email us at marketing@mrcy.com.
commitment
if you have any comments or questions regarding our privacy policy, please contact us. we will address any issue to the best of our abilities. you can, under applicable european/uk data privacy laws, also write to the data protection authority in your country of residence.
thank you for your support of these policies and of mercury systems, inc.
