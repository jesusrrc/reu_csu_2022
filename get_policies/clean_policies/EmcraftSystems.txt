about us
we design, manufacture and sell arm cortex-a and cortex-m system-on-modules. on the software side, we provide production-ready, self-maintained linux board support packages (bsps) and software distributions for all of our system-on-modules.
our soms have been deployed in thousands of systems across a wide range of customer applications.
locations
emcraft is headquartered in beautiful carlsbad, california, usa. all our system-on-modules and kits ship from our us office.
