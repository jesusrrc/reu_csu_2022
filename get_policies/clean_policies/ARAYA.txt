personal information protection policy
purpose of use of personal information
(a) various types of communication to provide services tailored to the customer's needs. (b) to contact you in response to your inquiry. we will collect personal information necessary for the above purposes by fair and appropriate means. when we collect personal information that is necessary for the above purposes, we shall obtain the consent of the person concerned. acquired personal information and personal information requiring consideration shall not be used for purposes other than those stated without the consent of the individual. we will take measures to prevent the leakage of information and supervise not only our employees but also contractors. we will not provide information to third parties, whether in japan or overseas, without the consent of the person concerned. we will disclose information in response to a request from the person in question. if the disclosed personal information is not true, we will correct or delete it. we will deal with complaints about the handling of personal information in an appropriate and prompt manner. this privacy policy applies only within this website.
using google analytics
in order to provide better services and improve usability, this website uses google analytics to collect and analyze data on the use of this website. in doing so, google may collect information such as your ip address through "cookies", but the information collected by the "cookies" will not be able to identify you personally. the data collected will be managed in accordance with google's privacy policy. by using this website, you consent to the processing of data by google and this website in the manner and for the purposes set out above.
