cookies
cookies are small text files that sites send to the user’s computer. they are memorized and retransmitted to those same sites when the users log on again.
the site uses:
cookies that do not serve to identify the user but that are rather limited to transmitting data relative to the session and in the form of numbers automatically generated by the server. the cookies are not memorized permanently but are automatically cancelled once the browser is closed.
