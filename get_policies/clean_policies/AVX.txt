privacy plicy
the academic venture exchange (avx) will handle information you provide as confidential and/or proprietary. if you provide information to avx such as name and email address, we may store this information and use it to contact you. your provision of such information is entirely voluntary and we do not share such information with third parties, except in the specific case of your request to engage with a potential venture. in that case, an avx employee will review the application data you submit, along with other proprietary aggregated data, and will share it specifically with the point of contact for the venture you are contacting.there is no guarantee that any transmission of data over the internet is completely secure. we take all precautions and use currently accepted practices and protocols, but avx cannot warrant the security of any information you transmit to us.all emails and newsletters from this site allow you to opt out of further mailings.
