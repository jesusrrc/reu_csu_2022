chengdu ebyte electronic technology co.,ltd
chengdu ebyte electronic technology co.,ltd.is a high-tech enterprise which specializes in internet of things communications, has hundreds of self-developed products and unanimously recognized by customers. our company has a strong r&d capabilities, with a perfect after-sale system, we provide customers with complete solutions and technical support,shorten the r&d cycle, reduce r&d costs, and provide a powerful platform for new products development.
our company is an enterprise with large scale in wireless data transmission, high r&d capability and strong  core competence, comprehensive strength ranks in the top ten of the country.the products have obtained a number of patents and fcc/ce/rohs certification,and widely used in internet of things, consumer electronics, industrial control, medical, security alarm, field collection, smart home, highway, property management, water and electricity meter reading, power monitoring, environmental monitoring and other application scenarios.
chengdu ebyte electronic technology co., ltd. was formally set up in may 2012, with rapid development in the past 6 years; the r&d and production capabilities have been continuously improved and the products have obtained many international certifications such as fcc/ce/rohs. we have been built deep cooperation with many well-known domestic enterprises and actively carried out export work, also has established taobao, alibaba, aliexpress, ebay, wish and other e-commerce platforms, and through google global search, linkedin and other platforms for overseas synchronization promotion.the products are exported to over 30 countries around the world and are well admired by customers, expanding the international influence of the company. at the same time, our company actively participate in domestic and foreign electronic exhibitionssuch as 2018 united states ces exhibition, india convergence electronics show, russia sviaz exhibition and so on..
in the meantime, ebyte has never forgotten its mission, with promises to the employees: make employees settle down and start career and develop with the company; with promises to the customers: excellent quality, strive for better; with promises to the society: to be good citizens of china and do the best to take social responsibility. in the continuous improvement of employee benefits within the company, but also try the best to make social contributions.
