privacy policy
lg electronics u.s.a. inc. and its subsidiaries (collectively “we,” “us,” or "lgeus") care about your privacy. lgeus provides many products and services. this privacy policy applies specifically to (i) lgeus websites where it is posted or referenced (“lgeus websites”), (ii) the lgeus chatbot, (iii) lgeus customer service calls, (iv) lgeus emails, and (v) lgeus product registration (collectively, the "services"). this privacy policy does not apply to any other lgeus branded products or services. the purpose of this privacy policy is to explain how we collect information, what we collect, how we use it, how we share it, and what controls and rights you have. by using the services, you consent to the terms of this privacy policy.
a. how we collect information
when you use any of the services, information about you may be collected directly from you, automatically from your device, or from third parties.
direct collection. by using the services you may directly provide us with information. for example, you may click on buttons or enter text on an lgeus website, tell us information when you use the lgeus chatbot or call/email lgeus customer service, or fill out and send us a product registration card for your lgeus product.
automatic collection. by using the services you may automatically provide us with information. for example, when you visit one of our lgeus websites from your computer, browser or smartphone, your device may automatically send us information such as browser requests, your ip address, or cookie or pixel data. similarly, when you call lgeus customer service, your phone carrier may automatically send us information for the caller id function.
third parties. lgeus may also obtain information about you from third-party marketing, database, analytics partners or service providers.
b. the information we collect
as a general matter, lgeus collects information that may help us in providing you with the current services and provide you with future products and services.
basic identifiers. we may collect basic identifying information about who our customers or visitors are. for example, we may collect information about (a) the person (e.g., first name, last name, date of birth), (b) business (e.g., business name, address, phone number, web address), (c) contact details (e.g., e-mail address, phone number, postal address), (d) account details (e.g., username, password), and (e) device details (e.g., device model name, os version, uuid, imei, carrier, country, log files, internet protocol (ip) address). basic identifiers may be collected directly or automatically from you when you interact with the service (e.g., when you register a device, create an account, review a product, or contact us). note that your address may be also be considered your geolocation and your business name may also be considered as employment information.
commercial information. we may collect information about products you are interested in (e.g., wish list, compare), have ordered or received, purchases you have made, services you have ordered or received, payment information (e.g., credit card number, billing address) and your general purchase history. we may collect this information directly or automatically.
network activity. we may collect information about your internet and other network activity. such information may include the website that referred you to an lgeus website or how you interact with the service. we may collect this information directly or automatically.
personal characteristics. we may collect characteristics about you (e.g., age, gender, marital status) from third parties so we can better understand who you are as a customer.
audio information. we may record phone calls made to our customer service for quality assurance. we collect this information automatically.
inferences about trends and preferences.  through the course of your interactions with the service, we may create or collect inferences about you for purposes of creating a profile to understand you better, such as your basic preferences, predispositions, behavior, attitudes, aptitudes, or other characteristics. we may collect this information directly, automatically, or via third party marketing, database, or analytics partners.
c. how we use information
as a general matter, lgeus uses the information it collects to help us in providing you with the current services, to improve our products and services, and provide you with future products and services.
to provide you with functionality.  we may use your basic identifiers, network activity and commercial information to provide you with the services.
to create an lgeus account. we may use your basic identifiers to create an account with lgeus.
to serve you with relevant advertisements and marketing. we may use your basic identifiers, network activity, commercial information (e.g., transaction history), personal characteristics, and inferences to serve you with relevant advertisements, marketing and special offers.
to complete a transaction.  we may use your basic identifiers, commercial information and network activity in order to complete your requested purchase of goods or services, including monitoring the transaction for fraud.
to register purchase products. we may use your basic identifiers and commercial information to register your product with lgeus, verify ownership for your warranty, offer you extended service plans, provide you with tips on the use of your product and special offers, and to provide customer service.
to provide you with support.  we may use your basic identifiers, commercial information, and network activity in order to provide you with support for lgeus products and services via the lgeus chatbot, lgeus customer service calls and emails to lgeus.
to communicate with you.  we may use your basic identifiers to communicate with you about our products, services, and special promotions.
to compile statistics.  we may use basic identifiers, commercial information, and network activity to compile statistics and other analytics about users and/or the function and usage of the service.
to comply with our legal obligations.  we may use basic identifiers, commercial information and network activity to comply with any applicable legal obligations.
