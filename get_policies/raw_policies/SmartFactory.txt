After registering for the service, you will receive an e-mail from us, containing a link that needs to be confirmed by you. Until we receive your confirmation e-mail, your registration is not complete and you will not receive the newsletter. The provided information will solely be used for the mailing of the newsletter.<br/> To send out our newsletter we use rapidmail, which is a German certified newsletter software provider.
