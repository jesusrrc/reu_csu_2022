<h2>What we do at Fiix Inc.</h2>
<p>Fiix Inc. is in the business of supplying the industrial and corporate sectors with professional grade software and services for the maintenance management of assets and inventory. Fiix Inc CMMS runs as a software-as-a-service that securely protects your important information.</p>
<h2>The Fiix Inc. Privacy Policy</h2>
<p>Fiix Inc. has created this Privacy Policy in order to set out how we collect, use, and disclose personal information through our website and in the course of providing our CMMS software-as-a-service (SaaS) application and mobile app. This Privacy Policy covers the collection, use and disclosure of information about identifiable individuals and information which can be used to identify an individual. Personal Information may be collected about visitors to our Website, as well as our customers, their end users, and other users who use our mobile App, Platform or services.</p>
<p>This Privacy Policy covers the activities of Fiix Inc. only. This Privacy Policy does not apply to the practices of legal entities that we do not own or control, including our customers who may use the service to collect the Personal information of their employees. If you submit any Personal Information to Fiix Inc. or the CMMS Application Website, Mobile App or Services, you are responsible for ensuring that you have obtained the necessary authorizations and consents from the relevant individuals in order to make such Personal Information available to us for use in accordance with this Privacy Policy.</p>
<p>We take the privacy of our customers seriously. We provide transparency on what customer personal data we collect, for which purposes we use it, how we handle it, etc. This can relate to our marketing and lead generation activities, to webinars and trade shows we organize, to cookies on our website, etc. This Privacy Policy is a good resource for anyone that may have privacy related questions for Fiix. In addition, in certain circumstances, customers may require us to sign a contract covering data protection and privacy. In such cases, please reach out to the Fiix Privacy Officer for support at <a href="mailto:privacy@fiixsoftware.com" rel="noopener" target="_blank">privacy@fiixsoftware.com</a>.</p>
<h2>Data Processing: Definition</h2>
<p>The definition of data processing includes collection, use, protection, retention, disclosure and disposal of personal data. Our company has an obligation to ensure all internal and external data processing complies with privacy laws. Under privacy laws this data processing is interpreted broadly to mean anything and everything that can be done with data concerning individuals. As technology and business purposes for data processing continue to evolve, we will provide you updates on those activities.</p>
<h2>Privacy and Fiix Inc. Vendors</h2>
<p>When we engage with vendors who process the personal data of our employees, contractors or customers, we want to make sure they meet our information security, data protection and privacy expectations. The Fiix Information Security team takes the necessary steps in this regard, pursuant to Fiix Inc. and Rockwell Automation’s Information Security Policy.</p>
<h2>Payment Information</h2>
<p>When customers purchase our services, a valid credit card number, type, expiration date, name and billing address are collected, stored, used and processed by Stripe Inc., our third-party payment processing company, and not by Fiix.</p>
<p>Accordingly, the collection, storage, use and processing of your Payment Information is governed by Stripe's applicable terms of service available at <a href="https://stripe.com/us/terms" rel="noopener" target="_blank">https://stripe.com/us/terms</a> and privacy policy available at <a href="https://stripe.com/us/privacy" rel="noopener" target="_blank">https://stripe.com/us/privacy</a>.</p>
<h2>Information Security</h2>
<p>Fiix Inc. is committed to protecting the security of your information and we train our employees to keep clients' Personal Information private and confidential. Information is retained in controlled facilities and protected from unauthorized access. Personal information is kept only as long as is reasonably required to provide the services to you in accordance with applicable law. For more details on how we keep your information safe, please review our <a href="https://www.fiixsoftware.com/legal/security-policy/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Security Policy</a>.</p>
<h2>Confidentiality</h2>
<p>Your personal information will not be sold, leased or provided to third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent. When we hire other companies to provide services on our behalf, we only provide them with the personal information they need to provide the service (if any). They are required to keep the information confidential and may not use it for any other purpose.</p>
<h2>Fiix Inc. Data Gathering</h2>
<p>Like all web servers, the system that runs our website collects data about which IP Address accessed which pages. This information cannot be personally linked to you, and it is used only for product support and security logging. We use web analytics tracking on the site and this information is not linked to you. Web analytics data is collected to aggregate traffic patterns on the site and allows us to improve the design and the content selection on the site.</p>
<h2>Data Gathering for Marketing</h2>
<p>Our marketing team uses a system that helps us automate email messaging and marketing. When you submit an email address or other information into a form on the site, your browsing and actions on the site become linked to this information. This helps us send you more relevant information to match your interests.</p>
<p>The Fiix Inc. CMMS and Mobile App use cookies to show you our ads on other sites (including Google). This marketing technique is called “Remarketing”, and it lets us show you special offers based on your past browsing behavior on this site. None of the 3rd party sites where the ads appear would be aware that you came from this site. You can stop this kind of advertising, from us and from others, by going to the following site: <a href="http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">http://www.networkadvertising.org/choices/</a>.</p>
