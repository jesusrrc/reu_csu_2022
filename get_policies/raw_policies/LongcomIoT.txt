<h3 id="last-updated-on">LAST UPDATED ON JUNE 15, 2016</h3>
<p>LONG Building Technologies (“LONG,” “us,” or “we”) collects your personal information for a variety of reasons. This Privacy Policy applies to personal information we collect from you in the course of our business, including information which is collected through our website, www.LONG.com, (the “Site”).</p>
<p>You should read this Policy before using this Site or otherwise providing us with personal information. If you choose to use the Site or otherwise provide us with information about you, you are telling us you have read and agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy and consent to our actions that are consistent with this Privacy Policy.</p>
<h3>SCOPE OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY</h3>
<p>This Privacy Policy describes the types of information about you that we may collect, and describes how we may use your Personal Information. A more in-depth description of this Privacy Policy’s scope, and a fuller definition of “Personal Information,” can be found <a href="#scope-of-this-privacy-policy">here</a>.</p>
<h3 id="information-collection">INFORMATION COLLECTION</h3>
<p>We collect information about you. This information may include:</p>
<p>• information about you that you provide us, for example, by registering to use the site or ordering products on the Site;</p>
<p>• information that you otherwise provide us; and</p>
<p>• Information we collect by automated means.</p>
<p>Please click <a href="#scope-of-this-privacy-policy">here</a> for further information about what we collect.</p>
<h3 id="information-use">INFORMATION USE</h3>
<p>We do not sell or lease your Personal Information. We may use this Personal Information for our business purposes, for example:</p>
<p>• customer service and marketing to you;</p>
<p>• sharing this information with third parties who are working with or for us;</p>
<p>• improving our products and services;</p>
<p>• communication with you; and</p>
<p>• compliance with our legal obligations.</p>
<p>More information on our uses of your Personal Information can be found <a href="#use-and-transfer-of-information">here</a>.</p>
<h3 id="data-security">DATA SECURITY</h3>
<p>We are concerned with the security of your Personal Information and take care in implementing appropriate physical, technical and administrative safeguards. Unfortunately, no data transmission over the Internet, nor any data we collect or store, can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. As a result, while we strive to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee the security of any information you transmit to us. Further information on our security procedures and your responsibility for security can be found <a href="#we-do-not-sell-or-lease-your-personal-information">here</a>.</p>
<h3 id="access-integrity-and-choice">DATA ACCESS, INTEGRITY &amp; CHOICE</h3>
<p>You have choices about whether we use your Personal Information to communicate with you and, if we do communicate with you, what form that communication takes. We will use and share your Personal Information as described in the “Information Use” section of this Privacy Policy. If you wish for us to stop using your Personal Information to communicate with you, you can write us and tell us that you no longer consent to such use (you opt-out). You may also contact us to inquire as to what Personal Information about you we possess. Please do so by contacting us <a href="#contact-information">at the address below</a>.</p>
<p>If you opt-out, we will continue to retain and use your personal information for limited purposes. Click <a href="#access-integrity-and-choice">here</a> for a fuller explanation.</p>
<p>For more information regarding your options with respect to access, integrity and choice, please click <a href="#access-integrity-and-choice">here</a>.</p>
<br/>
<h3>OTHER AGREEMENTS REGARDING CONFIDENTIALITY</h3>
<p>We may enter into one or more written agreements directly with you which contain express provisions regarding the confidentiality of information (“Contract Provisions”). With respect to Personal Information we receive which is subject to any such Contract Provisions, those Contract Provisions, and the Agreement(s) of which those Contract Provision(s) are a part, and not this Privacy Policy, control for all purposes.</p>
<h3>EFFECTIVE DATE AND UPDATES</h3>
<p>We may revise this Privacy Policy at any time. The “Last Updated” date at the top of this Privacy Policy reflects the date of the most recent revisions. You are responsible for regularly reviewing the Privacy Policy. The most current version of the Privacy Policy can be reviewed by clicking on the “Privacy Policy” link located at the bottom of each of our pages associated with the Site.</p>
<h3 id="contact-information">CONTACT INFORMATION</h3>
<p>If you have questions, concerns or requests regarding LONG’s Privacy Policy, or LONG’s collection or use of your Personal Information, please contact us:<br/>LONG Building Technologies, Inc.<br/>Attn: Legal<br/>5001 South Zuni Street<br/>Littleton, CO 80120</p>
<h3 id="scope-of-this-privacy-policy">SCOPE OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY</h3>
<p>This Privacy Policy applies to our collection and use of Personal Information:</p>
<p>• we collect or use from your visit to the Site; and</p>
<p>• that you provide to us by other means (for example, as part of an email you send us).</p>
<p>When we use the term “Personal Information” in this Privacy Policy, we mean information that identifies, or can be used by us or on our behalf to identify, an individual. This can be, for example, a name, street address, e-mail address, or telephone number(s). Personal Information does not include encoded information, anonymized information, or information that is publicly available and not combined with non-public Personal Information.</p>
<p><a href="#last-updated-on">(Return to Summary)</a></p>
<h3 id="information-we-collect">INFORMATION WE COLLECT</h3>
<p>We collect Personal Information from you in certain areas of the Site, as well as through your other interactions with us. The following is a description of what Personal Information we may collect:</p>
<p><strong>Personal Information You Voluntarily Provide.</strong> If you voluntarily provide us with Personal Information, for example your name, email address, phone or mobile number, or payment information (such as a credit card), we collect this Personal Information. You may voluntarily provide this information to us in a variety of ways, including by filling out a registration form on the Site, providing us with Personal Information in order to initiate or complete a transaction, providing online feedback to us, or by contacting us (by email or otherwise) with questions about the Site or our products and services. </p>
<p><strong>Personal Information We May Automatically Collect.</strong> We use cookies and other technologies to collect information about you when you visit the Site or otherwise communicate with us. For example, we may automatically collect information such as: <br/>• Your browser type and operating system.<br/>• Navigation and click-stream data.<br/>• Search terms you use.<br/>• Web pages you view on our Site.<br/>• Links you click.<br/>• Your IP address.<br/>• Sites you visited before coming to our Site.<br/>• Metadata from your communications with us.</p>
<p><strong>IP Addresses.</strong> An IP address is a number assigned to your computer when you connect to the Internet. As part of the protocol of the Internet, web servers can identify your computer by its IP address. In addition, web servers may be able to identify the type of browser and/or type of computer you are using. We collect IP addresses and related information for the purposes of system administration, to assess the traffic to the Site, and to maintain and improve the Site.<br/>For security purposes, we may link account information you enter when you purchase products on the Site to the originating IP address, but we do not otherwise link IP addresses to your personally identifiable information. We can and will use IP addresses and other information we possess to identify a user when we feel it is necessary to enforce compliance with this Privacy Policy or our Terms of Use, to protect our service, the Site, customers or others, or to comply with applicable laws.</p>
<p><strong>Cookies.</strong> Cookies are small bits of information that some websites (including the Site) transfer to an individual's hard drive. We use cookies to improve users’ experience on our Site, for example to personalize features of the Site and for you, and to analyze the traffic, such as total visitors and pages viewed on the Site, and make improvements based on the data we collect. We never save passwords or credit card information in cookies. The use of cookies has become a standard in the industry. Most browsers are initially set up to accept cookies. You can usually set your browser to refuse cookies or indicate when a cookie is being sent. If you refuse cookies, you may not be able to use some of the functionality of the Site. Other partners or third parties associated with the Site may also use cookies in connection with the services and websites to which the Site links. We do not control these cookies.</p>
<p><strong>Web Beacons.</strong> Web beacons are small bits of code that communicate with third parties and are embedded in web pages, our ads or in emails. We may use web beacons to deliver or communicate with cookies, to count users who have visited a web page, to understand usage patterns or for other third party functionality. We also may include web beacons in e-mails to learn if messages have been opened, acted on, or forwarded. <br/>You can disable the ability of Web beacons to store cookie information by declining cookies.</p>
<p><strong>Do Not Track.</strong> As technologies to address Do Not Track signals are still evolving, we do not currently deploy any technology to respond to Do Not Track signals.</p>
<p><strong>Third Party Tracking via the Site.</strong> We do not allow third parties to utilize the Site to collect your Personal Information over time or across sites for such third parties’ own business purposes. Third parties who are service providers to us (for example, assisting us in analyzing traffic to the Site) may collect such information for our internal business purposes only.</p>
<p><strong>Other Technologies.</strong> Examples of other technologies we may use to provide better service to you include:</p>
<p>• <strong>Web Session Variables.</strong> Information that is passed from one web URL to another as you browse.</p>
<p>• <strong>Browser plug-ins/add-ons.</strong> Additional web components that may need to be installed to enable certain web features on our Site. You have the option not to install these components.<br/>As technologies continue to evolve, we may likewise adapt our methods of collection and processing. To the extent we understand such new technologies to materially alter our collection or use methods as described in this Policy, we will amend this Policy.</p>
<p>The Site may contain links to websites operated by third parties. This Privacy Policy does not apply to any information collected from you through such links or that you may provide on the websites you reach by those links. We are not responsible for any actions, practices or policies of other parties.</p>
<p>Please be advised that if you disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive information through public forums or message boards provided by LONG, this information may be collected and used by others. We do not monitor these public spaces and take no responsibility for the content, security or confidentiality of any information posted.</p>

<h3 id="use-and-transfer-of-information">USE AND TRANSFER OF INFORMATION</h3>
<p>Our use of your Personal Information for business purposes. We may use your Personal Information for our business purposes. Such business purposes could include, for example, using the information to provide services to you or complete a transaction with you. We may also use this information for various other business purposes, for example:</p>
<p>• Analyze and improve the performance of the Site.<br/>• Analyze our business, products and services.<br/>• Perform customer service.<br/>• Communicate with you regarding your account.<br/>• Communicate with you regarding products or services provided to you by us or our business partners.<br/>• Market our products and services to you.<br/>• Contact you on behalf of our subsidiaries or external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. <br/>• Ask you about your experience with or opinions about our company, the Site, or our products and services.<br/>• Protect against or identify possible fraudulent transactions.<br/>• Determine the effectiveness of our advertising.<br/>• Enforce our Terms and Conditions and otherwise manage our business.<br/>• Such other purposes as we disclose to you, and to which you consent, at the time we collect Personal Information from you.<br/>• Such other purposes as we disclose to you, and to which you consent, prior to our processing your Personal Information in a manner inconsistent with the terms of this Policy.</p>
<p>We may also provide your Personal Information to third parties who are service providers to us, for example third parties who provide computer-related, credit card processing, mapping, or debt collection services. These third parties will only use your Personal Information as directed by us for our business purposes.</p>
<p>LONG also works with third parties to jointly provide you with certain products or services (we call these third parties “external business partners”). We may provide your Personal Information to our external business partners.</p>
<p>We may also share your information if we merge with, or if we or any of our assets are sold to, another company.</p>
<p>We may also share anonymized data derived from your Personal Information with researchers performing studies in areas relevant to our business. When we do so, we take reasonable steps to anonymize your Personal Information such that it can no longer be used to identify you.<br/>We do not sell or lease your Personal Information.</p>
<p><strong>Our use of your Personal Information for legal purposes.</strong> Sometimes a law, court order or other judicial or administrative process requires us to provide information to a government authority or party to a private lawsuit. We will provide the information that we in good faith believe is required to comply with the law, order, or other process. We may also disclose certain information if:<br/>• we are required to bring, or defend against, litigation or any regulatory proceeding between, or relating to, you and us,<br/>• we have reason to believe that disclosing this information is necessary to identify, contact, or bring legal action against someone who may have breached our Site Terms of Use or who may be causing injury to or interference with (either intentionally or unintentionally) our rights or property, other Site users or anyone else who could be harmed by such activities, or <br/>• we believe in good faith that the law requires it.</p>
<p><strong>Information Transfer.</strong> Your Personal Information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in a country other than the one in which it was provided. When we do so, we transfer the information in compliance with applicable data protection laws.</p>
<p>We take steps to protect Personal Information no matter what country it is stored in or transferred to. We have procedures and controls as appropriate to help ensure this.</p>

We use a combination of technological, physical and policy methods to protect this data such as, for example, requiring a password to access our network and employing firewall security.</p>
<p>You are responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your passwords and any account information. Please be aware that we will never ask you for your password in an unsolicited phone call or e-mail. We do not provide your password to third parties.</p>

<p><strong>Access &amp; Integrity.</strong> If you believe that the Personal Information you voluntarily provided us is, or has become, inaccurate, incomplete or untimely, contact us and provide additional or corrected information. You may also <a href="#contact-information">contact us</a> to inquire as to what Personal Information about you we possess. We reserve the right to charge a reasonable fee to cover our costs in responding to such requests (if such a fee is otherwise allowed by law). We also reserve the right to disregard unreasonable or abusive requests, and we reserve the right, but do not assume any obligation, to verify your identity prior to responding to any such request.</p>
<p><strong>Choice.</strong> When you provide Personal Information to us, you consent to our using that information as described in this Privacy Policy. <br/>If you do not want us to use or continue using your Personal Information to communicate with you, you may request that we not do so (opt-out) by following the procedures described below.</p>
<p>• <strong>Opting-Out after Signup:</strong> If you wish to discontinue any further promotional communications from us, please send your opt-out request to <a href="#access-integrity-and-choice">this address</a>. We will also retain your personal information in order to contact you for non-promotional purposes regarding your use of our service.</p>
