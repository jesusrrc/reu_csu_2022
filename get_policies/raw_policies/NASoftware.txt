<h1 class="contentheading"><span style="color: #000080;"><strong>Data Privacy Policy</strong></span></h1>
<p> </p>
<h2><span style="color: #000080;"><strong>General Browsing</strong></span></h2>
<div class="article-tools" style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">People who access our website remain anonymous. We do not ask people to identify themselves or provide personal details. We do not use IP addresses or monitor other internet resources such as bulletin boards, to gather personal information.</span></div>
<div class="article-tools" style="text-align: justify;"><br/><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">Our web service provider (<u><a href="http://www.connect.org.uk/">Connect Internet Solutions</a></u>) collects domain information. This information is used by them to generate statistics on web usage. We use this data to monitor which pages are found to be most useful to our readers i</span><span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">n accordance with European Union Directive 95/46/EC on Data Protection.</span>
<h2> </h2>
<h2><span style="color: #000080;"><strong>Cookies</strong></span></h2>
<span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">No information is stored in or collected from cookies.</span>
<h2> </h2>
<h2><span style="color: #000080;"><strong>Forms</strong></span></h2>
<span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">There are currently no forms on this website.</span>
<h2> </h2>
<h2><strong><span style="color: #000080;">E-mail</span></strong></h2>
<span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">The following accounts are accessible as mail to and from this site: marketing, support, webmaster, and quality. When you send an e-mail to these accounts, we understand that you have given your informed consent to the processing described below. Information sent to marketing is seen by members of the Sales and Marketing team. The messages are kept in a database. Personal information is used only to respond to questions raised. The e-mail or postal addresses may be used for marketing purposes.</span></div>
<div class="article-tools" style="text-align: justify;">
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Information sent to support is logged automatically in our customer support database. The mail header and the content of the message, including any address or other signature material, are recorded. Personal information is used only to respond to questions raised. The data are also subject to monitoring and statistical analysis to ensure we respond effectively and quickly. The database is pruned periodically to delete old messages.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Information sent to the webmaster is seen by the Company Webmaster. After any appropriate actions or responses have been made, the message is deleted. The information is not logged in a database and will not be used for any other purpose.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif;">Information sent to quality is seen by the Company Quality Manager, and possibly by other Directors or Internal Auditors, if appropriate. After any appropriate actions or responses have been made, the message is deleted. The information is not logged in a database and will not be used for any other purpose.</span></p>
<h2> </h2>
<h2><span style="color: #000080;"><strong>Third Parties</strong></span></h2>
<span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">No personal information is transferred to, sold to, or shared with any third party.</span>
<h2> </h2>
<h2><span style="color: #000080;"><strong>Your Rights</strong></span></h2>
<span style="font-family: arial, helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">If you want to be removed from our mailing lists, or want your details updated, <a href="/index.php/about/how-to-contact-us"><u><span style="color: #0000ff;">write to us</span></u></a>.</span>
<p> </p>
