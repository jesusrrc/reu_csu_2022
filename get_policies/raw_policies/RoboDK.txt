<h1 align="center" style="text-align:center">PRIVACY POLICY</h1>
<p class="text-justify">RoboDK is committed to protecting your privacy and
developing technology that gives you the most powerful and safe online
experience. This Statement of Privacy applies to the RoboDK Software Web site
and governs data collection and usage. By using the RoboDK Software website,
you consent to the data practices described in this statement.</p>
<h2>Purpose and Scope of the Policy</h2>
<p class="text-justify">This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use and
disclose your personal information and how your personal information can be
accessed, rectified, and deleted when necessary. This policy also explains how
cookies are used on robodk.com (the "Website").</p>
<p class="text-justify">This policy does not extend to websites operated by third
parties. Our Website and Service may contain content from third parties or
links to other websites that are not controlled by us. Furthermore, we do not
control privacy policies of such websites or third parties. RoboDK is therefore
not liable for their privacy policies, procedures and practices.</p>
<h2>Personal Information Collected</h2>
<p class="text-justify">RoboDK collects personally identifiable information, such as
your e-mail address, name, home or work address or telephone number. RoboDK
also collects anonymous demographic information, which is not unique to you,
such as your ZIP code, age, gender, preferences, interests and favorites.</p>
<p class="text-justify">There is also information about your computer hardware and
software that is automatically collected by RoboDK. This information can
include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and
referring Web site addresses. This information is used by RoboDK for the
operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide
general statistics regarding use of the RoboDK Website.</p>
<p class="text-justify">Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally
identifiable information or personally sensitive data through RoboDK public
message boards, this information may be collected and used by others. Note: RoboDK
does not read any of your private online communications.</p>
<p class="text-justify">RoboDK encourages you to review the privacy statements of
Web sites you choose to link to from RoboDK so that you can understand how
those Websites collect, use and share your information. RoboDK is not
responsible for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of
the RoboDK Software and RoboDK Website.</p>
<h2>Use of your Personal Information</h2>
<p class="text-justify">RoboDK collects and uses your personal information to
operate the RoboDK Website and deliver the services you have requested. RoboDK
also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other
products or services available from RoboDK and its affiliates. RoboDK may also
contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current
services or of potential new services that may be offered.</p>
<p class="text-justify">RoboDK does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to
third parties. RoboDK may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external
business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you.
In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name,
address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. In addition,
RoboDK may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical
analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange
for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal
information except to provide these services to RoboDK, and they are required
to maintain the confidentiality of your information.</p>
<p class="text-justify">RoboDK does not use or disclose sensitive personal
information without your explicit consent.</p>
<p class="text-justify">RoboDK keeps track of the Websites and pages our customers
visit within RoboDK, in order to determine what RoboDK services are the most
popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within
RoboDK to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a
subject area.</p>
<p class="text-justify">RoboDK Websites will disclose your personal information,
without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief
that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or
comply with legal process served on RoboDK or the site; (b) protect and defend
the rights or property of RoboDK; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to
protect the personal safety of users of RoboDK, or the public.</p>
<h2 id="cookies">Use of Cookies</h2>
<p class="text-justify">The RoboDK Website use "cookies" to help you
personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on
your hard disk by a Webpage server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or
deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can
only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.</p>
<p class="text-justify">One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a
convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the
Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you
personalize RoboDK pages, or register with RoboDK site or services, a cookie
helps RoboDK to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This
simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing
addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same RoboDK
Website, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can
easily use the RoboDK features that you customized.</p>
<p class="text-justify">
We sometimes embed content from some third parties such as social media plugin (e.g. "share" or "like" buttons), 
all of which may use cookies on their website. 
However, the use of these cookies is not covered by this Privacy Policy 
and we have no access to or control over these cookies.</p>
<p class="text-justify">You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web
browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser
setting to decline cookies if you prefer. 
Even if you have accepted part or all of the cookies we use, you may withdraw your consent at any time. 
If you choose to decline cookies, you
may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the RoboDK services
or Websites you visit.</p>
<h2>Security</h2>
<p class="text-justify">
We take reasonable precautions to protect the privacy, accuracy, and reliability 
of any information you provide to us and we use adequate technical 
and organizational safety measures in order to protect your information from 
loss, misuse, destruction, unauthorized access, copy or alteration. 
</p>
<p class="text-justify">RoboDK secures your personal information from unauthorized
access, use or disclosure. RoboDK secures the personally identifiable
information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure
environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When
personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other
Websites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure
Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.</p>
<p class="text-justify">However, while we endeavor to use security measures 
consistent with industry standards and reasonable efforts to safeguard 
the confidentiality of your information, 
we cannot guarantee that information provided to us will not be 
intercepted and remain secure. 
Therefore, you transmit any information to or from the Website and Service at your own risk.</p>
<h2>Changes to this Statement</h2>
<p class="text-justify">RoboDK will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to
reflect company and customer feedback. RoboDK encourages you to periodically
review this Statement to be informed of how RoboDK is protecting your
information. </p>

<p class="text-justify">If you have any concern, question or complaint regarding
