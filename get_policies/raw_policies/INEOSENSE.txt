<p><strong>The data controller is Ineo-Sense (referred to in this policy as “Ineo-Sense”, “we” or “us”).</strong></p>
<p>Ineo-Sense is committed to doing the right thing when it comes to how we collect, use and protect information that identifies or is associated with you as a real person (“personal data”). This is why we have developed this privacy, which:</p>
<ul><li>Outlines the types of personal data that we collect;</li><li>Explains how and why we collect and use your personal data;</li><li>Explains why and when we will share personal data within Ineo-Sense and with other organizations (suppliers and manufacturers); and</li><li>Explains the rights and choices you have when it comes to your personal data.</li></ul>
<hr class="wp-block-separator is-style-dots"/>
<p class="has-medium-font-size"><strong>Which data are collected?</strong></p>
<p>This section describes the personal data we may collect from you when you use our services.</p>
<p>When you contact us, any time Ineo-Sense contacts you, we may collect the following information:</p>
<ul><li>User identification data (surname, first name and email address)</li><li>Service usage data (IP address)</li><li>Correspondence between Ineo-Sense and users</li></ul>
<p>You are free to choose which information you want to provide to us, or whether you want to provide us with information at all. However, some information, such as your name and telephone number, is necessary to process your request. By not providing this information, you will not be able to contact us through our website.</p>
<hr class="wp-block-separator is-style-dots"/>
<p class="has-medium-font-size"><strong>Which use?</strong></p>
<p>Ineo-Sense processes your data in accordance with all applicable data protection laws, and to the best of our ability, we adhere to the principles of those complex laws. We process your data for the purposes explained to you in this Data Privacy Policy. These are mainly used to reply to your contact request.</p>
<p>The most important purposes are:</p>
<ul><li>Non-promotional communication with you on technical, security-related and contractually relevant subjects (e.g. fraud warnings, account blocking or product recall).</li><li>Answer the questions asked in the contact form,</li><li>Production of anonymous statistics on the number of visitors to our Site,</li><li>In order to communicate to you information relating to our company and its activity, its participation in trade fairs, its products and / or services and commercial prospecting messages,</li><li>Carrying out, with your agreement, of surveys and polls</li><li>Production of statistics on the use of the service</li></ul>

<p class="has-medium-font-size"><strong>Who will use your data?</strong></p>

<p>In any case, INEO-SENSE only transmits your personal data to a third party when:</p>
<ul><li>You have given us your prior consent to share this information, or</li><li>INEO-SENSE must share this information with third parties to provide you with the service you request, or</li><li>INEO-SENSE is summoned by a judicial authority or any other administrative authority to communicate the information to it.</li></ul>
