<h1 class="">Privacy Policy</h1>
<h2 class="h4">General Statement of Privacy</h2>
<p>API is committed to respecting your privacy.  This privacy policy describes the information we collect from you, and how we use, share and protect that information.</p>
<h2 class="h4">Automatic Collection of Information</h2>
<p>Our web servers automatically capture certain information including, but not limited to, your IP address or mobile device identifier, the domain name of the web page from which you entered our site, the pages that you visit on our site, the click-through rates, and the amount of time that you spend here. However, this information does not identify you to us. We may use and analyze this data for analytics, trends and to improve our site. If we share any of this information with third parties, it will be provided in aggregate form only so that it does not identify any individual user.</p>
<h2 class="h4">Information You Provide</h2>
<p>If you request information from API or engage in similar activities on this website, you may be required to submit personal information to API.
