<h1>Azbil Corporation Privacy Policy for the Protection of Personal Information</h1>
<ol>
<li><p>Basic Principles</p>
<p>At Azbil Corporation we respect the privacy of personal data — that is, names, addresses, phone numbers and other information that can serve to identify an individual — and we fully appreciate the necessity for appropriate protection and management of such information. We therefore endeavor to protect personal data, in accordance with the following principles:</p>
<ol class="list_lalpha">
<li>We comply with the Act on the Protection of Personal Information and the related laws and regulations, and educate and train employees to handle personal information properly.</li>
<li>We take due care when acquiring personal data.</li>
<li>We take safety control measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, data destruction, data falsification, data leaks, and any other risks.</li>
<li>We continue to work to protect personal information and we take measures for improvement.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li><p>Customers’ Personal Information</p>
<ol class="list_lalpha">
<li>We use personal information provided by customers only for purposes notified when acquiring such information.</li>
<li>We do not provide or disclose customers’ personal information to any third party except when
				<ol class="list_knum">
<li>The individual in question gives his/her consent; or</li>
<li>The law requires or sanctions it; or</li>
<li>The information is provided in such a way that it does not enable any individual to be identified.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>We undertake to preserve the accuracy of all personal information and to manage it in a safe manner. Moreover, if we share personal data with a third party or commission a third party to handle such personal data, we will ensure that the party in question will manage the information in a similarly safe manner.</li>
<li>If customers should wish to see or amend their own personal data, they should make an application through the proper channels (or to our <a href="https://www.azbil.com/inquiry/index.html">Public Relations Section</a>), in which case we will respond with all reasonable promptness.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li><p>Protection of Specific Personal Information</p>
<ol class="list_lalpha">
<li>We comply with the Act on the Use of Numbers to Identify a Specific Individual in the Administrative Procedure, the Act on the Protection of Personal Information, and the related cabinet orders, ministerial orders, and guidelines for handling Individual Numbers and specific personal information (or the equivalent) properly.</li>
<li>We use specific personal information only for the purposes stated when acquiring such information and do not provide or disclose it to any third party even with the consent of the individual in question unless the law requires or sanctions it.</li>
<li>We comply with predefined rules and regulations in acquiring, using, storing, providing, deleting and discarding specific personal information and equivalent information, and take appropriate measures to ensure proper handling.</li>
<li>We understand the importance of specific personal information and take safety control measures according to the predefined rules and regulations.</li>
<li>We have set up an inquiry counter to respond in a proper and prompt manner to inquiries or complaints about our handling of personally indentifiable information, etc., and we strive to resolve such problems.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li><p>Inquiries</p>
<p>Please direct any comments or inquiries regarding this policy or your own personal information to the <a href="https://www.azbil.com/inquiry/index.html">Public Relations Section</a>.</p></li>
</ol>
<p class="txtRight">Established on April 1, 2005<br/>
Revised on January 1, 2016</p>
<p class="txtRight">Azbil Corporation<br/>
Hirozumi Sone , President and Group CEO</p>

<h2>How we use personal information</h2>
<p>Except where stipulated otherwise, Azbil Corporation makes use of customers’ personal information solely for the following purposes:
<ol>
<li>To send customers—by mail, e-mail, fax, telephone, or other means—information relating to Azbil Corporation, its products and services. This may include company profiles, catalogs, and public relations publications.</li>
<li>To send customers information about events that Azbil Corporation has organized or is participating in, including shows and exhibitions, seminars, campaigns, questionnaires, and free offers.</li>
<li>To conduct marketing activities and market research related to the conditions and environments in which Azbil products and services are used.</li>
</ol>
<p>Please note that personal information provided by customers may be shared with <a href="https://www.azbil.com/group/index.html">azbil Group companies</a> for the purposes listed above.</p>
