<p>Our members and guests may be asked to provide certain information when they apply to use the Services such as name, address, telephone number, and billing information (such as a credit card number). The personal information collected from members and guests during the registration process is used to manage each User’s account (such as billing and collections) and to provide the Services.</p>
<p>To better understand its members and guests, we may collect consumer, demographic and other publicly available information from third-parties. We may use this data to provide product and service offers. We may use data in aggregate to negotiate additional benefits and strategic partnerships, to develop products and offers and to better serve our Users.</p>
<p>We may additionally collect personal information from members and guests during their use of our Services. We use this information primarily to provide a customized experience.</p>
<p>We may share identifying information (like name and address) with affiliates or business partners for their use in delivering products and services or related offers to members. Affiliates or business partners are not allowed to use our data for any other purpose other than our approved programs designed to better serve members.</p>
<p>In instances where we jointly promote the Services with a partner, we may provide the affiliate certain personal information, as a result of the joint promotion. In this instance, the information may not be used by the partner for any other purpose. We may also generate aggregate profiles from information provided during registration (such as the total number, but not the names, of members). We may use this aggregated and non-identifying information to sell advertisements that appear on the Services.</p>
<p>We may use registration information to contact you in a variety of ways. We may send you offers through email, phone or regular mail of our products and services or offers on behalf of selected affiliates.</p>
<p>If you deactivate your account with Airly, your personal information will be stored for up to 12 months. This is in case you choose to reactivate your account at any point during this period. If you elect to have all your stored personal information deleted this can be done so upon written request to <a href="mailto:info@flyairly.com">info@flyairly.com</a>.</p>
