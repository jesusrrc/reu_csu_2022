<h3>Delivering actionable intelligence to decision makers</h3>
</div>
</div>
<div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element">
<div class="wpb_wrapper">
<p>DeviceLynk utilizes an Agent installed on local connectivity hardware to gather data from assets and other equipment using standard protocols. These Agents send data to the DeviceLynk application, hosted in the cloud, to create dashboards, charts and trends that provide actionable intelligence.</p>
<p>Equipping your business to manage the changing information landscape, driven by the industrial internet of things (IIoT), is a modern imperative. Yet having the people, time or CAPEX to leverage these advances is out of reach of many companies. DeviceLynk addresses these concerns by providing a cost-effective software-as-a-service (SaaS) solution that is simple to set-up, manage, and use.</p>
