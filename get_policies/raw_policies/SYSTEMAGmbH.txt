<div><div class="sub-definition">

</div><div class="sub-definition">

</div><div class="sub-definition">
<span class="icon" data-mm-icon="optimization"></span><span class="definition"><p>Optimization is the art of maximizing manufacturing efficiency, throughput, OEE, yield, and quality by monitoring, analyzing, and iteratively tuning manufacturing processes.</p></span>
</div><div class="sub-definition">
<span class="icon" data-mm-icon="visualization"></span><span class="definition"><p>Visualization is the art of providing transparency into manufacturing, engineering, and supply chain operations in order to enable continuous optimization.</p></span>
</div><div class="sub-definition">
<span class="icon" data-mm-icon="migration"></span><span class="definition"><p>Migration is the art of exchanging critical business processes and IT systems without disrupting manufacturing operations.</p></span>
