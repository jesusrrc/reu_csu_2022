<p><span style="color:#20252c"><strong><em>Cookies.</em></strong> A cookie is a data file placed on your computer or other device when you visit the Sites or otherwise access our online content. Web beacons or similar technologies may be used for a number of purposes, including, without limitation, to count visitors to the Sites, to monitor how users navigate the Sites, to count how many e-mails that were sent were actually opened or to count how many particular articles or links were actually viewed and to improve your experience on the Sites, including to provide you with content customized to your interests. <p><span style="color:#20252c"><strong><em>Embedded Scripts.</em></strong> An embedded script is programming code that is designed to collect information about your interactions with the Sites, such as the links you click on.
