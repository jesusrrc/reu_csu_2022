<p>The right to data portability</p>
<p>This right means that you can obtain your personal information from us and reuse it for your own purposes – to move, copy or transfer personal data easily from our site to another sites or services. If you want it, we may transmit the personal information directly to another organization (site or service) if this is technically feasible.</p>
<p>We don’t use automated decision making (making a decision solely by automated means without any human involvement) or profiling (automated processing of personal data to evaluate certain things about an individual) systems.</p>
