<h2 class="mb-3">Introduction</h2><p class="mb-3">FYELABS is committed to protecting both the privacy and confidentiality of your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit fyelabs.com, fyelabs.ca, circlab.ca, or any additional subdomains, hereinafter referred to as “our website”.</p><p>This policy was last updated on Wednesday, May 12, 2021.</p><h4> </h4><h3 class="mt-4">Summary</h3><p>-We will not sell or rent any of your personally identifiable information to third parties.</p><p>-We do keep information you voluntarily provide for the personalization of our services, marketing, and statistics.</p><p>-We do not share this information with third parties.</p><p>-You have the right to request personal information we store, and to delete such information.</p><p>-You have the right opt-out of marketing emails.</p><p>-We use third-party providers to collect web analytics to help us optimize our website, and to assess the success of marketing and advertising campaigns.</p><p>-We put in reasonable efforts in securing your personal information.</p><p> </p><h3 class="mt-4">Your Consent</h3><p>By using our website or services, you signify your consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Additionally, as you browse our website, we collect information about the individual web pages, what websites or search terms referred you to our website, and information about how you interact with our website. We refer to this automatically collected information as “Device Information”.</p><p>We collect Device Information using the following technologies:</p><p>-“Log files” track actions occurring on our website, and collect data including your IP address, browser type, Internet service provider, referring/exit pages, and date/time stamps.</p><p>-“Web beacons”, “tags”, and “pixels” are electronic files used to record information about how you browse our website.</p><h4> </h4><h3>How we use your Personal Information</h3><p>We use the Device Information that we collect to help us screen for potential risk and fraud (in particular, your IP address), and more generally to improve and optimize our website (for example, by generating analytics about how our customers browse and interact with our website, and to assess the success of our marketing and advertising campaigns).</p><h2> </h2><h2 class="mt-5 mb-3">Information you Voluntarily Provide</h2><p>Personal information you directly provide (for example, through contact or registration forms) is never shared with any third-party.
