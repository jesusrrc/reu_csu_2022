<p>Thank you for visiting our website. We, at TATA Consulting Engineers Limited, understand that your privacy is important and respect and value your privacy and will only collect and use your personal data in the manner and for the purpose as described in this policy.</p>
<p>In this policy, the term “Privacy” is defined as the person’s right to control access to his or her personal data/information. “Personal Data” is defined as “any information relating to an identified or identifiable person”. Personal Data are, in simple terms, all types of information about a person based on which he/she can (directly or indirectly) be identified. Personal data not only cover the obvious personal information, such as name and contact details including online identity, but it also covers less obvious personal information such as religious, political beliefs, health and genetic or biometric data.</p>

<p>We may collect or process some or all the following personal data:</p>
<div class="listing-style1 mt-3">

<li>Email Address while communicating with us for any enquiry or interesting topic about us.</li>
<li>Any other personal data that you choose to send to us over email</li>
</ul>
</div>
<p class="mt-3">Before you disclose to us the personal data of another person, you must obtain that person's consent to both the disclosure and the processing of that personal data in accordance with this policy.</p>

<p>We will only use the personal data described above for the following purposes:</p>
<div class="listing-style1 mt-3">

<li>Administer and Manage our website using web analytics</li>
<li>Personalize our website for better use by you</li>
<li>Handling the enquiries or any topic of your interest as requested by you</li>
<li>Keep our website secure and available for you to use</li>
<li>Publishing of your personal data will only be in accordance with your consent and/or where permitted by law.</li>
</ul>

Now since these needs can vary for different data types and purposes, the actual retention period may vary significantly.</p>
<p class="lato-bold orange-color">Security of personal data</p>
<p>We will implement the necessary administrative, technical and organizational measures for ensuring a level of security appropriate to the specific risks that we have identified. We protect your personal data against destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorized disclosure of or access to personal data transmitted, stored or otherwise processed.
