This has converged the IoT (Internet of Things) and the blockchain into a decentralized ecosystem for various industries in order that they may establish an efficient, low-cost IoT data exchange platform to give the data more commercial value, according to their own individual needs. On the one hand, it will truly create high-value digital assets. The international standards of the convergence of IoT and blockchain can also be applied to the field of financial technology."</p><p xmlns="http://www.w3.org/1999/xhtml">SDChain is a public blockchain that fully considers the technical characteristics of IoT and the needs of ecosystem development. Based on the existing blockchain technology, SDChain carries out in-depth optimization to effectively realize the circulation and value transformation of digital assets under credit and promote the IoT business model.
