<h3 id="information"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Information We Collect About You</span></h3>The types of information we collect about you depends on your use of our products, services and the ways you interact with us.  This may include information about:<br/><ul><li>How you use or visit our websites, accounts with Dell or applications.</li><li>How you use our services and products and interact with us.</li><li>Contact, billing and other information you provide.</li><li>Improving and developing our products and services.</li><li>Personalizing and recommending our products and services.</li><li>Advertising and marketing to you, including sending promotional communications, targeting advertising and presenting you with relevant offers.</li></ul>We also obtain information from third-party sources. We protect information obtained from third parties according to the privacy practices as described in this Privacy Statement. These third-party sources can change, and may include:<br/><ul><li>Data brokers from which we purchase demographic data to supplement the information we collected about you.</li><li>Communication services, including email providers and social networks, when you give permission to access your information on such third-party services or networks.</li><li>Partners with which we offer co-branded products or services, or engage in joint-marketing activities.</li><li>Publicly available sources such as open government databases.</li></ul>The personal information we collect can include the following:<br/><ul><li><em>Contact details</em> such as your name, email address, mailing address and telephone numbers.</li><li><em>Employment information</em> such as your job title and other business or company information.</li><li><em>Credentials</em> such as your passwords, password hints and similar security information used for authentication and MyAccount access.</li><li><em>Demographic information</em> such as your age, gender, country, interests and preferences. </li><li><em>Web form information</em> you provide in our web forms (forms you choose to complete will indicate whether the data requested is mandatory or voluntary).</li><li><em>Payment information</em> to process payments, such as a credit card number and associated security code.</li><li><em>Account history information</em> related to products or services you purchase and activities associated with your account.</li><li><em>Cookie and tracking information</em> such as IP address, device identifier, location data, browser type and language, access times, the Uniform Resource Locator (URL), other unique identifiers and technical data that may uniquely identify your device, system or browser.</li><li><em>Browser history</em> information about the websites you visit.</li><li><em>Error reports and performance information</em> of the products and any problems you experience, including error reports.</li><li><em>Troubleshooting and help</em> information when you contact Dell to make a purchase or for technical support or customer support services, phone conversations or chat sessions with our representatives which may be monitored and recorded.</li><li><em>Content consumption information</em> about media content (e.g. T.V., apps and games) you access through our products.</li><li><em>Feedback and rating information</em> you provide to us such as customer survey feedback and product reviews you write.</li><li>COVID-19 health and vaccination status and thermal temperature readings (as permitted by applicable law or in compliance with applicable public health directives).</li><li><em>Workspace location information</em>.</li></ul></div>

<h3 id="hwu"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">How We Use And Share Your Information</span></h3>We use and share your information to:<br/><ul><li>Create, deliver and maintain our products and services.</li><li>Establish and maintain your account.</li><li>Measure credit and payment risk.</li><li>Provide account related services and information.</li><li>Help you with customer service and technical support issues or questions.</li><li>Help us improve and personalize our products and services. </li><li>Provide you with marketing and promotional communications and deliver targeted and relevant advertising and relevant offers to you, which includes making better predictions about content and marketing offers that may interest you.</li><li>Authenticate your identity.</li><li>Detect and prevent fraud.</li><li>Manage and protect our networks, services and customers.</li><li>Meet our legal obligations and conduct research.</li><li>Promote the overall health and safety of the workplace, including to help reduce the risk of COVID-19 infections at Dell premises.</li><li>Book desk reservations at Dell premises. </li></ul><h3><span style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 11pt;"><strong>COVID-19 Health Survey Tools, Thermal Vision Camera and COVID Self-Reporting<br/></strong></span></h3>In order to help reduce the risk of COVID-19 infections and keep our communities safe, all Dell employees, contingent workers, and visitors may be asked to complete a COVID-19 health and vaccine survey, attest to their COVID-19 vaccine immunization status and/or COVID-19 negative test results, and/or pass a thermal body temperature screening in order to gain access to Dell premises. The thermal vision camera measures your body temperature on an anonymous basis and Dell does not retain your body temperature. If your body temperature is equal to or above 100.4 degrees Fahrenheit, you will be denied entry and/or asked to leave Dell premises.<br/><br/>The COVID-19 health and vaccine survey collects your name, email address, Dell badge number, work location, COVID-19 vaccination status and limited health data you voluntarily provide. The COVID Self-Report voluntary reporting tool collects your name, email address, Dell badge number, work location, phone number and limited health data you voluntarily provide. Subject to local laws, we will collect proof of your COVID-19:<br/><br/>(a) Vaccine immunization status certificate issued by the local government authority, including digital certificates issued by the local government authority (e.g., COVID-19 digital certificate), or<br/>(b) Negative COVID-19 screening test result including:<br/><ul><li>your first and last name</li><li>specimen collection and negative test result dates</li><li>the entity issuing the COVID-19 negative test result</li></ul><h3 id="ycpr"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Your Consumer Privacy Rights</span></h3>You may personal information.</li><li><em>Right to Portability</em>: you may have the right to receive your personal information, in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format and to have that information transmitted to another organization in certain circumstances.</li><li><em>Right to Deletion</em>: you may have the right to request the deletion of your personal information that we have collected from you.</li><li><em>Right to Be Free from Discrimination:</em> Dell will not discriminate against you for exercising your consumer privacy rights. </li></ul><h3><span
