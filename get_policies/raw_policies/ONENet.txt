<li>For the purposes of the pursuit of the OneNet Consortium legitimate interests. The OneNet Consortium has a legal obligation to process the Personal Data.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>To allow you to use all of the Website’s functionalities. (Performance of a contract)</li>
<li>To check that the Website is functioning correctly. (Performance of a contract)</li>
