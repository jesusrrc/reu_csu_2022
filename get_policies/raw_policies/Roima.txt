<h3 class="wpb_accordion_header ui-accordion-header"><a href="#rights-of-the-data-subjects"><span>Rights of the Data Subjects</span></a></h3>
<div class="wpb_accordion_content ui-accordion-content vc_clearfix">
<div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element">
<div class="wpb_wrapper">
<p>The data subject has several rights under applicable data protection laws.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong><em>Right of access and right of inspection</em></strong></p>
<p>The data subject has the right to obtain confirmation as to whether or not personal data concerning them is being processed.</p>
<p>The data subject has the right to inspect and view data concerning them and, upon a request, the right to obtain the data in a written or electric form. This applies to information that the data subject has provided to Roima insofar the processing is based on a contract/consent.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong><em>Right to rectification and right to erasure</em></strong></p>
<p>The data subject has the right to demand the rectification of incorrect personal data concerning them and to have incomplete personal data completed.</p>
<p>The data subject has the right to require Roima to delete or stop processing the data subject’s personal data, for example where the data is no longer necessary for the purposes of processing.</p>
<p>However, please note that certain personal data is strictly necessary in order to achieve the purposes defined in this privacy policy and may also be required to be retained by applicable laws.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><em><strong>Right to data portability</strong></em></p>
<p>The data subject has the right to receive the personal data that he or she has provided to Roima in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format and, if desired, transmit that data to another controller. The right to data portability applies on the processing of the personal data based on consent or a contract.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong><em>Right to restriction of processing</em></strong></p>
<p>The data subject has the right, under conditions defined by data protection legislation, to request the restriction of processing of his or her personal data. In situations where personal data suspected to be incorrect cannot be corrected or removed, or if the removal request is unclear, Roima will limit the access to such data.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong><em>Right to object to processing</em></strong></p>
<p>The data subject has the right to object to the processing of his or her personal data where Roima is relying on its legitimate interests as the legal ground for processing. For example, the data subject may object to his or her personal data being used for marketing purposes.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong><em>Right to withdraw consent</em></strong></p>
<p>In cases where the processing is based on the data subjects’ consent, the data subject has the right to withdraw his or her consent to such processing at any time.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong><em>Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority</em></strong></p>
<p>The data subject has the right to lodge a complaint with a competent data protection authority if the data subject considers that the processing of personal data relating to the data subject infringes current legislation.</p>
<p>However, we request that the matter will be dealt with Roima in the first instance.</p>
<p>The relevant authority in Finland is the Data Protection Ombudsman (<a href="https://tietosuoja.fi/en/home" rel="noopener" target="_blank">http://www.tietosuoja.fi</a>).</p>
