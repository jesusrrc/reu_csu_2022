<h1>Renault Group and your personal data</h1>
</div>
<img alt="La protection des données personnelles au coeur de l'éthique Renault" loading="lazy" src="https://www.renaultgroup.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/renault-donnees-personnelles.jpg" srcset="https://www.renaultgroup.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/renault-donnees-personnelles-300x105.jpg 300w, https://www.renaultgroup.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/renault-donnees-personnelles-768x268.jpg 768w, https://www.renaultgroup.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/renault-donnees-personnelles-1024x357.jpg 1024w, https://www.renaultgroup.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/renault-donnees-personnelles-390x135.jpg 390w, https://www.renaultgroup.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/renault-donnees-personnelles-1190x414.jpg 1190w, https://www.renaultgroup.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/renault-donnees-personnelles.jpg 1600w"/>
</div>
</div>
<div class="zone-content">
<div class="tpl-white-title-subtitle-txt"><div class="l-zone"><div class="-content"><h3 style="text-align: left;">Protecting your personal data is central to Renault’s values</h3><p style="text-align: left;">Compliance with regulations relating to personal data protection constitutes an opportunity to build trust between Renault Group and its stakeholders (shareholders, customers, suppliers, employee). Since trust is a value which Renault Group particularly values, personal data protection is one of our ethical references in the conduct of our actions.</p><p style="text-align: left;">As such, Renault Group has launched a regulatory compliance program based on governance, procedures and tools dedicated to protecting everyone’s personal data.</p><p style="text-align: left;">As part of this program, Renault Group has:</p><ul style="text-align: left;"><li>Appointed <strong>a Group Data Protection Officer</strong>, who is responsible for enforcing the protection of your personal data throughout the Groupe;</li><li>established <strong>internal policies for the protection of personal data</strong> and built a training program for its employees</li><li>implemented procedures in particular allowing you to <strong>exercise your rights over your personal data</strong>.</li></ul><p style="text-align: left;"></p><h3 style="text-align: left;">Using your personal data with transparency</h3><p style="text-align: left;"><img alt="The Groupe Renault uses your personal data in a manner that respects your privacy" class="wp-image-27510496 size-full aligncenter" height="500" src="https://www.renaultgroup.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/renault-privacy-respect.jpg" width="700"/></p><p style="text-align: left;">In the course of its activities, Renault Group collects, uses and stores some of your personal data, i.e. information that makes it possible to identify you. Renault Group intends to ensure the greatest transparency in the processing it performs on the personal data you provide to it or on the personal data it collects through the various contacts you may have with it.</p><p style="text-align: left;">The Groupe's privacy policies aim to inform you of the conditions of use of your personal data by Renault Group, as well as the rights and options you have to control your data and protect your privacy.</p><p style="text-align: left;">Renault Group uses your personal data in particular to manage its relationship with you, improve its products and services, manage its research and development activities and organize its events.</p><p style="text-align: left;">More detailed information regarding the processing of your personal data (in particular the procedure to follow to exercise your rights) is provided to you at the time that your personal data is collected, on each of the Groupe's websites (Information on the protection of your personal data), mobile application or contractual documentation supplied to you.</p><p style="text-align: left;">For information relating to personal data that we automatically collect, such as IP address and cookies, a cookie policy is also available on each of Renault's websites or mobile applications.</p><p style="text-align: left;"></p><h3 style="text-align: left;">The use of your personal data with respect to obligations</h3><p style="text-align: left;">Renault strives to comply with all applicable obligations relating to the protection of personal data.</p><p style="text-align: left;"><img alt="" class="wp-image-27510493 size-full aligncenter" height="400" src="https://www.renaultgroup.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/personal-data-protection.jpg" width="1200"/></p><p style="text-align: left;">As such, Renault Group ensures that each processing of personal data complies with the principles arising from the applicable regulations:</p><ul style="text-align: left;"><li>Renault Group undertakes to collect and process your personal data for explicit, legitimate and determined purposes in connection with its various activities (determined purpose)</li><li>Only the data strictly necessary to carry out the activity are collected (minimization)</li><li>Data is accurate, complete, and, when necessary, updated (data relevance)</li><li>A data retention policy is defined, and at the end of the retention periods, the data is deleted or anonymized (limited retention)</li><li>An activity creating a high risk for the rights and freedoms of individuals is subject of a prior impact study (data protection impact assessment)</li><li>Data protection is integrated "by design" (privacy by design) and "by default" with a high level of protection (privacy by default)</li><li>Data is secure and kept confidential (security)</li><li>Each processing is based on a legitimate basis provided by law (lawfulness)</li><li>Relations with our partners and service providers are under control and data transfers are supervised</li></ul><p style="text-align: left;"></p><h3 style="text-align: left;">Safe use of your data</h3><p
