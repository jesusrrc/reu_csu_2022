<p><strong>USE OF COOKIES</strong></p>
<p>A “cookie” is a sequence of data that allows you a unique identifier that we save on your computer. Your browser then gives that unique identifier to use each time you submit a query to the website. We use cookies on the website to, among other devices, keep track of assistance you have used, record-filing information, record your user choices, keep you logged into the website, promote purchase procedures, and track the pages you visit. Cookies help us know how the website is being used and enhance your user experience.</p>
<p><strong>TYPES OF COOKIES</strong></p>
<p>The following types of cookies may be used when you visit the Site:</p>
<p><strong>Advertising Cookies</strong></p>
<p>Advertising cookies are stored on your computer by advertisers and ad servers to display advertisements that are most likely to be of interest to you. These cookies allow advertisers and ad servers to collect data about your visits to the website and other websites, rotate the ads sent to a specific computer, and track how often an ad has been viewed and by whom. These cookies are associated with a computer and do not gather any private information about you.</p>
<p><strong>Analytics Cookies</strong></p>
<p>Analytics cookies watch how users entered the website, and how they communicate with and move around once on the website. These cookies let us know what features on the website are working the best and what features on the website can be improved.</p>
<p><strong>Our Cookies</strong></p>
<p>Our cookies are “first-party cookies” and can be either permanent or temporary. These are necessary cookies, without which the website won’t work properly or be able to store certain points and functionalities. Some of these cookies may be manually disabled in your computer’s browser but may influence the functionality of the website.</p>
<p><strong>Personalization Cookies</strong></p>
<p>Personalization cookies are used to recognize repeat visitors to the Site. We use these cookies to record your browsing history, the pages you have visited, and your settings and preferences each time you visit the Site.</p>
<p><strong>Security Cookies</strong></p>
<p>Security cookies help identify and prevent security risks. We use these cookies to authenticate users and protect user data from unauthorized parties.</p>
<p><strong>Site Management Cookies</strong></p>
<p>Site management cookies are used to advance your integrity or session on the website so that you are not logged off surprisingly, and any data you enter is retained from page to page. These cookies cannot be turned off individually, but you can disable all cookies in your browser.</p>
<p><strong>Third-Party Cookies</strong></p>
<p>Third-party cookies may be placed on your computer when you visit the Site by companies that run certain services we offer. These cookies allow the third parties to gather and track certain information about you. These cookies can be manually disabled in your browser.</p>
<p><strong>CONTROL OF COOKIES</strong></p>
<p>Most browsers are set to allow cookies by default. However, you can eliminate or deny cookies in your browser’s settings. Please be knowledgeable that such activity could affect the availability and functionality of the website.</p>
