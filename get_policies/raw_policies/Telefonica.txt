<h2 class="heading-3">1. Our principles and the purpose of this Privacy Policy</h2>
<p>Telefónica Group companies are committed to respecting the privacy of users and the secrecy and security of personal data, in accordance with the provisions of applicable data protection legislation.</p>
<p>Your privacy and the security of your data is our priority. It is part of our DNA and is reflected in the principles that govern our Privacy Policy:</p>
<div class="wp-container-6 wp-block-columns">
<div class="wp-container-4 wp-block-column" style="flex-basis:3%"></div>

<h3 class="heading-4">1.1. Transparency</h3>

<p>This is your right to ask us for details of the data we hold about you and how we process it, and to obtain a copy of it.</p>
<h3 class="heading-4">6.3. Right to rectification</h3>
<p>This is your right to obtain the rectification of your inaccurate or erroneous data, as well as to complete incomplete data.</p>
<h3 class="heading-4">6.4. Right to erasure</h3>
<p>This is your right to request deletion or suppression of your data and information in certain circumstances. However, please note that there are certain occasions when we are legally entitled to continue to retain and process your data, for example, to comply with a legal obligation to retain data.</p>
<h3 class="heading-4">6.5. Right to restriction of the processing</h3>
<p>This is your right to restrict or limit the processing of your data in certain circumstances. For example, if you apply to have your data erased, but, instead of erasing it, you would prefer that we block it and process it only for record- keeping purposes because you will need it later to file a complaint. Again, please note that there may be times when we are legally entitled to refuse your request for restriction.</p>
<h3 class="heading-4">6.6. Right to object</h3>
<p>This is your right to object to our processing of your data for a specific purpose, in certain circumstances provided for by law and related to your personal situation.</p>
<h3 class="heading-4">6.7. Right to data portability</h3>
<p>This is your right to ask us to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable and interoperable format and to transfer it to another data controller, provided that we process your data by automated means.</p>
<h3 class="heading-4">6.8. Right not to be subject to automated individual decisions</h3>
<p>This is your right to ask us, in certain circumstances, not to make you subject to a decision based solely on automated processing of your data, including profiling, that produces legal effects on you or similarly significantly affects you.</p>
