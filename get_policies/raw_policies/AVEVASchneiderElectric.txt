<p><b>Your rights to know what personal data we hold and to control how we use it</b></p>
<p>You have a legal right to know what personal data we hold about you - this is called the right of subject access. You can exercise this right by sending us a written request at any time. Please mark your letter<b>Subject Access Request</b>and send it to us by post using the following contact details:</p>
<p>FAO: Data Protection Officer</p>
<p>Subject Access Request<br/>
AVEVA Group PLC<br/>
High Cross, Madingley Road,<br/>
Cambridge, CB3 0HB<br/>
<br/>
or email us at<a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#b3d7d2c7d2c3c1dcc7d6d0c7dadcddf3d2c5d6c5d29dd0dcde"><span class="__cf_email__" data-cfemail="85e1e4f1e4f5f7eaf1e0e6f1eceaebc5e4f3e0f3e4abe6eae8">[emailprotected]</span></a>.</p>
<p>You also have rights to:</p>
<ul>
<li>object to your personal data being used for marketing purposes (see How we use your personal data for marketing for further details);</li>
<li>object to decisions being made about you by automated means or to your personal data being used for profiling purposes;</li>
<li>object to our using your personal data in ways that are likely to cause you damage or distress;</li>
<li>have inaccurate personal data corrected, blocked or erased;</li>
<li>restrict our use of your personal data;</li>
<li>require that we delete your personal data;</li>
<li>require that we provide you, or anyone that you nominate, with a copy of any personal data you have given us in a structured electronic form such as a CSV file.</li>
</ul>
<p>You can find full details of your personal data rights on the Information Commissioners Office website at ww.ico.org.uk if you are based in the UK, and on <a href="https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/about-edpb/members_en" target="_blank">https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en</a> if you based in the EU (as applicable in your jurisdiction).</p>
<p>The California Consumer Protection Act (the CCPA) provides various rights to California residents with respect to the collection and use of their personal data that we have collected. This includes a right to request that we: (i) disclose to you any personal information that we have about you; (ii) delete personal information that we have about you (subject to certain exceptions); or (iii) not sell your information to a third party (excluding qualified service providers), as that term is interpreted under the CCPA. It is unlawful for us to discriminate against California residents for exercising any rights under the CCPA. If you are a California resident, you can submit such requests to us via the contact information provided above and we will complete the request within the timeframe permitted by law.In addition, you can <a><u></u></a><a href="/en/legal/cookie-policy/">click here</a> <u>to relaunch our cookie banner and disable cookies as part of a do-not-sell request.</u></p>

<div class="cmp-text" style="width:100%;">
<p><b>How we use your personal data for marketing</b></p>
<p>We may use your personal information to send you communications about our products or services that we feel you may be interested in and will add your details to our marketing database if:</p>
<ul>
<li>you make an enquiry about our products or services;</li>
<li>you attend an industry related event;</li>
<li>you buy our products or services;</li>
<li>you have told a third party that you would like them to pass us your contact details so that we can send you updates about our products or services;</li>
<li>you have consented to receiving marketing communications on our website via <a href="/en/contact/">https://www.aveva.com/en/contact/</a></li>
</ul>
<p>This may include marketing relating to products or services offered by other companies within AVEVA that we think you may be interested in. We may send you marketing communications by email, telephone or post.</p>
<p>You can ask us to only send you marketing communications by particular methods (for example, you may be happy to receive emails from us but not telephone calls), about specific subjects (for example about specific portfolio areas or industry specific information) or you may ask us not to send you any marketing communications at all.</p>
<p>We may ask you to indicate your marketing preferences when you first access our website. You can check and update your current marketing preferences at any time by accessing your specified profile link provided in our marketing communications or emailing us at<a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#84e7ebeaf0e5e7f0c4e5f2e1f2e5aae7ebe9"><span class="__cf_email__" data-cfemail="c2a6a3b6a3b2b0adb6a7a1b6abadac82a3b4a7b4a3eca1adaf">[emailprotected]</span></a>.</p>
<p>We may share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.</p>

<div class="cmp-text" style="width:100%;">
<p><b>Automated decision making and profiling</b></p>
<p>If you confirm that you would like to be contacted with further information regarding our products and services we will add your details to our marketing database and a profile regarding your interaction with our website and content will be created.</p>
<p>Once you are added to our marketing database we will update your profile with information regarding your interaction with our website, online portals (including social media) and regarding which products or services you have shown a particular interest. Further interactions with our website will then be linked to this account so we can better tailor our correspondences with the interest that you have shown in AVEVA and our products and services and provide further information on products and services that are likely to be of interest to you.</p>
<p>This information is used to assess your profile objectively to consider whether you might be interested in purchasing new or further AVEVA products and/or services. If we think this is likely, a member of the AVEVA team will contact you to take things forward in this regard.</p>
<p>You are entitled to ask that we do not make automated decisions about you or use your personal data for profiling purposes. Please refer to the section on Your rights to know what personal data we hold and to control how we use it for further details in this regard.</p>

<div class="cmp-text" style="width:100%;">
<p><b>When we will share your personal data with others</b></p>
<p>We share your data with the following people in the day to day running our business:</p>
<ul>
<li>other companies that are part of the AVEVA group;</li>
<li>business partners, suppliers and sub-contractors we work with to provide you with products or services that you have requested from us;</li>
<li>analytics and search engine providers that assist us in the improvement and optimisation of our site.</li>
</ul>
<p>We may also share your personal information with third parties on a one-off basis, for example, if:</p>
<ul>
<li>we sell or buy any business or assets (including our own), in which case we will disclose your personal data to the prospective seller or buyer of such business or assets;</li>
<li>we are under a duty to disclose or share your personal data in order to comply with any legal obligation, or in order to enforce or apply our website terms of use and other agreements; or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our customers, ourselves or others. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organisations for the purposes of fraud protection and credit risk reduction.</li>
</ul>
<p><u>Why might we share your personal information with third parties?</u></p>
<p>We may share your personal information with third parties where required by law, where it is necessary to administer the working relationship with you, where we have another legitimate interest in doing so, or where you have provided your consent.</p>
<p><u>Which third-party service providers process you personal information?</u></p>
<p>"Third parties" includes third party service providers (including contractors and designated agents) and other members of the AVEVA Group, including our marketing teams and business partners. The following activities are carried out by third party service providers: IT services and AVEVA.com live chat box.</p>
<p><u>How secure is your information with third party service providers and other entities in the AVEVA</u> <u>Group?</u></p>
<p>All our third-party service providers (including other entities in the AVEVA Group) are required to take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information in line with our policies. We do not allow our third-party service providers to use your personal data for their own purposes. We only permit them to process your personal data for specified purposes and in accordance with our instructions.</p>
<p><u>When might we share you personal information with other entities in the AVEVA Group?</u></p>
<p>We will share your personal information with other entities in the AVEVA Group as part of our regular reporting activities on company performance, where other AVEVA Group companies act as service providers to us, in the context of a business reorganisation or group restructuring exercise, for the purpose of dealing with your or a third partys HR Helpdesk query, for system maintenance support and hosting of data<b><i>.</i></b></p>

<div class="cmp-text" style="width:100%;">
<p><b>How we keep your personal data safe</b></p>
<p>We take every care to ensure that your personal data is kept secure. The security measures we take include:</p>
<ul>
<li>only storing your personal data on our secure servers;</li>
<li>ensuring that our staff receive regular data security awareness training</li>
<li>keeping paper records to a minimum and ensuring that those which we do have, are stored in locked filing cabinets on our office premises;</li>
<li>maintaining up to date system security features to minimise the risk of unauthorised access to our systems;</li>
<li>Requiring third parties to respect the security of your data and to treat it in accordance with the law.</li>
</ul>
<p>Please remember that you are responsible for keeping your passwords secure. If we have given you (or you have chosen) a password which enables you to access certain parts of our website, you are responsible for keeping this password confidential. Please do not to share your passwords with anyone.</p>
<p>Sending information via the internet is not always secure. While we do our best to protect your personal data, we cannot always guarantee the security of data sent to our website. In this regard, please note that you send us personal data at your own risk. We will nonetheless use strict procedures and security features (some of which are described above) to try to prevent unauthorised access.</p>

<p><b>When we will send your personal data to other countries</b></p>
<p><a id="_Hlk69821341" name="_Hlk69821341"></a>Your personal data may be transferred to, and stored at, a destination outside the country in which you are located by us or by our sub-contractors. For example, our marketing database and associated systems are accessible by AVEVA personnel globally, including outside of the UK / EEA. As AVEVA is global company with global systems, personal data collected in one country may be processed in another country.</p>
<p>AVEVA seeks to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in relation to transfers of personal data to provide adequate levels of protection for your personal data. We will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy. In particular, if any of our processing activities require your personal data to be transferred outside of the UK and / or the European Economic Area, we will only make that transfer if, for example it is to a country which is deemed adequate by the UK Government and / or the European Commission (as applicable) or we have put in place appropriate safeguards to protect your personal data, (such as standard contractual clauses).</p>
<p>Transfers within AVEVA will be covered by an agreement entered into by the members of AVEVA (an intra-group agreement) which contractually obliges each group company to ensure that your personal data receives an adequate and consistent level of protection wherever it is transferred within AVEVA.</p>

Generally, this means that if you have not replied to our marketing emails or purchased any of our products or services for a period of one year we will remove you from our database.</p>

  (function () {
    if (!window.MktoForms2) return;
    // A form has successfully loaded.
    window.MktoForms2.whenReady(function (form) {
      // Get the jQuery form element
      var $form = form.getFormElem();
      // Prefil the form with products if needed.
      var $formWrapper = $form.closest(".marketo-form");
      if ($formWrapper) {
        var enquiryType = $formWrapper.attr("data-name-3");
        var product = $formWrapper.attr("data-value-3");
        if (enquiryType === "products" && product) {
          form.setValues({ enquiryType: 'Products' });
          // Product Field
          var $productField = $form.find("select#product");
          var productFieldValue = "product";
          if (!$productField.length) {
            $productField = $form.find("select#dx_product");
            productFieldValue = "dx_product";
          }
          var validProductTypes = $productField.find("option").toArray().map(function (el) { return el.value.trim().toLowerCase(); });
          if (product) { if (validProductTypes.indexOf(product.trim().toLowerCase()) === -1) { product = ''; } }
          form.setValues({ [productFieldValue]: product });
        }
      }
      // Fetch the nearest button to the form.
      var $modalButton = $form.closest(".smartbutton.embed").find("a[data-toggle]");
      // Check if the modalButton has a download link attached.
      var assetDownloadLink = $modalButton.attr("data-dx-download-link") || null;
      var assetThankYouMessage = $modalButton.attr("data-dx-thankyou-msg") || null;
      var formDefaultThankYouMessage = "<p>Thank you for downloading</p>";
      // Get the TY message from the Marketo form
      var $formThankYouElement = $form.find(".external-thank-you-message");
      if ($formThankYouElement.length) {
        formDefaultThankYouMessage = $formThankYouElement.html();
        $formThankYouElement.closest(".mktoFormRow").hide();
      }
      // Submission element
      form.onSuccess(function () {
        // Hide the form element
        $form.hide();
        // Download link? open this in another window.
        if (assetDownloadLink) {
          window.open(assetDownloadLink);
        }
        // Thank you message, fall back to default but attempt to show data attribute one
        var thankYouMessage = formDefaultThankYouMessage;
        if (assetThankYouMessage) {
          thankYouMessage = assetThankYouMessage;
        }
        // Create the thank you message container
        var $thankYouMessageContainer = $("<div>");
        $thankYouMessageContainer.append(thankYouMessage);
        // Append the thank you message to the form
        $form.closest(".marketo-form").append($thankYouMessageContainer);
        // Stop the redirect
        return false;
      });
    });
  })();
