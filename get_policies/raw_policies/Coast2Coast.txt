<p>At Coast 2 Coast, accessible from https://www.coast2coast.net, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that are collected and recorded by Coast 2 Coast and how we use it.</p>
<p>If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.</p>
<h2>Log Files</h2>
<p>Coast 2 Coast follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this and a part of hosting services’ analytics. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users’ movement on the website, and gathering demographic information.</p>
<h2>Children’s Information</h2>
<p>Coast 2 Coast does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.</p>
