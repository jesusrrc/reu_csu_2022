Such information may include, but is not limited to, the following:</p>
<p><strong>A.- Identification data:</strong> full name, address, home phone, email, cell phone and / or work, marital status, signature, electronic signature, RFC, CURP, INE (IFE), place and date of birth, nationality, age , names of relatives, dependents and beneficiaries and medical information.</p>
<p><strong>B.- Professional data:</strong> occupation, position, area or department, address, telephone and work email, extracurricular activities, job references, personal references, academic history, performance history in the company, among others.</p>
<p><strong>C.- Assets data:</strong> bonuses, wage withholdings, movable and immovable property, credit history, income and expenses, bank accounts, insurance, AFORES, sureties, contracted services, personal references, among others.</p>
<p>We also inform you that in order to fulfill the purposes of the relationship in this notice, sensitive personal data may be collected and processed, such as those that may reveal aspects such as present and future health status, union affiliation, political position and credit history.</p>
<p>We are committed to handle the following data under strict security measures, always guaranteeing their confidentiality.</p>
<p>For the purposes indicated in this notice, we may collect your personal data in different ways: when you provide it to us directly; when you visit our website or use our online services, and when we obtain information through other sources that are permitted by law.</p>
<p><strong>2. Purpose of Data Processing.</strong></p>
<p>Your personal data may be used for the following purposes related to our business relationship:</p>
<p>Marketing the products and services offered by Vitro, its affiliates and / or subsidiaries.</p>
<p>Processing and administration of returns, billing, customer purchase history, payments, discounts, notifications of promotions, pre-sales, access to customer and supplier portal.</p>
<p>Monitor any phone calls made with you.</p>
<p>Update the registers and systems programs of Vitro, its affiliates and or subsidiaries.</p>
<p>Use them in cases of mergers, divisions or acquisitions of Vitro, its affiliates and / or subsidiaries.</p>
<p>Comply with the requirements made by the authorities, when necessary to safeguard the public interest, the procurement or administration of justice.</p>
<p>Where appropriate, prepare and carry out contracts for the sale, consignment, distribution, supply of products or services, among others, according to the case of what the client requires.</p>
<p>Compliance with obligations arising from the legal relationship established with the client.</p>
<p>Provide the services and products required by the client in a timely and correct manner.</p>
<p>Make inquiries, investigations and reviews regarding your complaints or claims.</p>
<p>Contact you for any matter related to the products and services of the Company or to this Privacy Notice.</p>
<p>If you do not want Vitro and its subsidiaries to process your information for advertising, promotional, corporate communication and market research purposes, you can revoke your consent by contacting us at: protecciondedatos@vitro.com</p>
<p><strong>3. Data transfer and transmission</strong><span><br/></span></p>
<p>Your personal data will never be disclosed or shared with third parties (there will be no data transfer), nor will they be used other than as indicated in point 1 mentioned above, unless there is a change in this Privacy Notice.</p>
<p>Nevertheless, we may hire an external third party to manage and administer this Website as well as to support us in understanding how visitors use it.
