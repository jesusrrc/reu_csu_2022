<br/>
</p>
<p class="splunk2-h3">Interactions</p>
<p>When you interact online or offline with Splunk, we may receive your Information, including your Personal Data. For example, we receive your Information when you:</p>
<ul class="splunk-list splunk-body splunk-list-pink">
<li>Visit Splunk’s websites or offices</li>
<li>Download materials through our websites</li>
<li>Provide or update account information through our websites</li>
<li>Register for or attend Splunk-hosted or sponsored events (such as promotional events, webcasts, contests, or hackathons)</li>
<li>Participate in community programs and Splunk-managed repositories on third-party open-source platforms</li>
<li>Communicate with us including by emails, phone, texts, and online chats</li>
<li>Provide testimonials</li>
</ul>
<p>We collect Information about you from other sources such as public databases, commercial data sources, joint marketing partners, resellers, managed services providers and other partners, social media platforms, and conference/event hosts.</p>

</p>

<li>Email address</li>
<li>Physical address, including country</li>
<li>Employer</li>
<li>Title / position</li>
<li>Payment details</li>
<li>Phone number</li>
<li>Username / user ID</li>
<li>IP address</li>
<li>MAC address (or other device identifiers automatically assigned to your device when you access the internet)</li>
<li>Images and related metadata (for example, when visiting our offices or attending an event)</li>
<li>Content of your communications and files you input, upload, or create</li>
<li>Videos (for example, when you provide a product testimonial)</li>
</ul>

</li>
