<div class="search-details-txt">
                        Shanghai SmartState Co.,Ltd.(SSS) is a high-tech enterprise jointly established by Shanghai Jiaotong University, Shanghai Lingang Group and management team. It is located in advanced equipment industrial area of Shanghai Lingang with a factory covering an area of 10,000 square meters. Based on intelligent vehicle powertrain manufacturing, SSS is engaged in the R&amp;D of high precision NC machine tools, the integration of intelligent manufacturing system and the plan and construction of the powertrain smart factory. Relying on the talent intelligence advantage of Shanghai Jiaotong University and the support of local government, SSS undertakes national major projects and maintains good cooperation with well-known car manufactures and manufacturing equipment suppliers at home and abroad, to achieve rapid development.                     </div>
