<p style="text-align: left; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size:12px;"><br/>We design, manufacture and sell ARM Cortex-A and Cortex-M System-On-Modules. On the software side, we provide production-ready, self-maintained Linux Board Support Packages (BSPs) and software distributions for all of our System-On-Modules.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Our SOMs have been deployed in thousands of systems across a wide range of customer applications.</p>
