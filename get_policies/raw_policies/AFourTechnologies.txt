</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400; font-size: 12pt; font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; color: #000000;">By agreeing to access the WEBSITE, you have expressly agreed to this Privacy Policy and given your express consent by accepting the policy.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; color: #000000;"><b>Sensitive Personal Data or Information</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> of a person means such personal information which consists of information relating to-</span></span></p>
