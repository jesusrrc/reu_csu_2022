<h4 class="bx--content-item__heading">
          


    Your Account





    


        </h4>
<dds-content-item-paragraph class="bx--content-item__copy">
<div class="cms-richtext" data-dynamic-inner-content="description" id="rich-text-ac3f751728">
<p><a></a><a></a><a id="Your_Account"></a>You can create an account with IBM by creating an IBMid. An IBMid provides IBM with your name, email address, and country or region of residence. We may require an IBMid for certain services, such as the use of <a href="#IBM_Cloud_and_Online_Services">IBM Cloud and Online Services</a>.<a href="#Your_Account"></a></p>
<p>We may also store your details from business contact information that you provide to us, or that we collect from your organization, our Business Partners, or our suppliers.</p>
</div>
</dds-content-item-paragraph>

<p>An IBMid uniquely identifies you when you access our websites, make a request or order, or use a product or service. If you log into our websites with an IBMid we may link the information we collect with your account. An IBMid is also used to give you access to <a href="#IBM_Cloud_and_Online_Services">IBM Cloud and Online Services</a> and allows you to manage your contract and billing history. The email address in your IBMid may be used to contact you in relation to any services to which you subscribe.</p>
<p>Business contact information is typically information that you would find on a business card, such as name and business contact details. We use this information to contact or communicate with you about business matters. If we receive business contact information from a third party, such as an IBM Business Partner or supplier, we will confirm that the information was shared appropriately.</p>
<p>We may also combine your business contact information with other business-relevant information, such as information about your professional education, skills, work experience, or other publicly available information, such as business-related blogs, publications, job roles, and certifications. This information may be used to tailor our interactions with you in any part of IBM’s business, for example in the sales process, to maintain a relationship with you, and for post-contractual relationships.</p>

    IBM Websites





    


        </h4>
<dds-content-item-paragraph class="bx--content-item__copy">
<div class="cms-richtext" data-dynamic-inner-content="description" id="rich-text-8b73624ca7">
<p><a id="Our_Websites"></a>Our websites offer ways to communicate with you about us, our products, and services. The information that we collect on websites is used to provide you with access to the website, to operate the website, to improve your experience, and to personalize the way that information is provided to you. If you visit our websites without logging in with an <a href="#Your_Account">account</a>, we may still collect information that is connected to your website visit.</p>
<p>For more information on the technologies that we use to collect website information, and setting your preferences, see <a href="#Cookies_and_Similar_Technologies">Cookies and Similar Technologies</a>.</p>

<p>We collect information about your use of our websites, such as:</p>
<ul>
<li>the webpages you view,</li>
<li>the amount of time you spend on pages,</li>
<li>the website URL that referred you to our pages,</li>
<li>your geographic information derived from your IP address,</li>
<li>and any hyperlinks or advertisements you select.</li>
</ul>
<p>We use this information to improve and personalize your experience with our websites, provide you with content that you may be interested in, create marketing insights, and to improve our websites, online services, and related technologies.</p>
<p>We also collect the information that your browser or device automatically sends, such as:</p>
<ul>
<li>your browser type and IP address,</li>
<li>operating system, device type, and version information,</li>
<li>language settings,</li>
<li>crash logs,</li>
<li>IBMid information (if signed in),</li>
<li>and passwords.</li>
</ul>
<p>We use this information to provide you with access to our webpages, improve the webpage view on your device and browser, adapt to your settings and language, and adapt content for relevancy or any legal requirements for your country. We also use this information to comply with system and network security requirements, and to provide support. For more information see, <a href="#Support_Services">Support Services</a> and <a href="#You_and_IBM">Protecting You and IBM</a>.</p>
<p>We also provide platforms and forums that enable online sharing, support, and collaboration among registered members. Any information that you submit to these platforms may be made available others on the internet, or removed by us, as covered in the platform privacy notice or terms. We are not responsible for any content that you make available through your use of our products or services.</p>
<p>We prepare reports on our websites to derive insights into trending topics and general market knowledge. These reports may be provided to third parties with details on how users interacted or showed interest in the third-party product or service that was presented on our websites. All reports display aggregated information and cannot be used to identify our website visitors.</p>
<p>We accept no responsibility for the content provided on, or privacy practices, of third-party websites or applications.</p>

<p>A cookie is a piece of data that a website may send to your browser, which may be stored on your computer and can be used to identify your computer. Web beacons, including pixels and tags, are technologies that are used to track a user visiting an IBM web page or if a web page was copied to another website. Web beacons may be used in email messages or newsletters to determine whether messages are read, forwarded, or links selected. Local Shared Objects can store content information displayed on the webpage visited, and preferences. <p>Session cookies can be used to track your progression from page to page so that you are not asked for information that you have already provided during the current session, or information that is needed to be able to complete a transaction. The IBM Cookie Manager does not address all types of tracking technologies (for example, web beacons). When using mobile apps, use the options on your mobile device to manage settings.</p>
<p>Blocking, disabling, or rejecting IBM cookies may cause services to not function properly, such as in connection with a shopping cart, or block the use of websites or IBM Cloud services that require you to sign in. Disabling cookies does not disable other online tracking technologies, but prevents the other technologies from accessing any details stored in cookies.</p>
<p>Our websites offer the possibility to use third-party social media options.  If you elect to use these options, these third-party sites may log information about you, such as your IP address, access time, and referring website URLs. If you are logged in to those social media sites, they may also link collected information with your profile information. We accept no responsibility for the privacy practices of these third-party services and encourage you to review their privacy policies for more information.</p>
<p>For information on cookies and how to remove these technologies by using browser settings, see <a href="https://www.allaboutcookies.org/" target="_blank">https://www.allaboutcookies.org/</a>. </p>

<h2>
<a data-title="Information Security and Retention" name="anchor-831921916"></a>
  



    Information Security and Retention





    


    </h2>
</div></div>
<div class="rich-text text"><div class="cms-richtext" data-dynamic-inner-content="description" id="rich-text-7962cce77b">
<p>To protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure, we implement reasonable physical, administrative, and technical safeguards. These safeguards include role-based access controls and encryption to keep personal information private while in transit. We also require our Business Partners, suppliers, and third parties to implement appropriate safeguards, such as contract terms and access restrictions, to protect information from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure. </p>
<p>We only retain personal information as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it is processed, or to comply with legal and regulatory retention requirements. Legal and regulatory retention requirements may include retaining information for:</p>
<ul>
<li>audit and accounting purposes,</li>
<li>statutory retention terms,</li>
<li>the handling of disputes,</li>
<li>and the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims in the countries where we do business. </li>
</ul>
<p>We retain any contractual relationship information for administrative purposes, legal and regulatory retention requirements, defending IBM rights, and to manage IBM's relationship with you. The information that is provided in a supplementary privacy notice may provide more detailed information on applicable retention terms.</p>
<p>When personal information is no longer needed, we have processes in place to securely delete it, for example by erasing electronic files and shredding physical records.</p>
</div></div>

<div class="title major-title simple-title"><div class="privacy-title">
<h2>
<a data-title="Your Rights" name="anchor--20206437"></a>
  



    Your Rights





    


    </h2>
</div></div>
<div class="rich-text text"><div class="cms-richtext" data-dynamic-inner-content="description" id="rich-text-44c7f57290">
<p>You have certain rights when it comes to the handling of your personal information. The <strong>Contact Us</strong> form in the header of this page can be used to:</p>
<ul>
<li>request access to the personal information that we have on you, or have it updated. Depending on the applicable law, you may have additional rights concerning your personal information.</li>
<li>ask questions related to this Privacy Statement and privacy practices. Your message is forwarded to the appropriate member of IBM's Data Privacy Team, including the responsible Data Protection Officers.</li>
<li>submit a complaint to IBM if you are not satisfied with how IBM is processing your personal information.</li>
</ul>
<p>Your rights may be subject to limitations and exceptions resulting from applicable laws. For example, there may be situations where we cannot share certain information that you seek if disclosing this means disclosing information about others.</p>
<p>You may also have the right to complain to the competent supervisory authority. Information about additional rights and the right to complain to the competent supervisory authority can be found <a href="https://www.ibm.com/privacy/additional-data-subjects-rights" target="_blank" title="Your Additional Rights ">here</a>.</p>
<p>If you have an unresolved privacy or data use concern that we have not addressed satisfactorily, please contact our U.S.-based third-party dispute resolution provider (free of charge) <a href="https://feedback-form.truste.com/watchdog/request" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
<p>To set or update your marketing communications preferences, visit the <a href="https://myibm.ibm.com/profile/dataprivacypreferences/welcome/us-en" target="_blank">IBM Privacy Preference Center</a>. You can also submit an <a href="https://www.ibm.com/account/reg/us-en/signup?formid=urx-42537" target="_blank">opt-out request</a>, or select <strong>Unsubscribe</strong> at the end of each marketing email.</p>
</div></div>

<h2>
<a data-title="Legal Basis" name="anchor--1472371968"></a>
  



    Legal Basis





    


    </h2>
</div></div>
<div class="rich-text text"><div class="cms-richtext" data-dynamic-inner-content="description" id="rich-text-778ff85302">
<p>In some jurisdictions, the lawful handling of personal information is subject to a justification, sometimes referred to as legal basis. The legal bases that we rely on for the lawful handling of your personal information vary depending on the purpose and applicable law.</p>
<p>The different legal bases that we use are:</p>
</div></div>
<div class="accordion-container">
<div class="bx--content-block">
<div class="bx--content-item" data-autoid="dds--content-item">
<bx-accordion role="list" size="null">
<bx-accordion-item label="Necessary for the performance of a contract with you " role="listitem" size="null" title-text="Necessary for the performance of a contract with you "><div class="rich-text text"><div class="cms-richtext" data-dynamic-inner-content="description" id="rich-text-a3897162d7">
<p>We rely on this legal basis when we need to process certain personal information, such as your contact details, payment details, and shipment details, to perform our obligations or to manage our <a href="#Contractual_Relationship">contractual relationship</a> with you.</p>
<p>Examples:</p>
<ul>
<li>If you intend to purchase a product or service, we require your business contact information to enter into a contract with you or you may need to create an IBMid (see <a href="#Your_Account" target="_self" title="Your Account">Your Account</a><em>)</em> to access a purchased product online<em>.</em></li>
<li>When fulfilling a contact, you may need to receive <a href="#Support_Services">support services</a>, for which we will need to collect your contact information.</li>
<li>We need personal information to consider job applicants or manage the pension entitlements of retirees (see <a href="#Recruitment_and_Former_Employees">Recruitment and Former Employees</a>).</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
</bx-accordion-item>
<bx-accordion-item label="Necessary for the purposes of IBM’s or a third party’s legitimate interest " role="listitem" size="null" title-text="Necessary for the purposes of IBM’s or a third party’s legitimate interest "><div class="rich-text text"><div class="cms-richtext" data-dynamic-inner-content="description" id="rich-text-4ac97e0dbb">
<p>Legitimate interests relate to being able to conduct and organize business, which includes the marketing of our offerings, protecting our legal interests, securing our IT environment, or meeting client requirements.<br/>
Examples:</p>
<ul>
<li>We capture your use of, and interaction with <a href="#Our_Websites">our websites</a> to improve them.</li>
<li>We process your IBMid (see <a href="#Your_Account">Your Account</a>) to manage access authorization of our services.</li>
<li>Where we have a <a href="#Contractual_Relationship">contractual relationship</a> with the organization that you are working for, we have a legitimate interest to process your personal information used to manage this contract.</li>
<li>We process your business contact information (see <a href="#Your_Account">Your Account</a>) in combination with other business-relevant information to tailor our interactions with you and promote our products and services. We may process your contact information together with details of an IBM event you attended to develop <a href="#Marketing">Marketing</a> and business intelligence.</li>
<li>We process the personal information of applicants based on our legitimate interest to source suitable talent (see <a href="#Recruitment_and_Former_Employees">Recruitment and Former Employees</a>).</li>
<li>We have to keep our <a href="#Business_Operations">general business operations</a> functional. To this end we may, for example, processes the login information of our IT systems and networks, or CCTV footage at <a href="#IBM_Locations">IBM locations</a> for security and safety purposes.</li>
</ul>
<p>We may also process personal information where it is necessary to defend our rights in judicial, administrative, or arbitral proceedings. This also falls under the legal basis of legitimate interest in countries where they are not a separate legal basis.</p>
<p>We process personal information for credit protection, which is a specific legal basis under Brazilian law (LGPD) but is also covered under the legal basis of legitimate interest in other countries.</p>
</div></div>
</bx-accordion-item>
<bx-accordion-item label="Consent" role="listitem" size="null" title-text="Consent"><div class="rich-text text"><div class="cms-richtext" data-dynamic-inner-content="description" id="rich-text-1ed43307c2">
<p>The processing is based on your consent where we request this.<br/>
Example:</p>
<ul>
<li>the optional use of <a href="#Cookies_and_Similar_Technologies">Cookies and Similar Technologies</a> or email of <a href="#Marketing">Marketing</a> materials.</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
</bx-accordion-item>
<bx-accordion-item label="Legal obligation" role="listitem" size="null" title-text="Legal obligation"><div class="rich-text text"><div class="cms-richtext" data-dynamic-inner-content="description" id="rich-text-0dff0d8bb2">
<p>Where we need to process certain personal information based on our legal obligation<strong>. </strong>Example:</p>
<ul>
<li>We may be obliged to ask for a government-issued ID for certain transactions, such as for a financing transaction (see <a href="#Contractual_Relationship">Contractual Relationship</a>).</li>
</ul>
</div></div>
</bx-accordion-item>
