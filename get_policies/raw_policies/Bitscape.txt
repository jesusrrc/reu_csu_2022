<h1>Bitscape’s Privacy Policy</h1>
<p> </p>
<p>Your privacy is important to us. This privacy statement explains the personal data Bitscape processes, how Bitscape processes it, and for what purposes.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Bitscape offers a wide range of products, services, packaged IP solutions-based offerings and managed services, including support used to help operate enterprises worldwide. References to Bitscape products in this statement include Bitscape services, websites, apps, software, servers, platforms and devices bundled offerings.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Please read the specific details in this privacy statement, which provide additional relevant information. This statement applies to the interactions Bitscape has with you and the Bitscape offerings, as well as other Bitscape products that display this statement.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>Personal data we collect</strong></p>
<p> </p>
<p>Bitscape collects data from you, through our interactions with you and through our "services", "applications", and "platforms". You provide some of this data directly, and we get some of it by collecting data about your interactions, use, and experiences with our products, services and offerings. The data we collect depends on the context of your interactions or usage of "services", "applications", and "platforms" with Bitscape and the choices you make, including your privacy settings and the products and features you use. We also obtain data about you from third parties.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>You have choices when it comes to the technology you use and the data you share. When we ask you to provide personal data, you can decline. Many of our "services", "applications", and "platforms" require some personal data to provide you with an effective service. If you choose not to provide data necessary to provide you with a product or feature, you cannot use that product or feature or in case of service we cannot render services. Likewise, where we need to collect personal data by law or to enter into or carry out a contract with you, and you do not provide the data, we will not be able to enter into the contract; or if this relates to an existing "services", "applications", and "platforms" or offerings you’re using, we may have to suspend or cancel it. We will notify you if this is the case at the time. Where providing the data is optional, and you choose not to share personal data, features like personalization or similar offerings that use such data will not work for you.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>How we use personal data</strong></p>
<p> </p>
<p>Bitscape uses the data we collect to provide you with rich, interactive experiences. In particular, we use data to:</p>
<p> </p>
<ul style="padding: revert;">
<li>Provide our "services", "applications", and "platforms", which includes updating, securing, and troubleshooting, as well as providing support. It also includes sharing data, when it is required to provide the service or carry out the transactions you request.</li>
<li>Improve and develop our "services", "applications", and "platforms" offerings.</li>
<li>Personalize our "services", "applications", "platforms" and delivery and make recommendations.</li>
<li>Advertise and market to you, which includes sending promotional communications, targeting advertising, and presenting you with relevant offers.</li>
</ul>
<p> </p>
<p>We also use the data to operate our business, which includes analyzing our performance, meeting our legal obligations, developing our workforce, and doing research.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>In carrying out these purposes, we combine data we collect from different contexts (for example, from your use of our two different engagement or "services" or "applications", or "platforms" usage scenario) or obtain from third parties to give you a more seamless, consistent, and personalized experience, to make informed business decisions, and for other legitimate purposes.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>Reasons we share personal data</strong></p>
<p> </p>
<p>We share your personal data with your consent or to complete any transaction or provide any "services", "applications", and "platforms" you have requested or authorized.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>We also share data with Bitscape’s-controlled affiliates and subsidiaries; with vendors working on our behalf; when required by law or to respond to legal process; to protect our customers; to protect lives; to maintain the security of our products; and to protect the rights and property of Bitscape and its customers.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>How to access and control your personal data</strong></p>
<p> </p>
<p>You can also make choices about the collection and use of your data by Bitscape. You can control your personal data that Bitscape has obtained, and exercise your data protection rights, by contacting Bitscape. In some cases, your ability to access or control your personal data will be limited, as required or permitted by applicable law.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>If you want to access or control personal data processed by Bitscape, you can always contact Bitscape at the address in the <a href="/contact-us" rel="noopener" style="color: #007bff;" target="_blank">How to contact us</a> section.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>Cookies</strong></p>
<p> </p>
<p>Most Bitscape sites use cookies, small text files placed on your device which web servers in the domain that placed the cookie can retrieve later. We use cookies to store your preferences and settings, help with sign-in, provide targeted ads, and analyze site operations.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>CCPA Privacy Rights (Do Not Sell My Personal Information)</strong></p>
<p> </p>
<p>Under the CCPA, among other rights, California consumers have the right to: Request that a business that collects a consumer's personal data disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal data that a business has collected about consumers. Request that a business delete any personal data about the consumer that a business has collected. Request that a business that sells a consumer's personal data, not sell the consumer's personal data. If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please <a href="/contact-us" rel="noopener" style="color: #007bff;" target="_blank">contact us</a>.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>GDPR Data Protection Rights</strong></p>
<p> </p>
<p>We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:</p>
<p> </p>
<p>The right to access – You have the right to request copies of your personal data.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>The right to rectification – You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request that we complete the information you believe is incomplete.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>The right to erasure – You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>The right to data portability – You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please <a href="/contact-us" rel="noopener" style="color: #007bff;" target="_blank">contact us</a>.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>Children's Information</strong></p>
<p> </p>
<p>Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Bitscape “services”, “applications”, and “platforms” does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our “website”, “applications”, and/or “platforms”, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>EU-U.S. and Swiss-U.S. Privacy Shield</strong></p>
<p> </p>
<p>Bitscape adheres to the principles of the EU-U.S. and Swiss-U.S. Privacy Shield frameworks. To learn more, <a href="https://www.privacyshield.gov/welcome" rel="noopener" style="color: #007bff;" target="_blank">visit the U.S. Department of Commerce’s Privacy Shield website</a>.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>Contact us</strong></p>
<p> </p>
<p>If you have a privacy concern, complaint, or question for the Bitscape’s Privacy and compliance team, please contact us by using our <a href="/contact-us" rel="noopener" style="color: #007bff;" target="_blank">web form</a> or by emailing us at <a href="mailto:info@bitscape.com" style="color: #007bff;">info@bitscape.com</a> with subject: Attn: Privacy Policy. Our other general contact method is <a href="tel:1-844-294-5383" style="color: #007bff;"><img alt="" src="/~/images/phone-icon.svg"/>1-844-294-5383</a></p>
