<p>Last Changes to Privacy Policy: <strong>March 17, 2022</strong></p>
<p>This Privacy Policy (“<strong>Policy</strong>”) describes how DataRobot (“<strong>we</strong>,” “<strong>us</strong>” or “<strong>our</strong>”) collects, uses, and discloses personal data, and the choices you have related to this.</p>
<p>Please review this Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data. If you do not agree with the practices described in this Policy, please do not access or use our Websites or Services (defined below).</p>
<h3>Privacy Policy Applicability</h3>
<p>This Policy is applicable to data collected through datarobot.com, DataRobot Community, and other websites managed by DataRobot (collectively, the “<strong>Websites</strong>”), through our software products (the “<strong>Solution</strong>”) and through any other interactions you might have with us, such as through email newsletters, online and in person events, and other interactive features and communications.</p>
<p>This Policy does not apply to any third party websites, applications or businesses to which we link or who may link to us. You should review the privacy policies of those third parties to understand how they may collect and use your personal data.</p>
<p>DataRobot’s <a href="https://www.datarobot.com/legal/">Master Subscription Agreement (“<strong>MSA</strong>”)</a> governs delivery and use of the Solution, including any data imported into the Solution by a customer or provided by a customer to import into the Solution on a customer’s behalf (“<strong>Customer Data</strong>”). If you are applying to work with DataRobot, please see our <a href="https://www.datarobot.com/privacy/applicantpolicy/">Applicant Privacy Policy</a> for additional information on how we handle your personal data during and after the application process.</p>
<h3>Privacy Policy Updates</h3>
<p>We may update the terms of this Policy at our discretion. We encourage visitors to frequently check this page for any changes.</p>
<h3>How We Collect</h3>
<p>We may collect your personal data when you:</p>
<ul><li>Visit our Websites, through automated cookies</li><li>Use our Websites or Solution</li><li>Interact with us by communicating through the Solution, Websites, by email, by social media, or by telephone</li><li>Participate in our online communities, blogs or other forums</li><li>Register for or attend an online or in person event sponsored by us</li><li>Submit an application to work for us</li></ul>
<h3>What We Collect</h3>
<p>The types of information we collect from you may differ depending on how you interact with us.</p>
<h4>Personal Data We May Collect from You</h4>
<ul><li>Your name, username, email address, postal address, phone number, and other contact information</li><li>Your employer, job title and other employment related information</li><li>Records of your correspondence with us or on our forums</li><li>Information you provide through other types of interactions, such as surveys, events, social media, or other communication with us</li></ul>
<h4>Personal Data You May Provide to Us</h4>
<ul><li>Device and connection information, including IP address, unique device identifiers, browser type, browser language, and other transactional information</li><li>Approximate location information based on your IP address</li><li>Cookies and tracking technologies that analyze your interaction with our emails, use of the Websites, show traffic data, and log files such as time of access, date of access, software crash reports, session identification number, access times, and referring website addresses (see our <a href="https://www.datarobot.com/cookie-policy/">Cookie Policy</a> for more information)</li><li><strong>User Metrics</strong>: When you use the SaaS version of the Solution, we may automatically collect and analyze data about your usage of the Solution using our <a href="https://www.datarobot.com/legal/uam-overview">User Activity Monitor</a>. This data includes technical logs, frequency of logins, number of models deployed, and feature usage and engagement. For a full list of data points collected through the User Activity Monitor, please see <a href="https://www.datarobot.com/legal/uam-user-activity-types">User Activity Monitor Report Types</a> (collectively “<strong>User Metrics</strong>”). When you use the on-premise version of the Solution, we can only analyze User Metrics if you provide those to us or the organisation that you work for provides those to us. The <a href="https://www.datarobot.com/legal/uam-overview">User Activity Monitor</a> is also accessible to your admin user so that customers may review their own User Metrics. For SaaS users, in addition to the data points collected through our User Activity Monitor, we may collect user interaction and navigation data, including clickstream and mouse tracking. When we use User Metrics for any purposes other than those described below, it is anonymized of personal data and Customer Data in accordance with applicable law.</li><li><strong>Metadata</strong>: When you use the SaaS version of the Solution, we may automatically collect and analyze data that describes your Customer Data, <a href="https://docs.datarobot.com/en/docs/glossary/index.html#modelsmodeling" target="_blank">models</a> and <a href="https://docs.datarobot.com/en/docs/glossary/index.html#project" target="_blank">projects</a>. This includes data points such as dataset summary statistics, dataset size, project type, model accuracy metrics, run times, project and model flags or errors, specific models and<a href="https://docs.datarobot.com/en/docs/glossary/index.html#blueprint" target="_blank"> blueprints</a> run, and the parameters of such models and blueprints (collectively “<strong>Metadata</strong>”). When you use the on premise version of the Solution, we can only analyze Metadata if you provide it to us or the organisation you work for provides it to us. Metadata is always anonymized of personal data and Customer Data.</li></ul>

<ul><li>Business contact information from third party providers, such as event co-hosts, B2B intelligence platforms, or your company, if the Solution is provided to you via your company</li><li>Publically available data from third party sources, like social media</li></ul>

<p>When you sign up for a DataRobot account to access the Solution, we require certain minimum personal information from you. This includes your name and email address. <p>How we use your personal data depends on how you interact with us. The specific purposes for which we use the data we collect about you are listed below. In some jurisdictions, including those subject to the EU General Data Protection Regulation (“<strong>GDPR</strong>”) or UK General Data Protection Regulation (“<strong>UK GDPR</strong>”), we may only process your personal data when we have a legal basis to do so. Our legal basis for processing your personal data is listed with each purpose for processing below.</p>

You may not opt out of these messages, as they are considered part of the Solution.<br/><strong>Legal Basis for Processing:</strong> We may have a contractual obligation to you, or to your company, to provide you with access to and support for the Solution, which requires the processing of your personal contact data. Otherwise, we have a legitimate interest in providing you access to and support for our Websites.</li></ol>
<ul><li><strong>To provide an individualized experience on the Solution and Websites. </strong>We use the information we collect to personalize content and experiences on our Solution and Websites, to better understand your interests and make personalized recommendations based on your interests, and tailor your experience with us to your preferences.<br/><strong>Legal Basis for Processing:</strong> We have a legitimate interest in processing your personal data to improve your experience with us.</li></ul>
<ul><li><strong>To understand and manage our relationship with you.</strong> We use your personal data to understand your use of the Solution and Websites so that we can monitor the health of our relationship with you, and for our enterprise users, identify usage trends and suggest new services or features based on your company’s usage of the Solution. We also report this data back to our enterprise customers, so that you can maximize your company’s use of our Solution.<br/><strong>Legal Basis for Processing:</strong> We have a legitimate interest in making sure that you are getting the full value out of your use of the Solution and Websites, identifying product champions, and making suggestions to optimize your usage or subscriptions.</li></ul>

You can always opt out of these communications by following the opt out instructions in the message or by contacting us through the contact information listed below.<br/><strong>Legal Basis for Processing:</strong> We have a legitimate interest in corresponding with you when you have contacted us, or when we have identified you may have a particular interest in the Solution (when not prohibited by law). Otherwise, when processing your personal data for marketing communications, we rely on your consent.</li></ul>
<ul><li><strong>To create anonymous data for use in product development.</strong> We may remove personal identifiers from data containing personal information so that it cannot be traced back to an individual, and aggregate it by combining it with the data from multiple sources and/or individuals. <ul><li><strong>For security, compliance, fraud prevention and safety. </strong>We may use your personal data as we believe appropriate to investigate or prevent violation of the law, our MSA, our Terms of Service, to secure the Solution, to protect our, your or others’ rights, privacy, safety or property; and to protect, investigate and deter against fraudulent, harmful, unauthorized, unethical or illegal activity. We may also use the information we collect about your device to detect users violating our MSA or Terms of Service and prevent further violations.<br/><strong>Legal Basis for Processing:</strong> We have a legitimate interest in protecting ourselves and our users against unauthorized use of our Solution and Websites to ensure the security of the data processed within them. We are also obligated to process certain personal data to monitor compliance with our MSA and performance of other agreements we may have with you. </li></ul>
<ul><li><strong>For compliance with law or to investigate legal claims. </strong>We may use your personal data to comply with applicable laws, lawful requests and legal process, such as to respond to subpoenas or requests from government authorities.
