<p></p><div style="text-align: justify;">To provide more information about Netronome and its products to customers, Netronome collects personal data (“Personal Information”) from our website and through other means. The Personal Information we collect is solely for the purpose of best meeting your particular needs.</div><p></p><p></p><div style="text-align: justify;">The Personal Information we collect includes, without limitation, your name, address and telephone number. Netronome will occasionally request other Personal Information, depending on the products or services ordered or promoted. While any Personal Information you may provide assists Netronome in delivering information suited to your specific needs, you are not required to provide any Personal Information to Netronome.</div><p></p>
<p><strong></strong></p><div style="text-align: justify;"><strong>How We Collect Your Information</strong></div><div style="text-align: justify;">Netronome collects Personal Information from our registration forms, including information requests, software downloads, webinar registrations, product support purposes and training requests. Netronome also collects Personal Information by phone, mail, email and online.</div><p></p>
<p><strong>How We Use Your Personal Information</strong> <br/>Netronome will limit the use of your Personal Information to: <br/>• Help us identify products and services that may interest you <br/>• Inform you about other products and services <br/>• Maintain our ongoing relationship with you <br/>• To conduct market and customer satisfaction reports <br/>• For product support purposes including technical product support <br/>• As required to protect our rights or in connection with a judicial or administrative proceeding to the extent that such disclosure is required under applicable law <br/>   or court order</p>

We may also use aggregated user data as part of research to develop new web features, tools and content, and for market research related to our products. Netronome is able to recognize your return use of our website through the use of cookie technology. By continuing to use this website, you acknowledge and consent to our use of cookies.</div><p></p>
<p><strong></strong></p><div style="text-align: justify;"><strong>Transfer</strong></div><div style="text-align: justify;">Although Netronome is headquartered in the United States, we operate worldwide with offices and affiliates in other countries. By providing us with your Personal Information, you consent to Netronome’s sharing and transfer of your Personal Information among these international entities.<p></p>
<p><strong></strong></p><div style="text-align: justify;"><strong>Employment</strong></div><div style="text-align: justify;">If you have submitted Personal Information included in a resume or application for employment, Netronome will use that information for recruitment or other human resources purposes.<p></p>
<p><strong></strong></p><div style="text-align: justify;"><strong>Security</strong></div><div style="text-align: justify;">Netronome adheres to all relevant laws regarding the collection, use and protection of your Personal Information including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (Regulation 2016/679) (the “Directive”). With respect to the Personal Information provided by, or collected from you, Netronome will be the Controller of such Personal Information as that term is defined by the Directive.<p></p>
<p></p><div style="text-align: justify;">Netronome uses a range of techniques to ensure that your Personal Information is secure in order to help protect its accuracy and security and to prevent its unauthorized access or improper use.</div><p></p>
