<h2 class="h-2" id="policy">Personal Information Protection Policy</h2>
<p class="text">Relia provides a variety of outsourcing services focused on call center operations. We are fully aware of the importance of our clients' and their customers' personal information, as well as our employees' personal information, and we make every effort to ensure that such information is managed securely. We comply with laws, regulations and internal rules and work to continuously strengthen our information management system in order to protect such personal information.</p>
<ol class="list-ol">
<li class="list-ol_item">Acquisition and Use of Personal Information<br/>
        Personal information we acquire on our clients' users and our employees, etc. is limited to that necessary for business purposes. Use of such personal information shall not deviate from the purpose for which it was acquired.</li>
<li class="list-ol_item">Management and Protection of Personal Information<br/>
        We strictly manage personal information and do not disclose or provide data to third parties without the consent of the individual concerned. We take appropriate measures to prevent unintentional information leaks.</li>
<li class="list-ol_item">Governing Law<br/>
        We comply with applicable laws, regulations, and standards concerning personal information we retain or that is entrusted to us.</li>
<li class="list-ol_item">Ongoing Improvements to Our Personal Information Protection Management Systems and Mechanisms<br/>
        We make ongoing improvements to our systems and mechanisms for managing the protection of personal information.</li>
<li class="list-ol_item">Inquiries<br/>
        We have established a personal information inquiries desk to respond appropriately to inquiries relating to personal information we acquire and use.</li>
</ol>
<p class="text align-right">Enacted: May 23, 2003<br/>
      Revised: June 26, 2018<br/>
      Relia, Inc.<br/>
      Representative Director, President &amp; CEO: Takashi Amino</p>

<h2 class="h-2" id="offer">Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties</h2>
<p class="text">Relia only provides personal information to third parties in the following cases:</p>
<ol class="list-ol">
<li class="list-ol_item">When we have obtained prior consent from the individual concerned.</li>
<li class="list-ol_item">When acquiring personal information for the purpose of provision to a third party and the individual concerned has been notified of the purpose of provision, the items of personal information to be provided, the way in which personal information will be provided and the recipient, and consent has been obtained from the individual concerned.</li>
<li class="list-ol_item">When corresponding to Article 23.1 of the Personal Information Protection Act.</li>
<li class="list-ol_item">When the personal information is provided to third parties within the scope necessary for achieving the purpose of use</li>
<li class="list-notice_item"><span class="list-notice_head">NB:</span><span class="list-notice_body">Includes the provision of personal information of employees to financial institutions, such as banks, to carry out the direct deposit of salaries, etc.</span></li>

<h2 class="h-2" id="consignment">Actions Taken for Security Management</h2>
<p class="text">With regard to retained personal data, Relia shall take the following actions for security management.<br/>
<ol class="list-ol">
<li class="list-ol_item">Formulation of basic policies<br/>
Formulate basic policies regarding "compliance with related laws, regulations, codes, etc.," "items related to safety management actions," and "contact points for handling inquiries and complaints," etc., in order to ensure the proper handling of personal information</li>
<li class="list-ol_item">Establishment of rules regarding handling of personal information<br/>
Formulate rules for handling personal information that prescribe a handling method, a responsible person, assigned persons for the handling and the related duties, etc., for each stage of handling, such as acquisition, use, preservation, provision, deletion, and disposal of personal information;</li>
<li class="list-ol_item">Organizational security management actions<br/>
Establish a responsible person for handling personal information, clarify who the employees are that will handle personal information as well as their roles, and the scope of the personal information to be handled, and establish a system for contacting and reporting to the responsible person in the event a violation of laws or rules for handling personal information, a leak of personal information, or any other problematic circumstances has occurred or there are signs that they may occur;</li>
<li class="list-ol_item">Personal security management actions<br/>
Include items for maintaining confidentiality concerning personal information in the work regulations, and conduct regular training, etc., for employees to make them aware of the necessity of protecting personal information:</li>
<li class="list-ol_item">Physical security management actions<br/>
In each area where personal information is handled, carry out actions that call for physical access control of employees, as well as limiting devices that can be brought into the area, and also actions that call for locked storage and limitation on the removal from the premises of electronic media and documents on which personal information is recorded, in order to prevent theft, misplacement, etc., of personal information;</li>
<li class="list-ol_item">Technical security management actions<br/>
Carry out access control of the information system that handles personal information so that only authorized personnel are able to access to the personal information, and implement a structure to protect from unauthorized access and unauthorized software from the outside; and</li>
<li class="list-ol_item">Fully grasping the external environment<br/>
In cases where the handling of personal information is outsourced to our group company located in a foreign country, fully grasp the system in the relevant foreign country for protecting the personal information, then carry out actions necessary to ensure that the Overseas Group Company is able to continuously conduct security management actions to protect personal information</li>
</ol></p>
