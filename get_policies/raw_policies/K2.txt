<p><b>Cookies</b></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A cookie is a small file of letters and numbers that is downloaded on to your computer when you visit a website, which enables the website to tailor its offerings to your preferences when you visit it.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">K2 uses Cookies to evaluate website visits. A Cookie is a small file which is transferred onto your computer when you visit this website. A cookie can only contain such information that K2 sends to your computer – private data cannot be read with cookies. If you accept the cookies on our website we do not have access to your personal data but we can identify your computer. Most browsers accept cookies as standard. You can, by accessing the properties function, accept or reject temporary or saved cookies separately. If you deactivate cookies, you might not be able to see all features on our website and some website might not be displayed properly.</span></p>
