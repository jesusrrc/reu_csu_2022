<ul>
<li>you create a user account to become a member of kaazing.com or other Kaazing websites;</li>
<li>you make online purchases;</li>
<li>you register products online;</li>
<li>you request support for a Kaazing product;</li>
<li>you register for webinars;</li>
<li>you request information or materials (e.g., whitepapers);</li>
<li>you participate in surveys and evaluations;</li>
<li>you participate in promotions, contests or giveaways;</li>
<li>you submit questions or comments to us.</li>
</ul>
<p>The types of personal information collected may include (but are not limited to):</p>
<ul>
<li>your first and last name;</li>
<li>your title and your company’s name;</li>
<li>your home, billing, or other physical address (including street name, name of a city or town, state/province);</li>
<li>your e-mail address;</li>
<li>your telephone number;</li>
<li>any other identifier that permits Kaazing to make physical or online contact with you;</li>
<li>any information that Kaazing collects and maintains in association with your account, such as your username, your password, and your credit card account information.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Using Your Personal Information</h2>
<p>Kaazing uses your Personal Information primarily to provide you with our products and services and to provide customized content on our Sites that are of interest to you. The information is also used to verify your authority to access our Sites and to contact you when reasonably necessary. Kaazing may also use any information you have provided as reasonably necessary to administer or provide customer support, and to provide you with periodic printed or e-mail communications about new products and services, discounts, special promotions or upcoming events. We also use this personal information to provide you with information related to your account and the products or services you purchased from us, to better understand your needs and interests, to improve our service, and to personalize communications.</p>
<p>For example, Kaazing uses the personal information you provide to:</p>
<ul>
<li>create and maintain your accounts;</li>
<li>process, fulfill, and follow-up on your orders;</li>
<li>register your products;</li>
<li>answer your questions;</li>
<li>register you in programs (per your request);</li>
<li>send you information;</li>
<li>send you surveys.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
<li>As may be required to fulfill orders, to conduct billing/credit card processing, to provide service, and for e-mail housing (as a consequence of uses already described in this Privacy Statement).</li>
<li>As required by law (such as responding to a valid subpoena, warrant, audit, or agency action, or to prevent fraud). For research activities, including the production of statistical reports (such aggregated information is used to describe our services to current and prospective partners and other third parties, and is not used to contact the subjects of the report).</li>
<li>In the context of a business transaction, such as a merger, acquisition, consolidation, or divestiture.</li>
<li>In addition, with your assent, our business partners may send you such information.Kaazing may also contract with various third parties who help us provide and maintain our Sites and our products and services. For example, we use a third-party to process payments made to us, and may subcontract out production, fulfillment, or other operations. In these cases, we will use commercially reasonable efforts to prevent such third parties from disclosing your Personal Information, except for the purpose of providing the services in question.</li>
</ul>

<p>Kaazing may send you e-mail about your account, about the products you have purchased, to remind you about a webcast for which you have registered or a service you have purchased, or in response to your questions. <p>You may let us know whether or not you would like to receive information, special offers, and promotional materials from Kaazing or our business partners when you create a kaazing.com account, when you register for a service, when you provide us with your personal information, or when we send you e-mail.
