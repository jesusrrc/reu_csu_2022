class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><p><em>Last update: 19/11/2020</em></p><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><p>Opti Digital offers technology solutions and consulting to publishers to help them monetize their digital media. </p><p>Amongst the solutions offered by Opti Digital is an advertising marketplace that proposes smart advertising services to publishers and advertisers. This service gathers advertising demand including demand from other advertising buyer partners (hereafter referred to as “Opti Digital Ad Marketplace” or “our services”  or “the services” in this document). </p><p>Advertisers or their intermediaries (e.g. media agencies) display their advertising campaigns through Opti Digital advertising solutions on the websites of our publishers.</p><p>Opti Digital advertising technology collects and uses data to help make online advertising more relevant as well as for ad delivery and reporting purposes.</p><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><h2>Introduction</h2><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><p>Opti Digital SAS (hereafter referred to as “Opti Digital” or “We”, “Us”) recognizes the importance of privacy and is committed to providing transparency and control over the use of the data in online advertising. </p><p>Opti Digital has taken all necessary steps to comply with the provisions of the Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council dated April 27, 2016 and applicable as of May 25, 2018 (the “GDPR”). Opti Digital, when applicable, also adheres to the fullest extent to the IAB Europe’s GDPR Transparency &amp; Consent Framework 2.0.</p><p>Opti Digital is a French company and falls within the competence of the French Privacy Authority, the Commission Nationale Informatique et Libertés (CNIL).</p><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><h2>General scope of this Privacy policy </h2><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><p>This privacy policy details Opti Digital’ data practices on how we collect, use, process and share the data we may collect through our advertising service “Opti Digital Ad Marketplace” as well as the ways in which individuals may exercise control over the use of information collected by us. </p><p>This privacy policy does not describe Opti Digital’ data practices relating to information Opti Digital collects through its website www.optidigital.com, and only applies to Opti Digital Ad Marketplace. </p><p>Your use of the services is also governed by the Collaboration Agreement between Opti Digital and our Publishers.</p><p>As used herein, “personal data” means any information that identifies, relates to, describes, is reasonably capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with a particular Opti Digital customer or consumer or their household.</p><p>This privacy policy may be updated.  If we make any material changes we will notify you by means of a notice on our website prior to the change becoming effective.</p><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:30px"></div><h2>1. User data</h2><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><p>We do not collect or process information related to (i) name, user address, place of work, date of birth, email address, or phone number, (ii) hardware-based identifiers such as UDID or MAC Address or (iii) sensitive targeting categories as defined by the European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance (EDAA) in the European Economic Area (EEA) and under other applicable law (e.g. religion, political opinion, health).</p><p>In order to deliver personalized ads as well as non-personalized ads, we use the following 3 methods to identify users depending on the device they use, whether they access our services on desktop or mobile, and depending on your operating system:</p><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><h3>1.1. Cookie-based identification</h3><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><p>Opti Digital drops a cookie on the user web and/or mobile browser. Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that are created on your device through your web browser for record-keeping purposes. Cookies are used to identify a user and store a randomly generated identifier to identify their browser across visits. <a href="https://www.optidigital.com/cookie-policy/">You may find the list of cookies dropped by Opti Digital here</a>.</p><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><h3>1.2. Cookie-less environments</h3><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><p>For users denying to give their consent to store and access a cookie on their device or in browsers setup which do not allow cookies to be dropped, Opti Digital uses an identification methodology based on IP address non-reversible encryption, browser, and device characteristics. This method allows Us to identify a user in order to provide a quality level of service, such as reporting, contextual targeting and capping of the number of times the same ad is shown to the same user.</p><p>You can opt-out of the use of these technologies as described in Section 8.</p><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:30px"></div><h2>2. Data collected</h2><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><p>We do not collect information about you directly, but we collect data through our web tags on publisher web pages. We also automatically collect the information listed below about your use of Opti Digital Ad Marketplace through tracking technologies such as cookies, pixel tags, and embedded scripts. These tools help us collect and measure session information, time spent on page, and page interaction information.</p><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><h4>Automatically collected data</h4><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><ul><li><strong>Identifiers: </strong>IP address</li><li><strong>Internet or other electronic network activity information:</strong><ul><li>Page URL</li><li>Browser Information (e.g. Browser name and version)</li><li>Device Information (e.g. Device type, OS)</li><li>Network carrier information (e.g. Wifi, 4G or Wired connection)</li><li>User Interactions with ads displayed by Opti Digital Ad Marketplace</li></ul></li><li><strong>Geological Information:</strong><ul><li>Non-Precise Geolocation data: On desktop and mobile web, we collect non-precise location information at the country and/or city level that are derived from user IP Address.</li></ul></li></ul><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><h4><a href="https://www.optidigital.com/cookie-policy/">List of cookies used by Opti Digital</a></h4><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:30px"></div><h2>3. Data use</h2><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><p>We use the information collected for several purposes, including those described below: </p><ul><li><strong>Targeted advertising:</strong> We use the data to filter ad delivery according to the user profile, such as user interests, location, device used or page content;</li><li><strong>User frequency capping:</strong> We use data to prevent displaying the same ad to the same user too many times;</li><li><strong>Traffic and user interactions analisis: </strong>We use data to monitor and report the campaign delivery to advertisers and publishers and for internal business analysis;</li><li><strong>Ads delivery: </strong> We may communicate part of the user profile to programmatic buyers so that they can deliver their advertising campaigns;</li><li><strong>Solutions maintenance and improvement: </strong>We use data to improve our Service.</li><li><strong>Customer service:</strong> We may use data to resolve disputes or other issues related to Opti Digital Ad Marketplace to publishers and advertisers;</li><li><strong>Troubleshooting, debugging, error identification:</strong> We may use data to fix issues that affect the functionality and performance of our Service;</li><li><strong>Opti Digital rights and property protection and complying with law:</strong> We may use data to protect our rights and property and comply with our legal obligations, including to detect, investigate, and prevent fraud and other illegal activities and to enforce our agreements;</li><li><strong>Insights analysis:</strong> We may use the data to deliver aggregated insights, at advertising campaign level to advertisers and publishers or market levels.</li></ul><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:30px"></div><h2>4. Data disclosure </h2><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><h3>4.1. Data communicated to Third Parties</h3><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><p>In order to provide Opti Digital Ad Marketplace, we need to share data (including personal information) with third party vendors, bidders, service providers, contractors or agents who perform functions on our behalf, including the ones listed below. Our service providers are authorized to use the information we provide only as necessary to provide these services to us.</p><ul><li><strong>Programmatic Buyers: </strong>We may send part of the user profile to programmatic buyers using demand side platforms such as Google Display &amp; Video 360, who buy our ad placements through programmatic ad auctions. Those companies are responsible as independent controllers of the data used to deliver their ads on Opti Digital’ network.</li><li><strong>Affiliates</strong>. We may disclose the information we collect through our services to our affiliates or publishers for the purposes described in this privacy policy; however, if we do so, their use and disclosure of your personal information will be subject to this privacy policy.</li><li><strong>Hosting partners</strong>. We utilize third party hosting and data-center providers such as Google Cloud Platform to store data.</li></ul><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><h4 id="tw-target-text">Cookies deposit by Sirdata</h4><p id="tw-target-text">Sirdata is a data marketing company that allows its Customers to send relevant and tailored offers based on Users’ interests. The Data collected by Sirdata is kept for a maximum period of 365 days, depending on the processing, in accordance with applicable laws and the minimization principle.</p><p id="tw-target-text"><strong>Find out more:</strong> <a href="https://www.sirdata.com/privacy/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">https://www.sirdata.com/privacy/</a></p><p id="tw-target-text"><strong>Disable your data collection by Sirdata:</strong> <a href="https://www.sirdata.com/object/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">https://www.sirdata.com/object/</a></p><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><h3>4.2. Other Disclosures</h3><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px"></div><p>We also may disclose information in the following circumstances.</p><ul><li><strong>Business Transfer. </strong>If we are or may be acquired by or merged with another company, if any of our assets are transferred to another company, or as part of a bankruptcy proceeding, we may transfer the information we have collected from you to the other company.</li><li><strong>In Response to Legal Process. </strong>We also may disclose the information we collect from you in order to comply with the law, a judicial proceeding, court order, or other legal processes, such as in response to a court order or a subpoena.</li><li><strong>To Protect Us and Others. </strong>We also may disclose the information we collect from you where we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the safety of any person, violations of our Collaboration Agreement with publishers or this Policy, or as evidence in litigation in which Opti Digital is involved.</li><li><strong>Aggregated and De-Identified Information.</strong> We may share aggregated or de-identified information about users with third parties for marketing, advertising, research or similar purposes.</li></ul><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:30px"></div><h2>5. Please be aware that despite our best efforts, no data security measures can guarantee security.</p><p>You should take steps to protect against unauthorized access to your password, phone, and computer by, among other things, signing off after using a shared computer, choosing a robust password that nobody else knows or can easily guess, and keeping your log-in and password private. We are not responsible for any lost, stolen, or compromised passwords or for any activity on your account via unauthorized password activity.</p><div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:30px"></div><h2>7.
