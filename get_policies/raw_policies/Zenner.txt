Zenner Nieuw Vennep B.V.

<h2> What personal data do we collect? </h2>
Zenner Nieuw Vennep B.V. can process the following personal data:
• Your name and address details
• E-mail address
• Telephone number
<h2> For what purposes do we collect your personal data? </h2>
Zenner Nieuw Vennep B.V. processes your personal data for the following purposes:
• To be able to call or e-mail you if necessary to carry out our services;
• To inform you about changes to our services and products;
• If we are legally obliged to do so.
<h2> Who do we share your personal data with? </h2>
Zenner Nieuw Vennep B.V. does not sell your information to third parties. We may, however, share your personal data with companies affiliated with us, its clients, subcontractors (such as processors) who provide services or carry out assignments on our behalf, such as suppliers, government agencies and other business relations.

Zenner Nieuw Vennep B.V. may be obliged to provide your data due to a legal obligation on us and / or in response to requests from authorities.

Zenner Nieuw Vennep B.V. has taken the necessary organizational and contractual measures to ensure that the personal data is processed and protected in accordance with European and Dutch legislation.
<h2> Cookies </h2>
Cookies are small files that are sent along with the pages of a website and are stored by your browser on your computer’s hard drive. The information stored therein can be sent back to our servers on a subsequent visit. This makes using our website easier for you. Cookies ensure, for example, that you can stay logged in on a website or that your preferences, such as location and language settings, are remembered. <a href="https://zenneropt.grafi-offshore.com/en/cookie-policy/"> View our cookie policy here </a>
<h2> How long do we keep your data? </h2>
Zenner Nieuw Vennep B.V. does not store your data longer than necessary for the execution of the agreements. We use the following retention periods for the following (categories) of personal data:

Customer data: 7 years (in accordance with tax law).
Newsletter subscribers: after unsubscribing from the newsletter, we will delete your data.
Prospects: 2 years.

Complaints about the use of personal data can be addressed to Zenner Nieuw Vennep B.V. or to the Dutch Data Protection Authority.
<h2> How do we protect your personal data? </h2>
Zenner Nieuw Vennep B.V. takes the protection of personal data very seriously and takes appropriate physical, administrative, organizational and technical measures to prevent misuse, loss, unauthorized access, unwanted disclosure and unauthorized modification.
