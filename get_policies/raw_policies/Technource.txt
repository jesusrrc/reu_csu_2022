<div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element">
<div class="wpb_wrapper">
<p>At Technource, we believe in secure and ethical means of business operations. Confidentiality of client data, project details, IP and intellectual property is our prime responsibility. We maintain strict policies for guarding client information and assure that it lies in authorized hands only.</p>
<p><b>To safeguard client information we ensure</b><br>
<b>IP protection</b><br/>
<b>Sign Non Disclosure Agreement</b></br></p>
<p>Apart from this, we operate in secured networks and keep a check on internet usage. Our employees are instructed about IP rights and are made to sign NDA during their induction session. As a part of our moral and professional responsibility, we maintain high security of physical infrastructure throughout our premises.</p>
</div>
</div>
</div></div></div></div><div class="vc_row wpb_row vc_inner vc_row-fluid"><div class="Confidentiality_pera_section Confidentiality_pera_section_bottom wpb_column vc_column_container vc_col-sm-12"><div class="vc_column-inner"><div class="wpb_wrapper"><h2 class="vc_custom_heading section_title" style="text-align: left;font-family:Abril Fatface;font-weight:400;font-style:normal"><span class="ez-toc-section" id="We_maintain_security_of_confidential_information_by"></span>We maintain security of confidential information by<span class="ez-toc-section-end"></span></h2>
<div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element">
<div class="wpb_wrapper">
<ul>
<li>Maintaining a fully equipped development center with internal and external security</li>
<li>Guarding proprietary information and not allowing copying or publishing it in any ways</li>
<li>Providing only authorized access to project information and other technology detail</li>
<li>Segmenting system resources for individual teams</li>
<li>Creating separate networks for individual projects and teams</li>
<li>Allotting discrete IP addresses to each project</li>
<li>Implementing firewall protection on all systems</li>
<li>Taking frequent backups to combat loss of information</li>
<li>Using data encryption at various levels of information transfer</li>
<li>After giving source code and data, we remove complete client data from our systems</li>
</ul>
<p>All this and more is practiced to keep client information, data and intellectual property safe and secure. Our management, and staffs  are dedicated to maintain high level of confidentiality as a part of business rules. Client satisfaction forms the base of our business and so you don’t need to worry about safety of your information!</p>
