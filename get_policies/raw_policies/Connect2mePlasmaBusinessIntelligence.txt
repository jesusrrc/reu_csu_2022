<h2>Privacy Policy</h2>
<p>Plasma Business Intelligence Inc. (“Plasma,” “we,” or “us”) is serious about protecting the private nature of any personal information that you may be asked to provide. This Privacy Policy describes how we treat any personal information we may collect when you visit and use the website C2M.net (the “Site”) or any of the C2M.net Services (“Service). We encourage you to read this Privacy Policy to learn more about the ways in which Plasma uses and protects your personal information.</p>

<p>The C2M.net Site and Service are not intended for use by children. If you are under 18, you may use the Site and/or Service only with involvement of a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are under 13, please do not attempt to register for the Site or Service or send any personal information about yourself to us. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.</p>
<p><strong>Information We Collect Automatically</strong><br/>
Browsing the Site does not automatically require you to register for an account or provide any personal information to us. However, Plasma will gather some generic information automatically. This generic information may include the internet address assigned to your computer (or mobile device), your location, the number and frequency of your visits to the Site, the duration of each visit, the URL that you just came from (whether this URL is on the Site or not), which URL you next go to (whether this URL is on the Site or not), and your computer browser information. Generic information does not reveal the identity of the visitor. This type of aggregate data may enable us to determine how often users access certain parts of the Site or Service, conduct internal research on our users’ demographics, interests, and behavior to better understand, protect and serve you and our user community. Plasma may use this data in aggregate form in any manner. As part of this use of information, we may provide aggregate information, not information about you personally, to our partners about how our users, collectively, use our Service.</p>
<p><strong>Information You Provide to Us</strong><br/>
We receive and store any information you enter on the Site or Service (e.g., via account registration, uploads of content, etc.), including name, phone number, email address and demographic data. You can choose not to provide us with certain information, though this may result in your not being able to take full advantage of certain Site or Service features. The personal information you provide may be used for such purposes as responding to your questions or requests, customizing the content you see, and communicating with you about new Site or Service features. You agree that we may use your personal information to contact you as provided herein, though our intent is that email is the only channel we will use to contact you without your consent.</p>
<p>In order for you to take full advantage of the Site and Service, you must provide registration information, including an e-mail address and ID. We may also request demographic information and unique identifiers in order to provide you with a more personalized service. Once you register for the Site and Service, create channels, and/or give us your personal information, you will no longer be anonymous to us. Usernames and links to public accounts are only shown on your profile with your consent. You always have the option to not provide information by choosing not to register for the Site or Service or to create channels. You are responsible for managing the flow of your private information from one third party service to another. If you post personal information online that is accessible to the public, you acknowledge that such information becomes publicly available and can be collected and used by others and redistributed through the internet and other media channels.</p>

<p>Plasma may also use analytics tools to analyze your use of the Site and Service. Analytics tools generate statistical and other information about website use by means of cookies. The information generated relating to the Site and Service is used to create reports about the use of the Site and Service. Plasma will store and use this information.</p>

Plasma is not responsible for the privacy practices (if any) of such other websites, and you are encouraged to review their privacy policies (if any). <p>We treat personal data as an asset that should be protected against loss and unauthorized access. We use industry-standard Secure Socket Layer (SSL) software to protect the security of your personal information during transmission, which encrypts all of the information you input to the Site or Service. All personal information collected from other services that you provide through the use of the Site and Service is stored securely. Only employees who need personal information to perform a specific job (for example, a customer service representative) are granted access to it. <p><strong>Cookies</strong></p>
<p>When you visit the Site, we may send one or more “cookies” to your computer hard drive through your Web browser. A cookie is a small file containing a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Any use of cookies, web beacons, log file information and similar mechanisms by us will be solely for the purposes of: (1) improving the quality of access to the Site by storing user preferences so that you will not have to re-enter such information during your visit or the next time you visit the Site; (2)tracking user trends, such as how people use the Site, and their Site access preferences; (3) delivering custom, personalized content and information; (4) identifying you when you access, visit, or log-in to the Site or Service; (5) noting the different areas of the Website which have recently been accessed through your computer; (6) monitoring the effectiveness of any promotions or marketing campaigns by Plasma or its affiliates; (7) tracking your entries, submissions, and status in promotions, sweepstakes, and contests; (8) improving the content of the Site and conducting various other diagnostics to improve the Service; and (9) analyzing and improving Site security. Cookies tell us how and when pages in our Site are visited and by how many people. To the extent Plasma uses cookies, such cookies do not collect personal information, and we do not combine information collected through cookies with other personal information to tell us who you are or what your username or e-mail address is. You are always free to decline our cookies if your browser permits, although note that the Site does not respond to “do not track” signals from browsers. Thus, your selection of the “do not track” option provided by your browser may not have any effect on Plasma’s collection of cookie information for analytic and internal purposes. To effectively manage Plasma’s collection of cookie information, you may set most browsers to notify you if you receive a cookie, or you may choose to block cookies through the settings associated with your browser. But, please note that if you choose to erase or block cookies, you will need to re-enter your information to gain access to certain parts of the Site and may not be able to access other parts of the Site.</p>

<p>You may change your email address and contact information by going to the settings page. You must promptly update your personal information if it changes or is inaccurate. You may be able to add or update certain additional information via the Site or Service. When you update information, however, we often maintain a copy of the unrevised information in our records. Please note that information may remain in our records after deletion of your account.</p>
<p><strong>User Communications</strong></p>
<p>You may input your contact information into the Service so that Plasma may contact you. When you enter your contact information or send an email or other communication to Plasma, we may retain those communications in order to process your inquiries and respond to your requests. We do not give or sell this information to any third parties. To the extent your e-mail address is provided to Plasma, you may receive e-mail or other communications from us. You may stop email messages and other promotional mailing by contacting Plasma via e-mail at info@c2m.net.</p>
