<h3 class="elementor-heading-title elementor-size-default">Introduction</h3> </div>

<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-6182950 elementor-widget elementor-widget-text-editor" data-element_type="widget" data-id="6182950" data-widget_type="text-editor.default">
<div class="elementor-widget-container">
<div class="elementor-text-editor elementor-clearfix">
<p>Supply Dynamics Inc. is committed to ensuring the security of our customers by protecting their information. This policy is intended to give security researchers clear guidelines for conducting vulnerability discovery activities and to convey our preferences in how to submit discovered vulnerabilities to us.</p><p>This policy describes what systems and types of research are covered under this policy, how to send us vulnerability reports, and how long we ask security researchers to wait before publicly disclosing vulnerabilities.</p><p>We encourage you to contact us to report potential vulnerabilities in our systems.</p> </div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-24e2ed9 mb-2 elementor-widget elementor-widget-heading" data-element_type="widget" data-id="24e2ed9" data-widget_type="heading.default">

<h3 class="elementor-heading-title elementor-size-default">Authorization</h3> </div>
</div>
<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-3608186 elementor-widget elementor-widget-text-editor" data-element_type="widget" data-id="3608186" data-widget_type="text-editor.default">
<div class="elementor-widget-container">
<div class="elementor-text-editor elementor-clearfix">
<p>If you make a good faith effort to comply with this policy during your security research, we will consider your research to be authorized, we will work with you to understand and resolve the issue quickly, and Supply Dynamics, Inc. will not recommend or pursue legal action related to your research. Should legal action be initiated by a third party against you for activities that were conducted in accordance with this policy, we will make this authorization known.</p> </div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-d9fd2ce mb-2 elementor-widget elementor-widget-heading" data-element_type="widget" data-id="d9fd2ce" data-widget_type="heading.default">

<div class="elementor-widget-container">
<div class="elementor-text-editor elementor-clearfix">
<p>Under this policy, “research” means activities in which you:</p> </div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-da7a8ce elementor-icon-list--layout-traditional elementor-list-item-link-full_width elementor-widget elementor-widget-icon-list" data-element_type="widget" data-id="da7a8ce" data-widget_type="icon-list.default">
<div class="elementor-widget-container">
<ul class="elementor-icon-list-items">
<li class="elementor-icon-list-item">
<span class="elementor-icon-list-icon">
<i aria-hidden="true" class="fas fa-check"></i> </span>
<span class="elementor-icon-list-text">Notify us as soon as possible after you discover a real or potential security issue.</span>
</li>
<li class="elementor-icon-list-item">
<span class="elementor-icon-list-icon">
<i aria-hidden="true" class="fas fa-check"></i> </span>
<span class="elementor-icon-list-text">Make every effort to avoid privacy violations, degradation of user experience, disruption to production systems, and destruction or manipulation of data.</span>
</li>
<li class="elementor-icon-list-item">
<span class="elementor-icon-list-icon">
<i aria-hidden="true" class="fas fa-check"></i> </span>
<span class="elementor-icon-list-text">Only use exploits to the extent necessary to confirm a vulnerability’s presence. Do not use an exploit to compromise or exfiltrate data, establish command line access and/or persistence, or use the exploit to pivot to other systems.</span>
</li>
<li class="elementor-icon-list-item">
<span class="elementor-icon-list-icon">
<i aria-hidden="true" class="fas fa-check"></i> </span>
<span class="elementor-icon-list-text">Provide us a reasonable amount of time to resolve the issue before you disclose it publicly.</span>
</li>
<li class="elementor-icon-list-item">
<span class="elementor-icon-list-icon">
<i aria-hidden="true" class="fas fa-check"></i> </span>
<span class="elementor-icon-list-text">Do not submit a high volume of low-quality reports.</span>
</li>
</ul>

<div class="elementor-widget-container">
<div class="elementor-text-editor elementor-clearfix">
<p>Once you’ve established that a vulnerability exists or encounter any sensitive data (including personally identifiable information, financial information, or proprietary information or trade secrets of any party),you must stop your test, notify us immediately, and not disclose this data to anyone else.</p> </div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-9c37232 mb-2 elementor-widget elementor-widget-heading" data-element_type="widget" data-id="9c37232" data-widget_type="heading.default">

<span class="elementor-icon-list-icon">
<i aria-hidden="true" class="fas fa-check"></i> </span>
<span class="elementor-icon-list-text">Physical testing (e.g. office access, open doors, tailgating), social engineering (e.g. phishing, vishing), or any other non-technical vulnerability testing</span>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-e9a0bdc mb-2 elementor-widget elementor-widget-heading" data-element_type="widget" data-id="e9a0bdc" data-widget_type="heading.default">
<div class="elementor-widget-container">
<h3 class="elementor-heading-title elementor-size-default">Scope</h3> </div>
</div>
<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-898de99 mb-0 elementor-widget elementor-widget-text-editor" data-element_type="widget" data-id="898de99" data-widget_type="text-editor.default">
<div class="elementor-widget-container">
<div class="elementor-text-editor elementor-clearfix">
<p>This policy applies to the following systems and services:</p> </div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-c8e7ba5 elementor-icon-list--layout-traditional elementor-list-item-link-full_width elementor-widget elementor-widget-icon-list" data-element_type="widget" data-id="c8e7ba5" data-widget_type="icon-list.default">
<div class="elementor-widget-container">
<ul class="elementor-icon-list-items">
<li class="elementor-icon-list-item">
<span class="elementor-icon-list-text">supplydynamics.com</span>
</li>
<li class="elementor-icon-list-item">
<span class="elementor-icon-list-text">*.supplydynamics.com</span>
</li>
<li class="elementor-icon-list-item">
<span class="elementor-icon-list-text">benchmark.metalminer.com</span>
</li>
<li class="elementor-icon-list-item">
<span class="elementor-icon-list-text">*.benchmark.metalminer.com</span>
</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-d1bd9ca elementor-widget elementor-widget-text-editor" data-element_type="widget" data-id="d1bd9ca" data-widget_type="text-editor.default">
<div class="elementor-widget-container">
<div class="elementor-text-editor elementor-clearfix">
<p>Any service not expressly listed above, such as any connected services, are excluded from scope and are not authorized for testing. Additionally, vulnerabilities found in systems from our vendors fall outside of this policy’s scope and should be reported directly to the vendor according to their disclosure policy (if any). If you aren’t sure whether a system is in scope or not, contact us at <a href="mailto:security@supplydynamics.com" rel="noopener" target="_blank">security@supplydynamics.com</a> before starting your research.</p><p>Though we may develop and maintain other internet-accessible systems or services, we ask that active research and testing only be conducted on the systems and services covered by the scope of this document. If there is a particular system not in scope that you think merits testing, please contact us to discuss it first. We will increase the scope of this policy over time. Supply Dynamics, Inc. does not operate a bug bounty program for any of our systems or services.</p> </div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-9aa8781 mb-2 elementor-widget elementor-widget-heading" data-element_type="widget" data-id="9aa8781" data-widget_type="heading.default">
<div class="elementor-widget-container">
<h3 class="elementor-heading-title elementor-size-default">Reporting a vulnerability</h3> </div>
</div>
<div class="elementor-element elementor-element-77b2abb elementor-widget elementor-widget-text-editor" data-element_type="widget" data-id="77b2abb" data-widget_type="text-editor.default">
<div class="elementor-widget-container">
<div class="elementor-text-editor elementor-clearfix">
<p>Information submitted under this policy will be used for defensive purposes only – to mitigate or remediate vulnerabilities. If your findings include newly discovered vulnerabilities that affect all users of a product or service and not solely Supply Dynamics, Inc., we may share your report with the Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency, where it will be handled under their coordinated vulnerability disclosure process. We will not share your name or contact information without express permission.</p><p>We accept vulnerability reports at <a href="https://fta.supplydynamics.com/filedrop/security" rel="noopener" target="_blank">this form</a> or via <a href="mailto:security@supplydynamics.com" rel="noopener" target="_blank">security@supplydynamics.com</a>. Reports may be submitted anonymously. If you share contact information, we will acknowledge receipt of your report within 3 business days.</p><p>We do not support PGP-encrypted emails. For particularly sensitive information, submit through <a href="https://fta.supplydynamics.com/filedrop/vulnerability" rel="noopener" target="_blank">this form</a>.</p> </div>
</div>
