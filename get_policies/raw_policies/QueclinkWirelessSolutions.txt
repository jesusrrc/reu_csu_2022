<p>Queclink is committed to being responsible custodians of the personal information that we collect in the course of operating our business as a global provider of wireless machine-to-machine (M2M) devices and solutions.</p>
<p>This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes how we collect, use, share, or otherwise process personal information as a data controller in the course of operating our business, particularly in association with our marketing and business development practices and the operation of our main website (www.queclink.com) (“Site”).</p>

<ol>
<li><strong> The Types of Information We Collect</strong></li>
</ol>
<p>The types of personal information we collect about you depend on your interactions with us and are described below.</p>
<p><strong><em>Information You Provide to Us</em></strong></p>
<p>We collect personal information that you provide to us. For example, we may collect personal information when you contact us, send inquiries, give feedback, request that we send you newsletters, or other materials, fill out a form, respond to a survey, or otherwise communicate with us.</p>
<p>Depending on the context of your interactions with us, the categories of personal information we may collect from you include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Unique Identifiers that we can use to identify you as a unique individual, such as your name, telephone number, postal or e-mail address.</li>
<li>Professional Information related to your occupation, such as your current or previous employers or your job title.</li>
<li>Any other information you choose to provide.</li>
</ul>
<p>You generally can use our Site without providing us with personal information, though we may still collect your information automatically as described in the next section.</p>
<p><strong><em>Information We May Collect Automatically or Generate</em></strong></p>
<p>We may automatically collect and generate information from and about you when you interact with us or our Site, which may include the following categories of information:</p>
<ul>
<li>Device Information: We might automatically collect information pertaining to the device through which you interact with our Site, such as the type of device used to interact with the Site, that device’s operating system and version, your browser type.</li>
<li>Interaction Information: We might automatically collect information about your interaction with our Site, such as the content you view and features you access on our Site, whether and how you interact with content available on our Site.</li>
</ul>
<p>We or third parties engaged on our behalf may use cookies and similar tracking technologies (collectively, “Cookies”) to collect information from and store information on your device when you use, access, or otherwise interact with our Site. For information about how we use Cookies and the choices you may have, please see our <u><a href="/cookie-policy" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Cookie Policy</a></u>.</p>
<p><strong><em>Information We Collect From Other Sources</em></strong></p>

We may collect information about you from publicly available sources, such as public social media profiles, publications, and other websites or materials available through search engines.</p>
<p>We may collect the following categories of information from these other sources:</p>
<ul>
<li>Unique Identifiers, such as name, telephone number, postal or e-mail address and social media identifiers.</li>
<li>Professional Information about your occupation, such as employers and business contact information.</li>
<li>User Content you post or make available online, such as your social media posts or publications.</li>
</ul>
<ol start="2">
<li><strong>How We Use The Information We Collect</strong></li>
</ol>
<p>We may use the information we collect for the various purposes, including:</p>
<ul>
<li>Communication with You. This includes using Personal Information to send you messages, ship products, provide customer care, and respond to your requests.</li>
<li>Business Operations. This includes operating our Site, facilitating product purchases, protecting legal rights, and performing contractual obligations.</li>
<li>Advertising and Marketing. This includes providing you with newsletters, marketing communications, and other information or materials that may interest you, and advertising, providing, and assessing the effectiveness of our events, promotional campaigns, publications, and services.</li>
</ul>
<p>We may use anonymous, de-identified, or aggregate information that does not reasonably identify you for any purpose, as permitted by applicable law.</p>
<p> </p>
<ol start="3">
<li><strong>How We May Share the Information We Collect</strong></li>
</ol>
<p>Queclink does not sell your personal information. We also do not share personal information that reasonably identifies you with unaffiliated entities for their independent use except when we have your permission, are doing so at your direction, as needed to comply with our legal obligations, as permitted by applicable law, or as otherwise described in this Policy.</p>
<p>We may share information with our Queclink offices and business entities located in jurisdictions around the world for any of the purposes listed above.</p>
<p>We may share anonymous, de-identified, or aggregate information that cannot reasonably identify you with others for any purpose, as permitted by applicable law.</p>
<p> </p>
<ol start="4">
<li><strong>Grounds for Processing Your Information</strong></li>
</ol>
<p>Certain jurisdictions require businesses such as Queclink to have legal grounds for processing personal information. Where applicable law requires us to justify our processing activities, we rely on the following legal grounds:</p>
<ul>
<li>Performance of a contract: We may need to collect and use your personal information to enter into a contract you or to perform a contract that you have with us.</li>
<li>Consent: Where required to obtain your consent, we will do so. For example, certain jurisdictions require us to obtain your consent to process precise location information. To withdraw your consent to such use, you can contact us by send us an email <u>privacy@queclink.com</u>. Where required by applicable laws, we will rely on your consent for direct marketing and to collect information from your device or computer.</li>
<li>Legitimate interests: We may use your personal information for our legitimate interests to improve our products and services, as well as the content on our Site. Consistent with our legitimate interests and any choices that we offer or consents that may be required under applicable laws, we may use technical information and personal information for our marketing purposes.</li>
<li>Legal obligation: We may process personal information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations.</li>
</ul>
<ol start="5">
<li><strong>Third-Party Services and Content</strong></li>
</ol>
<p>Our Site may include links to content provided by third parties (such as video materials). This Privacy Policy does not address the privacy, security, or other practices of the third parties that provide such content.</p>
<p>Consistent with applicable law, we engage third parties that support the operation of our Site such as analytics providers. These third parties may track your online activities over time and across different websites and applications. Please see our <u><a href="cookie-policy">Cookie Policy</a></u> for more information about such third party providers and your choices.</p>

<li><strong>Protection and Storage of the Information We Collect</strong></li>
</ol>
<p>We deploy commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to comply with applicable legal requirements and safeguard the information that we collect. when required or appropriate and feasible, obtaining written assurances from third parties that may access your personal information that they will protect such information with safeguards designed to provide a level of protection equivalent to those adopted by Queclink.</p>
<p>However, no information system can be 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information. Moreover, we are not responsible for the security of information you transmit to us over networks that we do not control, including the Internet and wireless networks.</p>
<p>We retain the information we collect for no longer than is reasonably necessary to fulfil the purposes for which we collect the information and to comply with our legal obligations.</p>

        var reg = new RegExp("(^|&)" + name + "=([^&]*)(&|$)"); //构造一个含有目标参数的正则表达式对象
        if(url.split("?").length==1) return null;
        else{
            var r = url.split("?")[1].match(reg);  //匹配目标参数
            if (r != null) return unescape(r[2]); return null; //返回参数值
        }
    }
    $( document ).ready(function() {
    	$(".wpml-ls-menu-item a").click(function(){
    	    var targeturl=$(this).attr("href");
    	    var lang = getUrlParam(targeturl,"lang");
    	    if(lang==""||lang==null) $.cookie("queclink_lang","en", {expires: 1, path: '/'});
    	    else $.cookie("queclink_lang",lang, {expires: 1, path: '/'});
    	    
    	    var cururl = window.location.href;
    	    var curlang =getUrlParam(cururl,"lang");
    	    lang=lang==null?"en":lang;
    	    if(curlang==null){
    	        if(cururl.split("?").length==1) cururl=cururl+"?lang="+lang;
    	        else cururl=cururl+"&lang="+lang;
    	    }
    	    else {
    	        cururl=cururl.replace("lang="+curlang,"lang="+lang);
    	    }
    	    location.href=cururl;
    	    return false;
    	});
