<h1>Privacy policy and terms of use</h1>
<h1 style="font-size:30px">1.   PROCAD privacy policy</h1>
<p>PROCAD Software respects your right to privacy. Any
information provided to PROCAD through this web site will only be used for such
business purposes as:</p>
<ul><li>To assist in the development of software
applications most relevant for today’s drafting requirements. We can best focus
our software development efforts if we know what products and features are of
greatest interest to drafting professionals, and where that interest is coming
from.</li><li>To assist drafting professionals by providing
relevant information about our software (features, upgrades, and special
offers) by email, newsletters, and telephone.</li><li>For billing purposes, in the case of software
orders.</li><li>When permission granted, add to our newsletter
email list to keep you up to date PROCAD news.</li></ul>
<h1 style="font-size:30px">2.   How your data is handled</h1>
<h4><strong>DATA STORAGE LOCATION</strong></h4>
<p>We are Canadian based company and operate web servers hosted in Canada. Our hosting provider adheres to the EU/US “Privacy Shield”, ensuring that your data is securely stored and GDPR compliant. </p>
<h4><strong>CUSTOMER INFORMATION DATA</strong></h4>
<p>When using our website for any communication purpose, we store provided information in your account in Microsoft Dynamics CRM. This includes company information, contacts, technical support requests, general emails and any other communications with PROCAD. You can request to edit or delete this information at any time by contacting us at <a href="mailto:mail@procad.com">mail@procad.com</a>.</p>
<h4><strong>PURCHASE DATA</strong></h4>
<p>When you order a PROCAD subscription or term license, your credit card information is stored by <a aria-label="undefined (opens in a new tab)" href="https://www.elavon.com/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">Elavon</a> for the purposes of record keeping and for monthly recurring payments. This data is highly secured by <a href="https://www.elavon.com/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">Elavon</a> against fraud or unauthorized use. </p>
<h4><strong>GOOGLE ANALYTICS</strong></h4>
<p>We use Google Analytics on our site for anonymous reporting of site usage. No personalized data is stored. </p>
<h4><strong>CASES WHEN YOUR DATA IS USED</strong></h4>
