<h4>1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?</h4>
<h5><span style="font-weight: 400;"></span></h5>
<h5><span style="font-weight: 400;">Information automatically collected by our Products</span></h5>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We collect certain technical information automatically when you use our Products, primarily to deliver services to users and maintain the security and operation of our Products. <h5><span style="font-weight: 400;">Information requested when using our Products</span></h5>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We request certain account information to login and use our Products, namely a “private key” that is owned only by you and pseudonymous (i.e., not linked to any of your personal information). By default, no personally identifiable information (PII), such as name or email address, is required when you use our Products. </span></p>
