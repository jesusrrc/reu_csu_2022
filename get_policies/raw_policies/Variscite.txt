<div class="singleWidget sitBuilderWidget sitBuilderTextWidget" style="">
<div class="title-box"><h2 class="widgettitle bold" style="margin-top:0px;color:#0d0d0d;; font-size: 24px; display: inline-block;"><a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/ISO_13485.pdf" target="_blank">ISO 13485:2016</a></h2></div>
<div class="text-box" style="width: 85%; display: inline-block;"><p>Variscite production is performed at fully ISO 13485 compliant facilities, satisfying an international customer and regulatory requirements for a broad range of industries including medical devices and related services. To download Variscite’s ISO 13485:2016 certification</p><p>
<a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/ISO_13485.pdf" target="_blank">Learn More</a></p></div>

</div><div class="panel-grid-cell panel-grid-cell-empty panel-grid-cell-mobile-last" id="pgc-807-1-2"></div><div class="panel-grid-cell panel-grid-cell-empty" id="pgc-807-1-3"></div></div></div></div><div class="panel-grid panel-has-style showInAll" id="pg-807-2"><div class="compliance-types-row panel-row-style panel-row-style-for-807-2"><div class="innerWrap cols-4"><div class="panel-grid-cell" id="pgc-807-2-0"><div class="singleWidget sitBuilderWidget sobImageWidget"><a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/ISO_9001.pdf" target="_blank"><img alt="ISO 9001:2015" class="img-responsive" src="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/9001-2015-web.jpg" title=""/> </a></div></div><div class="panel-grid-cell" id="pgc-807-2-1">
<div class="singleWidget sitBuilderWidget sitBuilderTextWidget" style="">
<div class="title-box"><h2 class="widgettitle bold" style="margin-top:0px;color:#0d0d0d;; font-size: 24px; display: inline-block;"><a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/ISO_9001.pdf" target="_blank">ISO 9001:2015</a></h2></div>
<div class="text-box" style="width: 85%; display: inline-block;"><p>Variscite is a fully certified ISO 9001:2015 company, as a cornerstone for Variscite’s overall Quality Control methodology of developing and producing top quality products. From early design concept and up to production and customer service, Variscite sets the bar for the highest quality products and services in the SOM industry. To download Variscite’s ISO 9001:2015 certification</p><p>
<a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/06/ISO_9001.pdf" target="_blank">Learn More</a></p></div>
</div>
</div><div class="panel-grid-cell" id="pgc-807-2-2"><div class="singleWidget sitBuilderWidget sobImageWidget"><a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/UL-94V0_Compliance.pdf" target="_blank"><img alt="UL 94V-0" class="img-responsive" src="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/ur-2x.jpg" title=""/> </a></div></div><div class="panel-grid-cell" id="pgc-807-2-3">
<div class="singleWidget sitBuilderWidget sitBuilderTextWidget" style="">
<div class="title-box"><h2 class="widgettitle bold" style="margin-top:0px;color:#0d0d0d;; font-size: 24px; display: inline-block;"><a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/UL-94V0_Compliance.pdf" target="_blank">UL 94V-0</a></h2></div>
<div class="text-box" style="width: 85%; display: inline-block;"><p>UL 94 is the Standard for safety of flammability of plastic materials released by Underwriters Laboratories (UL) of the USA. The standard classifies plastics according to how they burn in various orientations and thicknesses. All Variscite's products are based on PCBs and components classified as UL 94V-0, which is the most flame-resistance class under the UL 94 standard.</p><p>
<a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/UL-94V0_Compliance.pdf" target="_blank">Learn More</a></p></div>
</div>
</div></div></div></div><div class="panel-grid panel-has-style showInAll" id="pg-807-3"><div class="compliance-types-row panel-row-style panel-row-style-for-807-3"><div class="innerWrap cols-4"><div class="panel-grid-cell" id="pgc-807-3-0"><div class="singleWidget sitBuilderWidget sobImageWidget"><a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Variscite_ISO_140012015.pdf" target="_blank"><img alt="ISO 14001:2015" class="img-responsive" src="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/iso-2015-g.jpg" title=""/> </a></div></div><div class="panel-grid-cell" id="pgc-807-3-1">
<div class="singleWidget sitBuilderWidget sitBuilderTextWidget" style="">
<div class="title-box"><h2 class="widgettitle bold" style="margin-top:0px;color:#0d0d0d;; font-size: 24px; display: inline-block;"><a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Variscite_ISO_140012015.pdf" target="_blank">ISO 14001:2015</a></h2></div>
<div class="text-box" style="width: 85%; display: inline-block;"><p>Variscite has achieved the internationally recognized ISO 14001:2015 certification awarded for an organization's environmental performance. By meeting the certification's standards, Variscite takes a significant step towards fulfilling its social and environmental responsibility while being up to date with the latest best-practice in environmental management. To download Variscite’s ISO 14001:2015 certification </p><p>
<a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Variscite_ISO_140012015.pdf" target="_blank">Learn More</a></p></div>
</div>
</div><div class="panel-grid-cell" id="pgc-807-3-2"><div class="singleWidget sitBuilderWidget sobImageWidget"><a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Variscite_RoHS_compliance.pdf" target="_blank"><img alt="RoHS Compliant I / II / III" class="img-responsive" src="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/RoHS-Compliant.jpg" title=""/> </a></div></div><div class="panel-grid-cell" id="pgc-807-3-3">
<div class="singleWidget sitBuilderWidget sitBuilderTextWidget" style="">
<div class="title-box"><h2 class="widgettitle bold" style="margin-top:0px;color:#0d0d0d;; font-size: 24px; display: inline-block;"><a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Variscite_RoHS_compliance.pdf" target="_blank">RoHS I / II / III</a></h2></div>
<div class="text-box" style="width: 85%; display: inline-block;"><p>Variscite certifies to the best of its knowledge that Variscite's products are compliant with the threshold requirements of the European Union Restriction on Use of Hazardous Substance Directive 2002/95/EC ("RoHS 1"), Directive 2011/65/EU ("RoHS 2"), Directive (EU) 2015/863 amendment of Directive 2011/65/EU ("RoHS 3") and amendment (EU) 2017/2102 to RoHS 2 - all collectively deחeferto as "RoHS", as well as the China Management Methods for controlling Pollution by Electronic Information Products ("China RoHS").</p><p>
<a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Variscite_RoHS_compliance.pdf" target="_blank">Learn More</a></p></div>
</div>
</div></div></div></div><div class="panel-grid panel-has-style showInAll" id="pg-807-4"><div class="compliance-types-row panel-row-style panel-row-style-for-807-4"><div class="innerWrap cols-4"><div class="panel-grid-cell" id="pgc-807-4-0"><div class="singleWidget sitBuilderWidget sobImageWidget"><a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Variscite_REACH_compliance.pdf" target="_blank"><img alt="REACH" class="img-responsive" src="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/reach-logo-2x.jpg" title=""/> </a></div></div><div class="panel-grid-cell" id="pgc-807-4-1">
<div class="singleWidget sitBuilderWidget sitBuilderTextWidget" style="">
<div class="title-box"><h2 class="widgettitle bold" style="margin-top:0px;color:#0d0d0d;; font-size: 24px; display: inline-block;"><a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Variscite_REACH_compliance.pdf" target="_blank">REACH</a></h2></div>
<div class="text-box" style="width: 85%; display: inline-block;"><p>Variscite produces “Articles” as defined in REACH regulation and not chemicals which need to be registered according to the REACH regulation. There is no intended release of substances subjected to REACH and therefore registration does not apply to our products. The European Chemicals Agency (ECHA) Candidate List of the Substances of Very High Concern (SVHC) is monitored regularly by Variscite with its approved suppliers. As far as we know, our products do not currently contain any of these SVHCs in a concentration above 0.1% of the weight of the product. To download Variscite’s REACH statement.</p><p>
<a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Variscite_REACH_compliance.pdf" target="_blank">Learn More</a></p></div>

<div class="singleWidget sitBuilderWidget sitBuilderTextWidget" style="">
<div class="title-box"><h2 class="widgettitle bold" style="margin-top:0px;color:#0d0d0d;; font-size: 24px; display: inline-block;"><a href="https://www.variscite.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Variscite_LTD_Conflict_Minerals.pdf" target="_blank">Conflict Minerals</a></h2></div>
<div class="text-box" style="width: 85%; display: inline-block;"><p>We have received several inquiries from our customers in respect to the Conflict Minerals issue addresses in Section 1502 of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act that was passed by the US Congress in July 2010. Variscite does not purchase 3T&amp;G directly from smelters or mines, so we must rely on the source information provided by our suppliers. We will continue to assess the conflict status of our supply chain and, if identified, will eliminate the use of supplier materials that include 3T&amp;G from conflict sources as soon as commercially practicable. To download Variscite’s Conflict Mineral letter.</p><p>
