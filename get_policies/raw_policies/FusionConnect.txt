This Privacy Policy (" <span class="reference-term">Privacy Policy</span>") explains Fusion's practices regarding the collection and use of Personal Data, defined below, collected through <a href="/">https://www.fusionconnect.com</a> (the " <span class="reference-term">Site</span>") and through Fusion's general product and service offerings (" <span class="reference-term">Fusion Services</span>"). This Privacy Policy applies to Fusion account holders and users of the Fusion Services (" <span class="reference-term">Customers</span>"), and anyone accessing or using the Site (Site visitors and Customers may be referred to herein as " <span class="reference-term">Users</span>", " <span class="reference-term">you</span>" and " <span class="reference-term">yours</span>"). This Privacy Policy is incorporated by reference into Fusion's Customer Service Agreements and Service Orders (each a " <span class="reference-term">Fusion Customer Agreement</span>"). In the event of a conflict between the terms of this Privacy Policy and any Fusion Customer Agreement, the terms of the Fusion Customer Agreement shall govern. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully.</p>
<hr class="my-5"/>
<h2>What Personal Data Do We Collect?</h2>
<p>We collect Personal Data when you communicate with us on our Site and when you use the Fusion Services. Specifically, we collect personally identifiable information that can be used on its own or with other information to identify you (" <span class="reference-term">Personal Data</span>").</p>
<h3>Personal Data may include:</h3>
<ul>
<li>Your Name;</li>
<li>Your home or work address;</li>
<li>Your home, work, or cellular telephone number;</li>
<li>Your personal or work email address;</li>
<li>Your company name and title;</li>
<li>Your credit card or financial account/bank account number and information (" <span class="reference-term">Protected Financial Information</span>" or " <span class="reference-term">PFI</span>"); and</li>
<li>Your Internet usage information including websites visited and service and device information.</li>
</ul>
<p>In some cases you have a choice about the collection and use of your Personal Data, and in other cases we collect Personal Data in accordance with our business and operations services and where we have a legal basis for collecting your Personal Data (" <span class="reference-term">Business Purposes</span>").</p>
<hr class="my-5"/>
<h2>How We Collect Personal Data</h2>
<p>When you access and use our Site and the Fusion Services, we collect Personal Data as outlined below:</p>
<h3>Business Purposes</h3>
<p>When you contact Fusion for information, we may use the Personal Data you supply to provide you with information about the Fusion Services.</p>
<p>When you use the Site or its applications, Personal Data is collected including:</p>
<ul>
<li>browsing and searching activity as you interact with the Site and its applications;</li>
<li>IP address information;</li>
<li>account information;</li>
<li>web addresses of the sites you come from; and</li>
<li>information about your connection, including your device's browser, operating system, platform type and Internet connection speed.</li>
</ul>
<p>We use this Personal Data for a variety of Business Purposes including:</p>
<ul>
<li>protecting our Site from malicious activity and exploits;</li>
<li>displaying service availability results for a particular physical location; and</li>
<li>fulfilling various types of user requests such as service quotes, training registration, special downloads, etc.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Marketing Purposes</h3>
<p>We also use Personal Data as part of our marketing and communications operations (" <span class="reference-term">Marketing Purposes</span>") to help us deliver more relevant marketing messages on the Site and directly to our Customers via email. Your Personal Data is used to tailor the content you see and manage the frequency with which you see an advertisement. You may opt-out of our marketing and communications emails by unsubscribing at <a href="/subscription-center">https://www.fusionconnect.com/subscription-center</a>.</p>
<p>We may collect Personal Data when you open Fusion emails to which you have subscribed, or click on links contained therein. This Personal Data is used to personalize the content of the emails themselves and make future communications more interesting to you.</p>
<p>Personal Data you provide on our Site about your preferred location and other preferences may be used to provide you with more relevant service recommendations and special offers.</p>
<h3>Surveys &amp; Job Applications</h3>
<p>We also collect Personal Data from you when you participate in surveys or provide other feedback to us regarding Fusion Services, or when you apply for a job with Fusion. We use this Personal Data only for the purpose for which it was provided.</p>
<h3>When you establish an online account ("Customer Account")</h3>
<p>We use Customer Account Personal Data to, among other things:</p>
<ul>
<li>establish, monitor, and maintain a Customer's account and billing records;</li>
<li>measure credit and payment risk;</li>
<li>provide account-related services;</li>
<li>help our Customers with service-related issues or questions;</li>
<li>deliver and maintain Fusion Services;</li>
<li>manage and protect Fusion's network, services, and Customers from abusive, fraudulent, or unlawful uses;</li>
<li>help Fusion improve and personalize its solutions;</li>
<li>authenticate access to systems; and</li>
<li>market new products and services to our Customers.</li>
</ul>
<h3>From third parties</h3>
<p>When you purchase Fusion Services, we may obtain Personal Data about you including credit information received from outside credit reporting agencies. We use this Personal Data to help us with Customer authentication and credit-related decisions.</p>
<p>Fusion obtains Personal Data from outside companies, including Personal Data related to your existing service provider, service locations and existing phone equipment type. We use this Personal Data and combine it with other information we have about existing Customers and potential customers to help make marketing offers more relevant to that Customer, and to help us better analyze Customer information for business modeling purposes.</p>
<p>We obtain Personal Data and other marketing lead information, including website referral options, from third parties.</p>
<p>We obtain Personal Data from social media platforms, including Facebook and LinkedIn, if you utilize these platforms to login and access your Customer Account.</p>
<p>We may combine your Personal Data with other information legally in Fusion's possession to contact Customers or market directly to Customers for Marketing Purposes. You may opt out of Fusion marketing communications at any time.</p>
<p>In each instance where we obtain Personal Data from a third-party, Fusion has a contractual relationship with the third-party and the third-party is required to obtain your consent prior to sharing your Personal Data with Fusion.</p>

<h2>Use of Cookies</h2>
<p>Cookies are small text files that can be used by us to make your experience more efficient. Cookies used on the Site are related to managing your identity and personalization; and can help Fusion remember information about your visit. Certain cookies can make your next visit more efficient and useful to you. The law provides that Fusion may store cookies on your device if such cookies are strictly necessary for the operation of Fusion's Site. For all other cookies, Fusion needs your consent. Fusion obtains this consent when you access our Site and where we provide you with an opportunity to opt-out of cookies. You may opt-out of cookies tracking at any time. For more information please select the "Help" option on your browser to assist you in changing your cookies preferences. Please note that by turning cookies off, you may not have access to certain features available on the Site.</p>
<p>Fusion uses cookies as outlined below.</p>
<ul>
<li>Necessary Cookies</li>
<li>Preference Cookies</li>
<li>Statistic Cookies</li>
<li>Marketing Cookies</li>
</ul>

<h2>Special Notice Regarding Minors</h2>
<p>Fusion respects the privacy of children of all ages and in particular recognizes the sensitivity of Personal Data concerning children under the age of 18 (" <span class="reference-term">Minors </span>") and therefore provides this special notice to parents and legal guardians regarding the collection and use of Personal Data collected from Minors. The Fusion Services are not directed toward Minors nor are the Fusion Services intended to solicit information of any kind from Minors. If we discover that a Minor has provided us with Personal Data, we will use commercially reasonable efforts to delete such Minor's Personal Data from our system.</p>
<hr class="my-5"/>
<h2>How Do We Keep Your Personal Data Safe?</h2>
<p>Fusion wants its Customers and Users to access and use the Site and the Fusion Services with confidence. We maintain appropriate technical, organizational, and physical safeguards designed to protect the Personal Data we process in accordance with our legal and regulatory obligations. Our safeguards include firewalls and access control procedures. Our employees are bound by confidentiality agreements that prevent them from disclosing any Personal Data except as explicitly outlined in Fusion's internal policies and procedures. Under Fusion's practices and policies, access to Personal Data is authorized only for those who have a business need for such access. In addition, our employees are trained on the importance of protecting privacy and on the proper access to, use and disclosure of Personal Data.</p>
<p>Although Fusion endeavors to protect the Personal Data that it collects and stores, no system is 100% secure and Fusion cannot guarantee that its safeguards will prevent every unauthorized attempt to access, use or disclose Personal Data.</p>
<p>To report a security issue, please contact visit <a href="/privacy">https://www.fusionconnect.com/privacy</a> or contact <a href="mailto:privacyofficer@fusionconnect.com">Fusion's Privacy Officer</a>. We will work with you to address any problems.</p>

<h2>Rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)</h2>
<p>If you are a California resident, under the California Consumer Privacy Act you have additional rights related to your Personal Data.</p>
<p><span class="subitem">Right to Know.</span> You have the right to know and see what Personal Data Fusion has collected about you over the preceding 12 months, including:</p>
<ul>
<li>The categories of Personal Data Fusion has collected about you;</li>
<li>The categories of sources from which Personal Data is collected;</li>
<li>The Business Purposes for collecting your Personal Data;</li>
<li>The Marketing Purposes for collecting your Personal Data;</li>
<li>The categories of third parties with whom Fusion has shared your Personal Data; and</li>
<li>The specific pieces of Personal Data Fusion has collected about you.</li>
</ul>
<p><span class="subitem">Right to Delete.</span> You have the right to request that Fusion delete the Personal Data we have collected from you.</p>
<p>Deletion of your Personal Data is not an absolute right, however. There are exceptions under which Fusion may retain your Personal Data and where Fusion is not required to delete your Personal Data.</p>
<h3>Business Purposes Exception</h3>
<ul>
<li>Fusion may retain your Personal Data for the following Business Purposes:</li>
<li>In order to process paperwork in connection with the sale or use of Fusion Services;</li>
<li>As part of the Business Purposes in servicing a Customer Account;</li>
<li>To share with an affiliated company that is providing services on behalf of Fusion;</li>
<li>To improve the Fusion Services currently offered or that may be offered in the future; and</li>
<li>To comply with legal or regulatory inquires, or responding to court orders.</li>
</ul>
<p><em><strong><u>You may not opt out of the use of your Personal Data for Business Purposes.</u></strong></em></p>
<h3>Marketing Purposes</h3>
<p>Fusion may collect Personal Data from you for the following Marketing Purposes:</p>
<p>For marketing and sales purposes in offering Fusion Services; and</p>
<p>For third parties with whom Fusion has contracted.</p>
<p><em><strong><u>You may opt-out of the sharing of your Personal Data for Marketing Purposes.</u></strong></em></p>
