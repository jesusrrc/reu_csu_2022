class="title-underline"/><p>FlexGate™ FG264 is a family of LoRaWAN private operator Gateway designed for Industrial Internet of Thing applications. Offering a wide range of options, it is the ideal Gateway to match different use cases on the field including Edge and IA.</p>
<div class="showcase-company-url"><a href="http://www.expemb.com" target="_blank">http://www.expemb.com</a></div><div class="product-purchase-grid"><a href="https://lora-alliance.org/alliance_member/expemb/"><button>View Member Profile</button></a></div></div></div></div><footer class="entry-footer"></footer></article></main></div></div><section class="showcase-product-details"><div class="row"><div class="product-services col-md-7 col-sm-7 col-xs-12"><h2>Product / Service Details</h2><div class="su-spoiler su-spoiler-style-default su-spoiler-icon-chevron product-spoilers su-spoiler-closed" data-anchor-in-url="no" data-scroll-offset="0"><div class="su-spoiler-title" role="button" tabindex="0"><span class="su-spoiler-icon"></span>Description</div><div class="su-spoiler-content su-u-clearfix su-u-trim"><p>The FlexGate™ FGW264 product line is a family of LoRa Gateway designed to bring hardware and software flexibility to meet the IoT technical challenges.</p>
<p>Beyond LoRa support, FlexGate™ FG264 product line offers a flexible Cloud and field connectivity within a unique platform ready to adapt to local configuration and facilitate deployment, maintenance, and installation.</p>
<p>The FlexGate™ FG264 product line integrates a high performance and low power processor. This flexible architecture offers a processing power that can easily be adapted to different use cases including Edge Computing and Fog Computing applications.</p>
<p>The FlexGate™ FG264 product line offers a rich connectivity with 1 Gbit Ethernet and 3G/4G. All links are simultaneously available on the Gateway and fall back can be set according to different scripts. With FlexServer Embedded LoRaWAN Network Server, MQTT connectivity to major Cloud such as Microsoft Azure, Amazon AWS is fully transparent.</p>
<p>Powered by PoE+, the FlexGate™ FG264 product line is designed to be a perfect indoor or outdoor platform able to handle all use cases with a great choice of connectivity.</p>
