<div class="wpb_wrapper">
<p>ANTSOMI.com is Antsomi’s website, a marketing channel to reach out to their leads and clients.</p>
<p><strong>We collect data to be able to:</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>Provide reports about our web traffic. e.g., x unique visitors clicked on a specific banner.</li>
<li>Decide which ad or message we should deliver to the visitors.</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Who has access to this data?</strong></p>
<p>Only the Antsomi team has access to the information collected from the site.</p>
<p>We never provide nor sell the data collected to any 3rd parties.</p>
<p>Internally at Antsomi, only staff at IT Operations have access to the raw logs at the servers.</p>
<p><strong>What we store:</strong></p>
<ol>
<li>Which ad or message was shown, when, and where (geolocation)</li>
<li>How long was the message visible in the browser</li>
<li>From the browser’s USER_AGENT string, we get:<br/>
– Which browser<br/>
– Browser language<br/>
– Operating system</li>
<li>Other data our clients provide for their own use</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>What we don’t store:</strong></p>
<p>1. personal information such as:<br/>
– IP addresses<br/>
– email addresses<br/>
– people’s names<br/>
– phone numbers</p>
<p>2. browser information such as:<br/>
– font cache<br/>
– screen resolution<br/>
– plugins</p>
<p><strong>What do we use cookies for?</strong></p>
<p>Some data is only stored in the visitor’s browser and not at our servers.</p>
