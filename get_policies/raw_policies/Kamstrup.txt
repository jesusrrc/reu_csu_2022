<div class="articleText" style=""><p>At Kamstrup, we respect data privacy and it is our top priority to protect data, prevent misuse and loss of data. We respect the trust all our stakeholders have in our ability to handle their data in a secure and trustworthy way. All data is handled in accordance to the European General Data Protection Regulation (EU GDPR) and other applicable data protection regulations. </p>
<p>We value the trust you show us, when entrusting your personal information to us, and we will only use your information in relation to the presented purposes and the consent given by you. Furthermore, Kamstrup will only keep your data for the period it is legitimately reasonable to do so. Thereafter, the data collected by us or given to us by you will be deleted. This Privacy Policy will provide a thorough overview of what type of information is collected, how data is used and if shared with any third party relation.</p>
<h4>Categories</h4>
<p>Kamstrup processes personal data in the following categories:</p>
<ol>
<li>when you visit our websites e.g. when surfing the websites, filling in a form etc.,</li>
<li>when we process data for marketing purposes, including subscription to a newsletter and responding to customer surveys via e-mail or on our website,</li>
<li>when you participate in an event, trade fair etc., and are in touch with Kamstrup before, during and after the event e.g. as part of queries or sales,</li>
<li>When you send inquiries to Kamstrup in general e.g. contacting Kamstrup by e-mail, social media or the like. </li>
</ol>
<h4>Data types</h4>
<p>Depending on which category you belong to (see the above); different data will be collected about you. The data types for each category are listed below: </p>
<p>1.<span> </span><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Website visitor</span></p>
<ul>
<li>Queries sent via the website and response received by e-mail</li>
<li>In relation to “Search the job” under the career tab separate information is given </li>
<li>Login information when creating an account on My Kamstrup</li>
</ul>
<p>2.<span> </span><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Marketing</span></p>
<ul>
<li>Name, telephone number, e-mail address, postal address, job title and area of interest</li>
<li>Company or organisation of employment </li>
<li>Information about consent granted for marketing purposes</li>
<li>Tracking the processing of online marketing received e.g. whether the message was opened and which links were clicked on etc. </li>
<li>Metering information including costs (mechanical and ultrasonic meters)</li>
</ul>
<p>3.<span> </span><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Events</span></p>
<ul>
<li>Name, job title, e-mail address and telephone number</li>
<li>Area of interest</li>
<li>Company or organisation of employment </li>
<li>Participation in trade fairs and events</li>
</ul>
<p>4.<span> </span><span style="text-decoration: underline;">General inquiries </span></p>
<ul>
<li>Name, telephone number, e-mail address, postal address, job title and area of interest</li>
<li>Data protocols and similar detailed product information</li>
</ul>
<h4>Purpose for collecting data</h4>
<p>Depending on the category (see above), we collect information with the following purposes:</p>
<ul>
<li>To improve our website enabling us to serve you and present our services in the best possible way</li>
<li>To follow up on queries and correspondence by e-mail, telephone and online forms</li>
<li>To improve our level of service and optimise our marketing initiatives</li>
<li>To send direct marketing via e-mail, etc. as described in the consent form</li>
<li>To ensure compliance with applicable legislation e.g. EU GDPR, ePrivacy Directive and the Danish Marketing Practice Act.</li>
<li>To be able to provide documentation of compliance with legislation</li>
<li>To ensure initiation and maintenance of technical and organisational security measures</li>
<li>To be able to process queries and complaints from people registered and others </li>
<li>To ensure that we can investigate any suspicion or knowledge of security breaches and report to the people registered and the authorities</li>
<li>To be able to deal with inspections and queries from supervisory authorities</li>
<li>To be able to manage disputes with people registered and third parties </li>
<li>To be able to perform statistical analysis</li>
</ul>
<p>The profiling enables us to deliver punctual and relevant communication based on submitted information, visitor history, visitor behaviour on the website and tracking data form e-mails etc. The information is used for segmentation of the target group. </p>
<h4>Source</h4>
<p>Personal data is collected directly from you, including any queries made by you or the company you represent by telephone, e-mail, at trade fairs etc. Data is also received via the website e.g. through e-mail inserts and other tracking mechanisms.</p>
<h4>Legal basis for collecting and processing personal data</h4>
<p>The legal basis for the processing of data for the above purposes is as follows:</p>
<ul>
<li>Consent for direct marketing e-mail, cf. the Danish Marketing Practice Act</li>
<li>Website and the use of other tracking mechanisms in e-mails, cf. ePrivacy Directive</li>
<li>The provision on the balancing of interests in Article 6(1)(f), as it is in Kamstrup’s legitimate interests to provide you and the company you represent a focused service and to be able to protect IT systems and data about the company</li>
<li>Our legal obligations as a data service provider e.g. in terms of IT security</li>
<li>Necessary steps taken at your request prior to entering into a potential contract</li>
<li>Necessary steps taken in order to provide in queries with any information requested by you</li>
</ul>
