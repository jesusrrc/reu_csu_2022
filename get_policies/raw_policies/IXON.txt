<h1>Privacy and Cookie Statement</h1>
<p><em>Version: 2.1</em><br/><em>Date: 3 January 2022</em></p>
<p><br/>IXON takes the protection of your privacy very seriously and wants to be transparent regarding how your personal data is collected, processed and shared by us. The personal data that is collected through our services is processed in accordance with the applicable privacy regulations.</p>
<p>This Privacy and Cookie Statement describes your rights and obligations with regard to two use cases:</p>
<ul>
<li>the processing of personal data and the use of cookies when visiting our website(s): <a href="/">www.ixon.cloud</a>, go.ixon.cloud and lp.ixon.cloud, and</li>
<li>the processing of personal data when we process quotes and orders.</li>
</ul>
<p> </p>
<h3>What data do we collect and how will we use it?</h3>
<p>We only collect, store and use personal data that is directly provided by you (for example via our contact form), or personal data of which it is clear that it is being provided to us in order to process it. We will not use your personal data for any other goals, unless you have given us permission to do so or if we are legally obliged or allowed to do so.</p>
<p>Depending on how you use our website or the request you made to us, different types of data may be processed by us. For example:</p>
<ul>
<li>name and address information;</li>
<li>email address and/or telephone number;</li>
<li>payment information;</li>
<li>IP address.</li>
</ul>
<p>We process your personal data for the following purposes:</p>
<ul>
<li>to grant access to the website ixon.cloud;</li>
