<section class="article_module" id="external_content_module_1">
<h2>Principles of data processing for MyLiebherr and the Liebherr SmartDevice app (information requirements)</h2>
<p><strong>A. General</strong></p>
<p><strong>I. What does this privacy policy regulate?</strong></p>
<p>We attach great importance to the protection and security of your personal data. It is therefore important for us to inform you about what personal data we use for which purposes and what rights you have in relation to your personal data.</p>
<p><strong>MyLiebherr Portal</strong></p>
<p>The “MyLiebherr Portal” includes your activities on the MyLiebherr website, in particular, but not limited to, the use of the MyLiebherr Account as well as all other connected modules. More information on the dependency between the MyLiebherr Portal and the MyLiebherr Account can be found in the next paragraph “The MyLiebherr Account”.</p>
<p><strong>MyLiebherr Account</strong></p>
<p>The "MyLiebherr Account" is your central user account at Liebherr-Hausgeräte GmbH. You have to register in order to create a MyLiebherr Account. Your can register on the “MyLiebherr Portal" (<a href="https://home.myliebherr.com/" target="_blank">https://home.myliebherr.com</a>). When you register via the MyLiebherr Portal, you will receive an e-mail with a confirmation link at the end of the registration process. You must click on the confirmation link in order to complete the registration process. Alternatively, you can also sign up for the MyLiebherr Account using your Google, Facebook or Microsoft accounts (hereinafter referred to as "Social Login"). Please note that we have no influence on the processing of your personal data by Google, Facebook and Microsoft. With your MyLiebherr Account you can use the Single-sign-on service of Liebherr-Hausgeräte GmbH (hereinafter referred to as "SSO"). SSO allows you to use other access-restricted digital services (hereinafter referred to as "Modules") connected to the SSO using identical access data. SSO allows you to navigate between modules without the need for a separate registration and login process. SSO automatically transfers the data stored in your MyLiebherr Account which are required for the use of the relevant modules to the respective module providers in order to offer you the best possible comfort. $('.fixed_servicebar').removeClass('open');
});

var openServicebar = function(target) {
$('.fixed_servicebar').addClass('open').find(target).slideDown(300);
};
var switchServicebar = function(prev, next) {
$(prev).fadeOut(300, function() {
$(next).fadeIn(300);
});
};
$('.fixed_servicebar').on('click', '.closeServicebar', closeServicebar);
$('.fixed_servicebar').on('click', 'li[data-servicebar-content]', function() {

switchServicebar($('.fixed_servicebar li.active').not(this).removeClass('active').attr('data-servicebar-content'), $(this).attr('data-servicebar-content'));
$('.fixed_servicebar li.active').not(this).removeClass('active');
$(this).addClass('active');
} else if ($(this).hasClass('active')) {
closeServicebar($(this).attr('data-servicebar-content'));
$(this).removeClass('active');
} else {
openServicebar($(this).attr('data-servicebar-content'));
$(this).addClass('active')
}
});
});
