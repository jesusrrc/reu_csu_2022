<h1>LINAK<sup>®</sup> Privacy Policy</h1>
<div class="clearfix"> </div>
<p>Thank you for reading our privacy policy. We value your privacy, and with this privacy policy we want to inform you of the personal data we collect, how and why we collect personal data, how it may be used by us, whom we share it with, and your rights as a data subject.</p>
<p>For ease of reference, we have divided this privacy policy into subsections based on the most common interaction you may have with LINAK as a data processor/controller. You may fall within more than one subsection. In such cases, the information provided in each subsection will apply to you.</p>
<p>This privacy policy does not cover personal data concerning employees of LINAK, job applicants using our recruitment portal, or other relations which are subject to separate privacy policies (as further disclosed as applicable).</p>
<p>We kindly ask you not to send us any sensitive personal data, such as information related to racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religion or other beliefs, health, biometric or genetic characteristics, criminal background, or trade union membership. We will not collect such data from you unless you give us your consent, or we are required to collect such data due to a legal requirement.</p>
<p>Our business and related services and related web pages are not intended for or designed to attract children.</p>
<p><strong>Who are we?</strong><br/>
The LINAK Group is an international company engaged in designing and manufacturing electric linear actuator systems. LINAK A/S is based in Guderup, Denmark, and is represented in over 30 countries all over the world through either subsidiaries or distributors. See a list of the LINAK group of companies here: <a href="/organization/subsidiaries-and-distributors/" title="LINAK subsidiaries and distributors">LINAK subsidiaries and distributors worldwide.</a></p>
<p>The website is controlled by LINAK A/S. LINAK A/S is in most cases the data controller responsible for your personal data. If your data is being collected or processed in your role as a customer or recipient of marketing materials, your local LINAK subsidiary will be a joint data controller together with LINAK A/S.</p>
<p>Our contact information is provided at the end of this privacy policy.</p>
<div class="clearfix"> </div>

When you visit our website(s), we automatically collect personal data from you.
The personal data collected includes, among other things:</p>

<div class="read-more">
<ul>
<li>Your IP address, operating system, and platform, type of browser, browsing activity, and other data on how you interact with our website, such as clicks you have made on LINAK ads before visiting our webpage and/or contact forms.</li>
<li>Details entered into our contact forms, such as email, name, phone number, geographical region, and other information on your inquiry, provided by you.</li>
<li>Survey and customer satisfaction, such as responses to surveys.</li>
</ul>
<p>We collect personal data on website visitors through the use of cookies, the scope of such collection will depend on your acceptance of individual types of cookies (marketing, statistic, and preference cookies). For further information please visit our <a href="/cookie-declaration/">Cookie Declaration</a>.
</p>
<p>We use interest-based advertising of our products. If you accept marketing cookies from LINAK when you visit our website, you may see LINAK advertising on various other platforms.
We process the personal data of website visitors for the following purposes:</p>
<ul>
<li>To operate, improve, and optimize the performance and visitor experience of our website and services.</li>
<li>To collect <span style="text-decoration: underline;">anonymous</span> feedback and usage data to improve our understanding of website visitors, and to provide better and customized website services.</li>
<li>To enhance security, to block possible spam, for bug fixing, and technical maintenance.</li>
<li>To build statistics.</li>
<li>To provide personalized advertising (if marketing cookies are accepted). We and our advertising partners use social media tracking tools such as Facebook Pixel and LinkedIn Pixel.</li>
<li>For compliance with legal obligations, we may be subject to.</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>Legal bases for the processing of personal data</strong><br/>
LINAK will only process personal data in order to pursue a legitimate purpose and provided that there is a legal basis for such processing. Such legal bases include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Your consent for the processing.</li>
<li>The processing is necessary for the performance of a contract.</li>
<li>The processing is necessary for compliance with legal obligation(s) LINAK is subject to.</li>
<li>The processing is based on legitimate interests, and such processing is limited and not harmful to you.</li>
</ul>
<div class="clearfix"> </div>
<p><strong>Whom we share personal data with<br/>
</strong>LINAK may share your personal data, when necessary, for the purposes described above. We may share personal data as follows:</p>
<ul>
<li>Within the LINAK Group.</li>
<li>To business partners of LINAK, such as sales and marketing partners, partners for digital offerings and IoT, logistic partners, etc.</li>
<li>IT service providers and data processors acting on behalf of LINAK, for purposes such as hosting services, IT support, cloud services, etc.</li>
<li>Consultants and advisers of LINAK.</li>
<li>Other authorized third parties may need to access or store personal data, as required or permitted by applicable law.</li>
</ul>
<p>LINAK will never sell your personal data, nor intentionally share it with unauthorized third parties.</p>
<div class="clearfix"> </div>
<p><strong>Social media platforms<br/>
</strong>To optimize our activities on social media platforms, we use tools such as lead ads and Facebook Custom Audiences, which means that we pay for the platforms to assist us in finding relevant persons to target our advertising and other marketing content. Criteria used for targeting is either set by LINAK or the social media platform. Such sponsored and targeted ads are marked “Sponsored”. If you interact with our posts, we will note this.</p>
<div class="clearfix"> </div>
<p><strong>Transfer of personal data</strong><br/>
In certain cases, your personal data may be transferred across borders. We take great care to ensure compliance with applicable data protection laws and other local requirements when transferring data across borders.</p>
<div class="clearfix"> </div>
<p><strong>Profiling<br/>
</strong>Where we are legally authorized, we may use profiling to provide you with personalized content and interactions. This can include but is not limited to personalized ads and other marketing materials which we believe will be of interest to you.</p>
<p>To the extent possible, we will anonymize and aggregate data when performing our analyses and compiling statistics.</p>
<div class="clearfix"> </div>
<p><strong>Retention of personal data</strong><br/>
At LINAK we only retain personal data for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, and never longer than prescribed by applicable law. In some cases, LINAK may have to store data longer than necessary for the purpose for which it was collected to comply with legal requirements on retention. In some cases, we will keep data in anonymized form for statistical purposes rather than deleting it.</p>
<div class="clearfix"> </div>
<p><strong>How we protect your data:</strong><br/>
We have implemented appropriate technical and operational security precautions to protect your data from being accidentally or intentionally manipulated, lost, destroyed, or accessed by unauthorized persons. Our security measures are reviewed regularly, for example, to address new technology.</p>
<p>We have implemented written procedures and processes on the protection of personal data and train our employees on data privacy regularly.</p>
<div class="clearfix"> </div>
<p><strong>Your rights</strong><br/>
You have certain rights related to the personal data we process. These include:</p>
<ul>
<li>You may have the right to obtain confirmation as to whether personal data concerning you is processed, and, where that is the case, to request access to the personal data. This right is, however, subject to statutory limitation, i.e., it is not absolute.</li>
<li>You may have the right to object to the collection and further processing of your personal data where the processing is not based on a legal requirement or otherwise necessary.</li>
<li>Upon request, LINAK will delete or correct any information, which is inaccurate or incorrect.</li>
<li>If you have provided your consent for any processing of personal data by LINAK, you may withdraw this consent at any time with future effect.
