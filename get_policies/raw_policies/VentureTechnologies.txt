<h3>Venture Expeditions, Inc. (DBA: Venture) Statement of Privacy</h3>
<p>(1) Venture will not sell, trade or share a donor’s personal information with anyone else, nor send donor mailings on behalf of other organizations.<br/> (2) Venture will only share personal information once a donor has given specific permission to do so.</p><p>
<h4>COLLECTION OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION</h4>
Venture is committed to protecting your privacy. This Statement of Privacy applies to Venture’s web site and governs data collection and usage at all Venture sites and services; it does not apply to other online or offline sites. Venture is a general audience web site, intended for users of all ages. Personal information of users is collected, used and disclosed as described in this Statement of Privacy. Venture encourages you to review the privacy statements of web sites you choose to link to from Venture so that you can understand how those web sites collect, use and share your information. We are not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on web sites outside of the Venture web site.</p><p>

Venture collects personal information on a voluntary basis, such as your email address, name, home or work address or telephone number when donations are submitted. For each visitor to our web page, our web server automatically recognizes only the consumer’s domain name, but not the email address (where possible). For online event registration, we set cookies to update attendance payment and verify successful online registration. At no time during any Venture transaction is credit card information stored in a cookie.</p><p>
<h4>USE OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION</h4>
Personal information collected by Venture is used solely for the purpose processing your donation, tracking donor information, sending receipts for donation and maintaining contact with donors. It is possible that Venture may also use personal information to inform you of other events or fundraising efforts of the organization. Venture may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of the organization.</p><p>

Venture does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties.</p><p>

We occasionally hire other companies to provide limited services on our behalf, such as handling the processing and delivery of mailings, providing customer support, processing transactions, or performing statistical analysis of our services. We will only provide those companies the personal information they need to deliver the service. They are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information and are prohibited from using that information for any other purpose.</p><p>

Venture does not collect sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations without consent.</p><p>

Venture may access and/or disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Venture or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Venture, including its web site; or (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Venture or the public. Personal information collected on this site may be stored and processed in the United States where Venture or its affiliates, subsidiaries or agents maintain facilities.</p>
<h4>CONTROL OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION</h4>
<p>Venture offers its customers choices for the collection, use and sharing of personal information. You may go notify us of your preferences by emailing info@venture.org. Please be sure to provide complete account information so we can identify you in our records. You may also stop the delivery of future promotional email from Venture by responding directly to any email you receive with a request to remove you from the mailing list.</p>
<h4>SECURITY OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION</h4>
<p>Venture is committed to protecting the security of your personal information. We use a variety of security technologies and procedures to help protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. For example, we store the personal information you provide on computer systems with limited access that are located in controlled facilities. When we transmit highly confidential information (such as a credit card number) over the Internet, we protect it through 128bit SSL encryption.</p>
<h4>CHANGES TO THIS STATEMENT</h4>
<p>Venture will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. If there are material changes to this Statement or in how Venture will use your personal information, Venture will prominently post such changes prior to implementing the change. Venture encourages you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of how Venture is protecting your information.</p>
<h4>Links to Other Sites</h4>
<p>Our Privacy Policy applies solely to this Site. This Site contains links to other sites. The links from this Site do not imply that we endorse or have reviewed these sites. Please be aware that Venture is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage you to be aware when you leave this Site and to read the privacy statements of each site that collects information.</p>
<p>If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the following address, email or phone number.</p><p>

Address: 511 East Travelers Trail, Burnsville, MN 55337<br/>
Phone: (952) 358-6444 <br/>
Email: info@venture.org<br/></p></div>

function VMForm(options) {
	if (!options || !options.kindfulUrl || !options.campaigns || options.campaigns.length == 0) {
		alert("Invalid configuration for donation form");
		return;
	}
	function createElement(tag, className) {
		const el = document.createElement(tag);
		el.className = className;
		return el;
	}

	function createRadio(opt, label) {
		return `<input id="amountSelect-${opt}" name="amountSelect" type="radio" value="${opt}" /><label for="amountSelect-${opt}">${label}</label>`;
	}

	function setupForm() {
		const form = document.createElement("form")
		form.id = "venture-custom-form";
		form.className = "venture-form";

		const label = createElement("div", "form-label");
		label.innerHTML = "I would like to give:";
		const labelRow = createElement("div", "form-row");
		labelRow.append(label);
		form.append(labelRow);

		if (options.amounts && options.amounts.length > 0) {
			const amounts = createElement("div", "form-row");
			for (var i = 0; i < options.amounts.length; i++) {
				const opt = options.amounts[i];
				const radio = createElement("div", "form-radio");
				radio.innerHTML = createRadio(opt, `$${opt}`);
				amounts.append(radio);
			}
			if (options.amountOther) {
				const radio = createElement("div", "form-radio");
				radio.innerHTML = createRadio("other", "Other");
				amounts.append(radio);
			}
			form.append(amounts);
		}

		const other = createElement("div", "form-row");
		other.style = (options.amounts && options.amounts.length > 0) ? "display: none;" : "";
		other.innerHTML = '<span class="form-prefix">$</span><input class="form-input" id="venture-form-amount" name="amount" type="number" placeholder="Gift Amount"/>';
		form.append(other);

		if (options.recurring) {
			const recurring = createElement("div", "form-row form-full-width");
			recurring.innerHTML = '<div class="form-radio"><input id="recurring-false" name="recurring" type="radio" value="false" checked="checked" /><label for="recurring-false">One Time</label></div>' +
				'<div class="form-radio"><input id="recurring-true" name="recurring" type="radio" value="true" /><label for="recurring-true">Monthly</label></div>';
			if (options.showPledge) {
				recurring.innerHTML += `<div class="form-radio"><input id="pledge" type="button" onclick="onClickPledge()"><label for="pledge">Pledge <i class="x-icon" aria-hidden="true" data-x-icon-o=""></i></label></div>`;
			}

			form.append(recurring);
		}

		if (options.campaigns == null || options.campaigns.length == 0) {
			const error = createElement("div", "form-row");
			error.innerHTML = '<div class="form-label" style="color: red;">Missing campaigns</div>';
			form.append(error);
		} else if (options.campaigns.length > 1) {
			const campaignsLabel = createElement("div", "form-row");
			campaignsLabel.innerHTML = '<div class="form-label" style="padding-top: 1.5em;">I would like my giving to go toward:</div>';
			form.append(campaignsLabel);

			const campaignsRow = createElement("div", "form-row");
			const campaigns = createElement("select");
			campaigns.setAttribute("name", "campaign_id");
			for (var i = 0; i < options.campaigns.length; i++) {
				const opt = options.campaigns[i];
				campaigns.innerHTML += `<option value='${opt.key}'>${opt.value}</option>`;
			}
			campaignsRow.append(campaigns);
			form.append(campaignsRow);
		}

		const button = createElement("div", "form-row");
		button.innerHTML = '<button type="submit">Donate</button>';
		form.append(button);

		if (options.showCrypto) {
			const strike = createElement("div", "form-row");
			strike.innerHTML = '<div class="strike">OR</div>';
			form.append(strike);
			const crypto = createElement("div", "form-row");
			crypto.innerHTML = '<button class="btn-crypto" onclick="onClickCrypto()" type="button">Or Donate with cryptocurrency <i class="x-icon" aria-hidden="true" data-x-icon-o=""></i></label></button>';
			form.append(crypto);
		}

		return form;
	}

	const form = setupForm();
	document.getElementById("venture-form").append(form);

	function onSelectChange(el, cb) {
		if (el) {
			el.forEach(radio => radio.addEventListener("change", e => cb(e.target.value)));
		}
	}

	const amountEl = document.getElementById("venture-form-amount");
	const amountSelect = document.querySelectorAll("input[name='amountSelect']");
	onSelectChange(amountSelect, (val) => {
		if (val == "other") {
			amountEl.parentElement.style.display = "block";
			amountEl.value = "";
		} else {
			amountEl.parentElement.style.display = "none";
			amountEl.value = val;
		}
	});

	window.onClickPledge = () => {
		window.open(`${options.kindfulUrl}/pledges/new/?campaign_id=${options.campaigns[0].key}`);
	}

	window.onClickCrypto = () => {
		window.open('https://platform.engiven.com/give/594/widget/485');
	}

	form && form.addEventListener("submit", (e) => {
		e.preventDefault();

		const targetElements = e.target.elements;
		const defaultCampaignId = options.campaigns && options.campaigns.length == 1 ? options.campaigns[0].key : null;
		const amount = targetElements["amount"] && targetElements["amount"].value;
		if (amount > 0) {
			const campaign_id = defaultCampaignId || targetElements["campaign_id"].value;
			const recurring = targetElements["recurring"] && targetElements["recurring"].value;

			var params = new URLSearchParams({
				amount: amount
			});
			recurring === "true" && params.set("recurring", true);

			const url =
				options.kindfulUrl + "campaigns/" +
				campaign_id +
				"?" +
				params.toString();
			window.location = url;
		} else {
			alert("Gift Amount needs to be higher than 0");
		}
	});
