<h1 id="privacy-policy">Privacy Policy</h1>

<p>Welcome to Shipwell’s Privacy Policy. Shipwell provides a platform for shippers, 3PLs, and carriers to connect and coordinate on the shipment of goods.</p>
<p>The Privacy Policy explains how Shipwell may collect, use, store, disclose, process and secure your personal information through your access to or use of the “<strong>Service</strong>“, defined as the Shipwell web platform, the mobile apps, and other offerings. The terms “<strong>Shipwell</strong>“, “<strong>we</strong>“, and “<strong>us</strong>” include Shipwell, Inc. and our affiliates and subsidiaries. This Privacy Policy is incorporated by reference into the <a href="https://shipwell.com/terms-conditions/">Shipwell Terms and Conditions</a>. Any capitalized terms used and not defined in this Privacy Policy shall have the meaning given to them in the Terms of Use. <strong>By using these Services, you signify your acceptance of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, please do not use the Services. Shipwell may change this Privacy Policy from time to time by posting changes at this URL and may send notification emails. Your continued use of the Services following the posting of such changes will be deemed your acceptance of those changes.</strong></p>
<p>“Personal Information” can be used to readily identify, contact, or locate you.</p>
<p>“<strong>Personal Information</strong>” means information that alone or when in combination with other information may be used to readily identify, contact, or locate you, such as: name, address, email address, or phone number. Except where required by law, we do not consider Personal Information to include information an individual makes public through the Service or information about a device.</p>
<ol><li><a href="#chapter1">Information We Collect</a> <p>We collect all information that you provide including information you provide via the Services, your devices (e.g., computers, smartphones, tablets, and electronic logging devices), name, company address, email address, telephone number, IP address, and geo-location, as well as information we receive from third parties and through the use of cookies, server logs, and other such technologies. If you are a carrier, you may also be asked to provide DOT#, MC#, CDL#, and insurance-related information. <a href="#chapter1">More about Information We Collect </a></p> </li><li><a href="#chapter2">Cookies and Third-Party Tracking</a><br/>When you visit our Service or open our emails, we and our third-party partners, such as advertising networks, social media widgets, and analytics providers, may collect certain information by automated means, such as cookies, web beacons and web server logs. <a href="#chapter2">More about Cookies and Third-Party Tracking</a><br/></li><li><a href="#chapter3">How We Use Information We Collect</a> <p>We process your personal information only with your consent or as needed to provide you with Services or to communicate with you. The Services are not for use by children under the age of 13 years old. You may have additional rights under applicable law. <p><a name="chapter1"></a>INFORMATION WE COLLECT</p>
<p>We collect information when:</p>
<ul><li>You register to use the Service;</li><li>You use the Service;</li><li>We receive it from third parties; and</li><li>You communicate with us</li></ul>
<p>We also collect information, such as de-identified usage statistics, by using cookies, server logs, and other similar technology as you use the Service.</p>
<p><strong>Registration and Account Information.</strong> You must register to use the Service. To register, you may need to provide personal information <strong>that can identify or relates to you</strong>, such as your name, company address, email address, and telephone number. If you are a carrier, you may also be asked to provide DOT#, MC#, CDL#, and insurance-related information. You may also provide other optional information. <strong>You have the choice of what information to share and the services you want to engage in. You can choose not to provide information to us, but in general, some information about you is required in order for you to access certain functionality of the Services, such as those mentioned above or for tracking your preferences or initiating other such actions.</strong></p>
<p><strong>Using the Service.</strong> We collect the information you provide through the Service. For example, when you interact with other users by posting reviews or other comments and by using the chat functionality to contact Shipwell, the Service will collect the information you provide in such submissions, including any personal information.</p>
<p><strong>Making Payments.</strong> When you make payments through the Service, you may need to provide financial account information, such as your credit card number or bank routing information, to our third-party service providers. We do not collect or store such information, though we may receive information about completed transactions that does not include credit card or bank account numbers.</p>
<p><strong>Communicate with Us.</strong> We may collect personal information through your communications with our customer support team or through other communications with us, including through social media.</p>
<p><strong>From Other Sources.</strong> We may receive information, including personal information, about carriers from third parties. For example, we may use third-party services to obtain information about an individual carrier’s shipping history and licensing and insurance status. We may combine this information with other personal information we received through the Service. If we do so, this Privacy Policy governs any of this combined information that is personal information.</p>
<p><strong>Location Information from Mobile Devices.</strong> We may collect and store geolocation information from the devices using our Apps. For example, we may collect and store geolocation information when carriers are searching for shipping jobs or transporting shipments initiated through the Service, so we can suggest shipping jobs to carriers, provide shipment status updates to shippers, and otherwise provide and improve the provision of the Service. We may also receive geo-location information any time the Apps are running and store it anonymously to help us improve the Service and perform data analysis.</p>
<p>Any user may at any time prohibit the Service from collecting location information by turning this feature off on the mobile device, but the Service likely will not permit the user to perform any shipment jobs if the Service does not have access to geolocation information. When your mobile device settings permit our collection of geolocation information, you expressly consent to our use of location-based services, and you expressly waive and release us from any and all liability, claims, causes of action or damages arising from your use of the Service, or in any way relating to the use of the geo-location and other location-based services.</p>
<p><strong>Information and Content from Your Device.</strong> The Service automatically collects information about your devices, such as a device ID, IP address and browser type, so that we can provide and customize functionality, such as push notifications. With your permission, the Service may access and collect information from your device’s contact list and photo storage.</p>

We may use this information, for example, to determine how many users have visited certain pages or opened messages or newsletters, or to prevent fraud. We may link this data to your profile. Our partners also may collect information about your online activities over time, on other devices, and on other websites or apps, if those websites and apps also use the same partners.
