                Privacy Policy for the App „magicplan“

In this respect, the companies have defined in an agreement which of them fulfils which data protection obligation. The essential content of this agreement is available to you from Sensopia or enapt on request.
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                If you have any question, please do not hesitate to contact Sensopia’s data protection officer:

<h2 class="mb-4 text-lg font-bold text-gray-1200">
            2. What qualifies as personal data?
        </h2>
<div id="what-data-do-we-use-for-what-purposes"></div>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
            Personal data means information relating to an identifiable natural person, such as your name, e-mail address, telephone number, postal address or the IP address of your computer.
        </p>
<h2 class="mb-4 text-lg font-bold text-gray-1200">
            3. What data do we use for what purposes?
        </h2>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
            In the following we explain which personal data we process. As far as such data is processed or stored, this occurs on your smartphone and /or in a IT environment.
        </p>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
            Unless it is explicitly stated otherwise in the following sections the legal basis for this processing of data is Art. 6 (1) b) GDPR (performance of a contract).
        </p>
<p class="mb-2 font-bold text-gray-1200">
            3.1 Technical communication data
        </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
            We collect and use personal data which your smartphone automatically transmits to us and which is used for communication with your smartphone. These are in particular
        </p>
<ul class="list-disc ml-5">
<li class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                the name of the device
            </li>
<li class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                the operating system and app version
            </li>
<li class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                the mobile phone provider
            </li>
<li class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                different identification numbers in order to be able to uniquely assign the device to you
            </li>
<li class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                the time of occurrence of app disturbances for troubleshooting, and
            </li>
<li class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
                the IP address assigned to you.
            </li>
</ul>
<p class="mb-2 font-bold text-gray-1200">
            3.2 Tracking
        </p>
<div id="registration"></div>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
            In order to display personalized content, we use service providers to track the usage of the app. Please find below in this document all service providers we use for personalization purposes.
        </p>
<h2 class="mb-4 text-lg font-bold text-gray-1200">
                4. Registration
            </h2>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                In order to be able to use the functions of this app, you must set up a personal user account (hereinafter “account”). You can store your personal information (e.g. floor plans) in the account.
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                In order to set up a personal account, users must enter their email address and a freely selectable password, which at the same time serve as an access code for the account. With the same access data, the user can also log on the magicplan cloud, which is part of the app, at
                <a class="text-magic underline hover:text-magic-blue-700" href="https://cloud.magic-plan.com/login" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
                    https://cloud.magic-plan.com/login.
                </a>
</p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                After successful registration, the user automatically receives confirmation by e-mail. In this way, the user concludes the contract for the provision of the account with us. In addition, users can store their personal contact details in their account and update these details at any time.
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                Unless you “log off” by clicking, so-called persistent cookies are stored on your terminal device, which serve to ensure that the user does not have to log on again during subsequent visits to the app.
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                The legal basis for the associated processing of your data and for the display of corresponding information in the App is Art. 6 (1) b) GDPR (performance of contract) or Art. 6 (1) f) GDPR (balancing of interests, based on our legitimate interest in providing users with a simple and customer-friendly user experience).
            </p>
<div id="processing-of-orders"></div>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
                The user can terminate his / her contract for the provision of the account at any time and without giving any reasons. The easiest way to do this is by clicking in your user account in the app or by sending an informal email to
                <a class="text-magic underline hover:text-magic-blue-700" href="mailto:support@magicplan.app">
                    support@magicplan.app.
                </a>
</p>
<h2 class="mb-4 text-lg font-bold text-gray-1200">
                5. Processing of orders
            </h2>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                We also offer you the opportunity to purchase products in our app (e.g. floor plans, standard or business subscriptions). Sensopia does not process any personal data in connection with the purchase of these products. The data you enter for electronic payment processing will only be collected by the relevant App store.
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                For more information on the processing of data in the App Stores, please refer to their privacy statements:
            </p>
<ul class="list-disc ml-5">
<li class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                    Apple App Store:<br/>
<a class="text-magic underline hover:text-magic-blue-700" href="https://www.apple.com/de/privacy/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
                        https://www.apple.com/de/privacy/
                    </a>
</li>
<li class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                    Google Play Store:<br/>
<a class="text-magic underline hover:text-magic-blue-700" href="https://policies.google.com/privacy/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
                        https://policies.google.com/privacy/
                    </a>
</li>
</ul>
<div id="app-permissions"></div>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
                The legal basis for the associated processing of your data is the fulfillment of the contract with your App Store, Art. 6 (1) b) GDPR (performance of contract).
            </p>
<h2 class="mb-4 text-lg font-bold text-gray-1200">
                6. App permissions
            </h2>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                In order to provide you with the functionalities of the app, the app must be able to access various functions and data on your mobile device.
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                For this purpose, it is technically absolutely necessary that you grant the app the following access permissions for the purposes described in each case. Otherwise, the app is not fully usable for technical reasons. The reason for this is that the permission settings in smartphone operating systems are usually programmed in such a way that some authorizations can only be granted or requested together and not in the form of individual partial authorizations. Therefore, Sensopia is dependent in such cases on requesting the entire authorization from customers, even if only part of it is required and used specifically.
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                In the following, we will inform you about the permissions required by this app, whereby we will only use these to the extent required in each case, even if an authorization technically includes more.
            </p>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
                The legal basis for the associated processing of your data is Art. 6 (1) b) GDPR (performance of contract) respectively Art. 6 (1) a) GDPR (your consent).
            </p>
<p class="mb-2 font-bold text-gray-1200">
                6.1 Your location
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                Your consent to the collection of your current location is required for location-based services. If you give your consent, your device may use your GPS data, WLAN identifiers and/or Bluetooth to determine your location, depending on what you have activated.
            </p>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
                In any case, the app only collects your location only if you have previously agreed to this in the corresponding device settings. For Android versions, consent is given after confirmation when downloading the app or in the device settings. For Windows Mobile and iPhone versions, consent is given in a dialog box when the app is first opened as well as in the device settings.
            </p>
<p class="font-base text-gray-700">
<strong>Right of withdrawal:</strong>
</p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                You are entitled to revoke your consent at any time for the future. Such revocation may be effected in the settings of the app under “Preferences -&gt; Send data to improve magicplan”.
            </p>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
                The legal basis for location processing is Art. 6 (1) a) GDPR (your consent).
            </p>
<p class="mb-2 font-bold text-gray-1200">
                6.2 Card functionality
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                Our app allows you to display your location on a map, provided you have consented to the collection of your location.
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                The app on the Android operating system uses the Google Maps service of Google Inc. (1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA), which receives your IP address to display the map. The legal basis for this is Art. 6 (1) f) GDPR (balancing of interests, based on our interest in offering you the most attractive app possible).
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                The relevant privacy policy of this third-party provider can be found at
                <a class="text-magic underline hover:text-magic-blue-700" href="https://www.google.com/intl/de_en/policies/privacy/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
                    https://www.google.com/intl/de_en/policies/privacy/.
                </a>
</p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                At
                <a class="text-magic underline hover:text-magic-blue-700" href="https://www.google.com/intl/de_en/policies/technologies/product-privacy/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
                    https://www.google.com/intl/de_en/policies/technologies/product-privacy/
                </a>
                you can adjust privacy settings as desired.
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                The app on the iOS operating system uses a map service from Apple Inc. (1 Infinite Loop, Cupertino, CA 95014, USA). Apple will only receive your IP address after your consent by using the map service. The legal basis for this is also Art. 6 (1) f) GDPR (balancing of interests, based on our interest in offering you the most attractive app possible).
            </p>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
                The privacy settings for this can be adjusted in the device settings of your end device.
            </p>
<p class="mb-2 font-bold text-gray-1200">
                6.3 Network Communication (full internet access)
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                This permission is needed to enable the app to establish a connection to us via the Internet and to communicate with us regarding the services you require.
            </p>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
                The legal basis for the data processing associated herewith is Art. 6 (1) b) GDPR (performance of a contract).
            </p>
<p class="mb-2 font-bold text-gray-1200">
                6.4 Camera
            </p>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
                Your consent to access your system-side camera is required so that you can digitize a room while using our app.
            </p>
<p class="font-base text-gray-700">
<strong>Right of withdrawal:</strong>
</p>
<div id="statistics"></div>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                You are entitled to revoke your consent at any time for the future. Such revocation may be effected in the settings of the app under “Preferences -&gt; Send data to improve magicplan”.
            </p>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
                The legal basis for access to your system camera is Art. 6 (1) a) GDPR (your consent).
            </p>
<h2 class="mb-4 text-lg font-bold text-gray-1200">
                7. Statistics
            </h2>
<p class="mb-2 font-bold text-gray-1200">
                7.1 Google Firebase
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                We also use Google Firebase (Google Inc., 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA, “Google”) to analyze overall usage, categorize user groups, and optimize our products. In some cases, the content of our app is also adapted to the measurement results. Google Firebase processes your data anonymously and in larger quantities together with data from other users – so that your individual data and measurements can no longer be assigned to you.
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                Google Firebase uses servers located in the EU for these services wherever possible. However, it cannot be ruled out that data may also be transferred to the USA. Google has joined the EU-US Privacy Shield, a data protection agreement between the EU and the US. If you want to know more about Google Firebase and data protection, click here:
                <a class="text-magic underline hover:text-magic-blue-700" href="https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
                    https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy/
                </a>
</p>
<div id="data-security"></div>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
                You can stop the data transfer to Google by deactivating the built-in slider in the settings of the app under “Preferences -&gt; Send data to improve magicplan”.
            </p>
<p class="mb-2 font-bold text-gray-1200">
                7.2 Segment
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
            We use 
                <a class="text-magic underline hover:text-magic-blue-700" href="https://segment.com/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
                    Segment
                </a>
                 (Segment.io Inc., 100 California Street Suite 700 San Francisco, CA 94111 United States, “Segment”) to analyze overall usage, categorize user groups, and optimize our products. In some cases, the content of our app is also adapted to the measurement results.
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                Segment processes your data anonymously and in larger quantities together with data from other users – so that your individual data and measurements can no longer be assigned to you.
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
            Segment uses servers located in the United States. If you want to know more about Segment and data protection, click here: 
                <a class="text-magic underline hover:text-magic-blue-700" href="https://segment.com/product/gdpr/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
                    https://segment.com/product/gdpr/
                </a>
</p>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
                You can stop the data transfer to Segment by deactivating the built-in slider in the settings of the app under “Preferences -&gt; Send data to improve magicplan”.
            </p>
<h2 class="mb-4 text-lg font-bold text-gray-1200">
                8. Data Security
            </h2>
<div id="external-service-providers"></div>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                As far as this app communicates with Sensopia, this is done in encrypted form. Please note, however, that personal data stored locally on your device may be misused by third parties, e.g. if your mobile phone is stolen. We therefore recommend that you carefully secure your device accordingly, e.g. with a code lock.
            </p>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
                If the app is uninstalled, all personal data in the app will be deleted.
            </p>
<h2 class="mb-4 text-lg font-bold text-gray-1200">
                9. External Service Providers
            </h2>
<div id="retention-periods"></div>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
                For certain activities in connection with the “magicplan” app, we use external services providers for data processing (e.g. data center operators). To the extent it is necessary these service providers also process personal data. Sensopia diligently selects and supervises its service providers. They process the data only in accordance with our instructions and are also bound by this privacy statement.
            </p>
<h2 class="mb-4 text-lg font-bold text-gray-1200">
                10. Retention Periods
            </h2>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                We store personal data only as long as it is necessary for the purposes described herein, or as long as it is necessary to comply with statutory storage periods.
            </p>
<div id="your-rights"></div>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
                Automatically collected technical communication data and tracking information within the meaning of sections 3.1 and 3.2 will be deleted or anonymized after a period of 3 years at the latest.
            </p>
<h2 class="mb-4 text-lg font-bold text-gray-1200">
                11. Your rights
            </h2>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                You have the following rights with regard to the personal data concerning you:
            </p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
<strong>
                    Insofar as the legal basis for the processing of data is Art. 6 (1) f) GDPR (the processing of data serves our legitimate interests), you have the right to object to the processing of data at any time. In order to do so, you may send us an email to
                    <a class="text-magic underline hover:text-magic-blue-700" href="mailto:support@magicplan.app">
                        support@magicplan.app.
                    </a>
</strong>
</p>
<p class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
<strong>You furthermore have the following rights with regard to the personal data concerning you:</strong>
</p>
<ul class="list-disc ml-5">
<li class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                    right of information,
                </li>
<li class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                    right of rectification or deletion,
                </li>
<li class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                    right to limitation of processing,
                </li>
<li class="mb-4 font-base text-gray-700">
                    right to data portability.
                </li>
</ul>
<p class="mb-8 font-base text-gray-700">
                You also have the right to complain to a data protection supervisory authority about our processing of your personal data.
