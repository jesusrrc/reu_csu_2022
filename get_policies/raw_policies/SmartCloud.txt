<h2 class="wsite-content-title" style="text-align:left;"><span style=""><span style=""><font size="6">INTRODUCTION</font></span><br/></span></h2>
<div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;"><span style=""><span style="">Thank you for taking the time to review our Privacy Policy. At times, SmartCloud will collect certain personal information about customers and visitors to websites hosted by us. Such information will include both identifiable personal data, as well as non-identifiable personal information. Identifiable personal information will be collected when you sign a contract for service with us, or use our website for a transaction or subscription service. Non-identifiable information is gathered automatically when you visit our website or those websites hosted by us, and stored for use in our system.</span><br/><span style=""></span><br/><span style="">The purpose of this privacy policy is to explain to customers what types of information we will collect and how that information is used. In most cases, we collect this information to ensure network integrity and that we continue to provide you with the most relevant content and best possible service that suits your needs. In some cases, we are required by law to collect personal information about customers. Except where the law requires otherwise, we undertake to protect the confidentiality of such data.</span></span><br/><span style=""><span style=""><br/></span></span></div>
<h2 class="wsite-content-title" style="text-align:left;"><span style=""><span style=""><font size="6">CONFIDENTIALITY</font></span></span></h2>
<div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;"><span style="line-height: 26.1000003814697px; background-color: initial;">SmartCloud respects customer privacy and the privacy of those accessing our website, or those websites hosted by us. We undertake to protect the confidentiality of our customers and users including all personal information supplied in the course of contracting with us for services. We undertake not to sell your personal information to third parties for commercial or marketing purposes.</span><br/><span style="line-height: 26.1000003814697px; background-color: initial;"><br/></span></div>
<h2 class="wsite-content-title" style="text-align:left;"><span style=""><span style=""><font size="6">COLLECTION OF PERSONAL DATA</font></span></span></h2>
<div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;"><span style="line-height: 26.1000003814697px; background-color: initial;">SmartCloud collects personal data about our users when you visit a website hosted by us; apply for a service subscription; respond to a customer questionnaire; enter SmartCloud competitions and through the use of cookie technology. We may also combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from our business partners or affiliates.</span><br/><span style="line-height: 26.1000003814697px; background-color: initial;"><br/></span></div>
<h2 class="wsite-content-title" style="text-align:left;"><span style=""><span style=""><font size="6">USE OF PERSONAL DATA</font></span></span></h2>
<div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;"><span style=""><span style="">SmartCloud may on occasion use your personal information to contact you about promotional offers; advise you of matters relevant to service provision and in some cases, solicit your feedback. However, SmartCloud will provide you with an option within every communication to opt out of receiving any communications of this nature or you can contact Michael McKenna, to ensure that you do not receive such promotional information, at +353 1 960 9057 or at </span><a href="mailto:abuse@smartcloud.ie" style="" title=""><span style="">abuse@smartcloud.ie</span></a><span style="">.</span><br/><span style=""></span><br/><span style="">SmartCloud collects and shares aggregated user data with business partners, sponsors or other third parties for the purposes of developing content and ensuring relevant advertising and content, such user data will never be used to identify individual users. These business partners and affiliated companies do not have any independent right to share this information.</span><br/><span style=""></span><br/><span style=""></span><span style="">SmartCloud may log the websites you visit; collect IP addresses and information about your operating system and the type of browser you use for the purposes of network/system administration; to report aggregate information to our advertisers, and to audit the use of our site. This data however will not be used to identify individual users who will at all times remain anonymous.</span><br/><br/><span style="">Any information SmartCloud collects from you through correspondence with us, whether via e-mail, telephonically or by written letter, will only be used to address the matters within that correspondence. If this requires referring such correspondence within SmartCloud or to a third party to ensure customer service, your personal information will only be disclosed to the point necessary to address your query or concerns, and will otherwise be kept confidential.</span></span><br/><span style=""><span style=""><br/></span></span></div>
<h2 class="wsite-content-title" style="text-align:left;"><span style=""><span style=""><font size="6">PUBLIC SPACE (BULLETIN BOARDS, CHAT ROOMS AND THIRD-PARTY SITES)</font></span></span></h2>
<div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;"><span style=""><span style="">Any information that customers disclose in a public space, including on any bulletin board, chat room or any site SmartCloud may host for you, is available to anyone else who visits that space. SmartCloud cannot safeguard any information you disclose there.</span></span><br/><span style=""><span style=""><br/></span></span></div>
<h2 class="wsite-content-title" style="text-align:left;"><span style=""><span style=""><font size="6">SITE LINKING</font></span></span></h2>
<div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;"><span style=""><span style="">SmartCloud’s websites contain many links to sites that belong to third parties unrelated to us. SmartCloud cannot be held responsible for any use of your personal information arising from you disclosing personal such information on third party sites. SmartCloud cannot protect any information you may disclose on these sites and recommends that you review the privacy policy statements of those sites you visit.</span></span><br/><span style=""><span style=""><br/></span></span></div>
<h2 class="wsite-content-title" style="text-align:left;"><span style=""><span style=""><font size="6">MINORS</font></span></span></h2>
<div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;"><span style=""><span style="">SmartCloud will not enter into a service subscription contract with a minor unless such minor has explicit written consent from a parent or guardian to do so. SmartCloud undertakes not to contact minors about promotional offers or for marketing purposes without a parental consent.</span></span><br/><span style=""><span style=""><br/></span></span></div>
