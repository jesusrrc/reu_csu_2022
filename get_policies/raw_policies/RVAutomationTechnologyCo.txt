<div class="container">
<div class="inner p-5 bg-white shadow rounded">
<p>
                As a service provider, we value our visitors and clients and respect their
                right to privacy. Therefore, we have designed a privacy policy to explain how their ‘Personally
                Identifiable Information’ is being used online.
            </p>
<p>It is our humble request for everyone to read the entire privacy policy carefully to understand how we
                collect, use, and protect their information with our website.</p>
<p>For instance, what information do we collect as soon as you sign up for our blogs, newsletters, or
                perhaps fill up an inquiry form?</p>
<h4>Information You Provide</h4>
<p>
                As soon as you register on our website, you may be asked to enter the
                following personal information.
            </p>
<ul>
<li>Name </li>
<li>Email </li>
<li>Phone Number </li>
<li>While filling out an inquiry form or a feedback form, </li>
</ul>
<p>for instance, you’ll be asked to enter the above personal information. This information
                helps us to provide a personalized experience for our service.</p>
<h4>IP Address </h4>
<p>
                The site may access your device’s location using your Internet Protocol (IP)
                address. This information helps us to follow your country’s data compliance regulations to their
                fullest. We may
                also use your IP address to determine the fastest route for your device to connect to our website.</p>
<h4>How do We Use Your Information?</h4>
<p>
                The information, we collect from our visitors and clients is
                used in the following manner:</p>
<ul>
<li>To personalize the user experience by delivering the type of content and services as per your
                    interests.
                </li>
<li>To notify you regarding special offers on services via sending periodic emails.</li>
<li>To send you reminders and confirmations as per your chosen services.</li>
<li>To send you technical notices, updates, alerts and support messages.</li>
<li>To respond to your queries, feedback, and comments to provide better customer service and support.
                </li>
<li>To notify you of the latest services, offers, news posts, and blogs, which we think will interest
                    you.</li>
<li>To analyze and manage the activities with our content and website’s performance.</li>
</ul>
<h4>Email Management</h4>
<p>
                RV Technologies may try to contact you via emails, which may happen due to
                several reasons. For instance, when you subscribe to our newsletters, you may receive periodic emails
                regarding
                the content update.
            </p>
<p>Emails, which you’ll receive from RV Technologies will always contain a link in
                the
                footer, where you can unsubscribe altogether. You can also select your communication preferences using
                the same
                link.</p>
<h4>When do We Share and Disclose Your Information?</h4>
<p>
                Our visitor’s privacy is our priority, and
                therefore, at every step, we make sure to protect it. We only share your personal information under one
                of the
                following circumstances.</p>
<h4>With your consent</h4>
<p>
                When you allow RV Technologies to share your personal information. It may
                happen when you submit your information through our offers or services.
            </p>
<p>For instance, if you decide
                to fill
                out a query form, your name, email address, and company name will be collected by RV Technologies.</p>
<h4>Legal Obligations</h4>
<p>
                RV Technologies may also disclose your personal information when it is
                required to satisfy a law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request. It may also happen when
                there
                needs to be an investigation carried out regarding violation of the Term of Use, or protect the rights,
                or
