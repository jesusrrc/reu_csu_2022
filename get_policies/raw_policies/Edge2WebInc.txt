<h1 class="entry-title">Edge2Web Privacy Policy</h1>
<section class="content-editor">
<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p>Our privacy policy will help you understand what information we collect at Edge2Web, how Edge2Web uses it, and what choices you have.</p>
<p>When we talk about “Edge2Web,” “we,” “our,” or “us” in this policy, we are referring to Edge2Web, Inc., the company which provides the Services. When we talk about the “Services” in this policy, we are referring to our cloud-based application development and runtime platform, as well as our commercial website. Our Services are currently available for use via a web browser.</p>
<p> </p>
<h2>Information we collect and receive</h2>
<h6><strong><br/>
1. Customer Data</strong></h6>
<p>Content, meta-data, and information submitted by users to the Services is referred to in this policy as<strong>“Customer Data.”</strong> As further explained below, Customer Data is controlled by the entity or other third party that administers an Edge2Web customer organization (the <strong>“Customer”</strong>). Where Edge2Web collects or processes Customer Data, it does so on behalf of the Customer.</p>
<p>If you join a Customer organization and create a user account on the Edge2Web Services, or if you access the Edge2Web Services via single sign-on from an environment that is hosting the Edge2Web Services,  you are a “user,” as further described in the <a href="https://edge2web.com/edge2web-user-terms-of-service/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">User Terms of Service</a>. If you are using the Services by permission of a Customer, whether that Customer is your employer, another organization, or an individual, that Customer determines its own policies regarding storage, access, modification, deletion, sharing, and retention of Customer Data which may apply to your use of the Services. Please check with the Customer about the policies and settings it has in place.</p>
<h6><strong><br/>
2. Other information</strong></h6>
<p>Edge2Web may also collect the following information:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Account creation information.</strong> Users provide information such as an email address and password to create an account.</li>
<li><strong>Organization setup information.</strong> When a Customer creates a tenant organization using the Services, we collect an email address, an organization name (such as org-name.edge2web.com), domain details, user name for the individual setting up the tenant organization, and password.</li>
