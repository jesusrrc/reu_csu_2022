<p>Your personal data will be stored in databases located in regions across the globe including India. The databases are controlled by our administrative staff located outside of the European Union including in India and can be accessed electronically.</p>
<p><strong>Specific transfer mechanisms outside of the European Union under the GDPR: </strong>Where we transfer personal data outside the EU and between our group companies, we either transfer personal data to countries that provide an adequate level of protection as determined by the European Commission, or use alternative safeguards. Standard contractual clauses are used as the appropriate safeguards. To find out more about standard contractual clauses, click <a href="http://eur-lex.europa.eu/LexUriServ/LexUriServ.do?uri=OJ:L:2010:039:0005:0018:EN:PDF" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
<p>To the extent Protection of Personal Information Act (“<strong>POPIA</strong>”) applies, where we transfer personal data outside of the country in which Genpact is established, we only transfer personal data to countries that provide an adequate level of protection and/or we ensure that we have appropriate safeguards in place to cover these transfers, as permitted under the applicable data protection legislation.</p>
<p>If you would like more information on any of the data transfer mechanisms we rely on, please contact us at <a href="/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#f6b2a6b9d8b1939886979582b6b1939886979582d895999b"><span class="__cf_email__" data-cfemail="3276627d1c75575c425351467275575c425351461c515d5f">[email protected]</span></a>.</p>
<p><strong>6. Data storage and retention.<br/></strong>Genpact shall retain your personal data pursuant to the business purposes and in line with our retention policies. We will only keep your personal data for as long as is reasonably necessary taking into consideration the purposes outlined above or to comply with legal, accounting or reporting requirements under applicable law(s).</p>
<p>To determine the appropriate retention period for personal data, we consider the amount, nature, and sensitivity of the personal data, the potential risk of harm from unauthorized use or disclosure of personal data, the purposes for which we process the personal data and whether we can achieve those purposes through other means, and the applicable legal requirements.</p>
<p>Personal data received from prospective candidates shall be retained in accordance with the retention criteria set out in our <a href="/privacy/notice-for-employment-candidates">Candidate Privacy Notice.</a></p>
<p>Personal data received from prospective customers shall be retained for the duration of the prospective customer’s business relationship with Genpact and in accordance with the retention criteria set out in our <a href="/privacy/notice-for-customers">Customer Privacy Notice.</a></p>

<p><strong>7. Existence of automated profiling.<br/></strong>Genpact uses automated profiling in limited circumstances relating to email campaigns. This enables us to identify and target potential customers and/or business partners and aims to provide you with relevant and timely content based on your indicated interests.</p>
