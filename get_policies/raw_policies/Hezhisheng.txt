<section class="privacyIntro">
<div class="wrapper">
<h3>Regarding the handling of personal information</h3>
<p>This "Privacy Policy Regarding the handling of personal information" sets forth the policy of HENNGE K.K. ("HENNGE") with respect to the handling of personal information.</p>
</div>
</section>
<section class="privacySection">
<div class="wrapper">
<h4 class="privacySection_ttl">
                Acquisition of Personal Information
            </h4>
<p>
                HENNGE will never acquire any personal information in a fraudulent or unlawful manner.
            </p>
</div>
</section>
<section class="privacySection">
<div class="wrapper">
<h4 class="privacySection_ttl">
                Purpose of Utilization of Personal Information
            </h4>
<p>
                Customers' Personal information provided to HENNGE may be utilized within the
                scope of purposes as set forth below;
            </p>
<ol class="privacySection_list listOL">
<li>
                    Purposes of Utilization of Customers' Personal Information
                    <ol>
<li>Support of HENNGE products and services;</li>
<li>Promotion of HENNGE products and services;</li>
<li>Conducting customer surveys;</li>
<li>Development of new HENNGE products and services; and</li>
<li>Communications related to our business or ancillary matters.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>Purpose of Utilization of Personal Information of Applicants for Hiring Recruiting activities, selection of and other relevant communication to applicants.</li>
<li>
                    Purposes of Utilization of Shareholders' Personal Information
                    <ol>
<li>Execution of rights and performance of obligations by the shareholders based on the provisions of the Companies Act of Japan;</li>
<li>Generating data and processing statistics based on applicable laws and regulations; and</li>
<li>Responding to inquiries from shareholders.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>
                    Purposes of Utilization of Personal Information of Employees (including employees who resigned and retired from HENNGE)
                    <ol>
<li>Work related to employment management (including employees' family members, employees who are loaned to other companies and employees who are transferred) ;</li>
<li>Work related to payment of remuneration;</li>
<li>Work related to Employees' welfare program;</li>
<li>Work related to health and safety of Employees;</li>
<li>Procedures upon resignation/retirement of Employees; and</li>
<li>Other necessary procedures and communications based on applicable laws and regulations as well as from work perspectives.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>Common purposes of Utilization of Personal Information of Those Who Fall under Above-mentioned Paragraphs 1 to 4 as well as those who made inquiries to HENNGE<br/>
                        Responding to various inquiries.</li>
</ol>
<p>
                Also, Customers' utilization information of the HENNGE website may be utilized for analytical purposes to provide optimal services to Customers and to improve the content of its website.
            </p>
</div>
</section>
<section class="privacySection">
<div class="wrapper">
<h4 class="privacySection_ttl">
                Scope of Utilization of Personal Information
            </h4>
<p>Personal information provided by Customers to HENNGE will never be disclosed nor provided to any third party except for the following cases where;</p>
<ol class="privacySection_list listOL">
<li>Customers consent to the disclosure;</li>
<li>the disclosure is required by applicable laws and ordinances;</li>
<li>the relevant personal information is provided where the handling of such information is delegated to a third party, whole or a part, to the extent necessary to achieve the previously mentioned purpose of utilization;</li>
<li>the relevant personal information is provided while the business is succeeded due to merger or other reasons;</li>
<li>the relevant personal information is jointly utilized in accordance with the paragraph, "Joint Utilization of Personal Information" below; and</li>
<li>the disclosure is otherwise permitted by the Act on the Protection of Personal Information of Japan and other applicable laws and ordinances.</li>
</ol>
</div>
</section>
<section class="privacySection">
<div class="wrapper">
<h4 class="privacySection_ttl">
                Joint Utilization of Personal Information
            </h4>
<p>
                HENNGE may share and jointly utilize personal information provided by Customers with its subsidiary, HENNGE Taiwan, Inc. under the previously mentioned "Purpose of Utilization of Personal Information".
            </p>
<ul class="privacySection_list listUL">
<li>Items of personal information which may be jointly utilized:<br/>
                    Customer's company name, address, full name, phone number, and email address</li>
<li>Responsible person at HENNGE with respect to management of personal information which may be jointly utilized:<br/>
                    Kazuhiro Ogura</li>
</ul>
</div>
</section>
<section class="privacySection">
<div class="wrapper">
<h4 class="privacySection_ttl">
                Disclosure, Correction and Stoppage of Utilization of Personal Information
            </h4>
<p>
                In the event that a Customer wishes HENNGE to disclose, correct, or stop
                utilization of his or her own personal information that is under the custody of
                HENNGE, based on the Act on the Protection of Personal Information of Japan, he
                or she is able to make such request anytime by the method designated by
                HENNGE. For details, please contact the "Point of Contact on Handling of Personal
                Information" as indicated below.
            </p>
</div>
</section>
<section class="privacySection">
<div class="wrapper">
<h4 class="privacySection_ttl">
                Compliance with Applicable Laws and Ordinances and Other Rules and Regulations
            </h4>
<p>
                HENNGE will comply with all the applicable laws, ordinances, rules and regulations
                with respect to personal information which is provided by our customers.
            </p>
</div>
</section>
<section class="privacySection">
<div class="wrapper">
<h4 class="privacySection_ttl">
                Secure Management of Personal Information
            </h4>
<p>
                HENNGE will make efforts to keep Customers' personal information accurate and
                up-to-date to the extent necessary to achieve the purpose of utilization. Also,
                HENNGE will make efforts to delete Customers' personal information without delay
                in the event that HENNGE no longer needs to utilize such information. HENNGE will
                develop appropriate internal schemes and will implement appropriate security
                measures in order to prevent divulgence, loss, or damage of personal information
                as well as to manage security of personal information handled by HENNGE.
            </p>
</div>
</section>
<section class="privacySection">
<div class="wrapper">
<h4 class="privacySection_ttl">
                Special Rule with respect to Customers of Enterprise SaaS Service provided by HENNGE
            </h4>
<p>
                HENNGE may acquire and process personal information of third parties for
                Customers as a processor and enterprise SaaS Service provider, on behalf of
                Customers in the spaces thereof located in cloud ("Tenants"). Such personal
                information may be acquired, processed, recorded and stored only for the Tenants
                based on the effective agreement between HENNGE and Customers owing such
                Tenants, notwithstanding the provisions of this policy ".
            </p>
</div>
</section>
<section class="privacySection">
<div class="wrapper">
<h4 class="privacySection_ttl">
                Revisions of This Policy
            </h4>
<p>
                HENNGE will review its operations with respect to the handling of personal
                information as appropriate, make efforts to keep improving, and may revise this
                policy as necessary. In the event of revision of this policy, the revised version will
                be made available on the HENNGE website and in other forms.
            </p>
</div>
</section>
<section class="privacySection">
<div class="wrapper">
<h4 class="privacySection_ttl">
                Point of Contact on Handling of Personal Information
            </h4>
<p>
                Please contact below with respect to requests for disclosure, correction, or
                stoppage of utilization of your personal information or any complaint and other
                inquiries with respect to personal information provided to HENNGE;
            </p>
<p>
<a href="mailto:privacy-info@hennge.com">privacy-info@hennge.com</a><br/>
                HENNGE K.K.<br/>
                Personal Information Management Staff
            </p>
</div>
</section>
