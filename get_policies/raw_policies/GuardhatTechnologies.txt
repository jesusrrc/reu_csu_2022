<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:12pt"><strong>In Short:</strong>  We collect personal information that you or your Employer provide to us, as well as information gathered through the use of our Services.  </span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:12pt">If you contact us through our website or via other services (such as via email, messaging applications or social media), we will collect the information you provide through that communication (such as your username, email address, phone number or social media handle).  </span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:12pt">When you use our Services, we will collect the information you or your Employer supply to us in order to associate information about your use of our Services with you, and the information gathered through the devices that you or your Employer link with our Services.  For example, from either you or your Employer, we will collect your name or employee ID number, and from the devices we will collect the information the particular device is designed to collect, which may include geolocation information, environmental information, activity tracking information, audiovisual information and information about health or safety incidents you experience.  In some instances, you or your Employer can choose to link our devices with third party devices, enabling our device to collect information from the third party device.  The information gathered by the third party device is determined by the third party, not by us, and you should consult their privacy policies to understand what data they collect and how they use it.  </span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:12pt">When you install our mobile applications, you will be asked to provide logon information that your Employer has provided us, in order to verify your identity and to associate the information we gather about you with the correct Employer.  In addition, these applications collect information gathered from the sensors in the mobile device, such as geolocation information and information collected through motion sensors.  They are also equipped to collect messaging and audiovisual information recorded using the applications.  Finally, the mobile applications can also collect information from devices that are linked to them, including devices we offer and certain third-party devices.  </span></span></p>
<p> </p>

<p><span style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:12pt"><strong>In short</strong>: We use your information to help you and your Employer gather data about your activities and environment, to enable us to provide the Services we offer (including to assess the how our Services are used and to help us further develop our Services over time) and to fulfill our legal obligations.  </span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:12pt">Our Services are designed to enable Employers to monitor workplace safety and to gather analytics about workplace behavior. We use the information gathered through our Services for those purposes. We also use your information for the purpose of fulfilling our contractual obligations to you and to your Employer, and as needed to fulfill our legal obligations.  We use the information we collect or receive:</span></span></p>
<ul style="margin-left:40px">
<li><span style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:12pt">To send safety alerts.  We will use the information we gather through our Services to send real-time safety alerts to the frontline worker and their supervisor and/or support personnel </span></span></li>
<li><span style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:12pt">To analyze workplace data. We will use the information we collect about you to help your Employer analyze workplace practices and safety, including to gather information about specific incidents. We also use and store this information in aggregated and anonymized form that is not associated with individual end users and does not include personal information.  </span></span></li>
<li><span style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:12pt">To send you marketing and promotional communications. You can opt-out of our marketing emails at any time (see the "WHAT ARE YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS" below).</span></span></li>
<li><span style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:12pt">To send administrative information to you. <li><span style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:12pt">To manage your account. We will use your information to manage your account with us.  </span></span></li>
<li><span style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:12pt">To request feedback.  We may, from time to time, use your information to request feedback and to contact you about your use of our Services.</span></span></li>

<p><span style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:12pt"><strong>In short:</strong> We will keep your information for one year, unless otherwise directed by your Employer or required by applicable law.  </span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:12pt">We retain information we collect as long as it is necessary and relevant to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy and to the extent permitted by applicable legal requirements.  As a general matter, we retain your information for up to one year after collecting it.  Your Employer can direct us to keep information for longer or shorter periods, and we will honor that direction unless legally required otherwise.  In addition, if we are required to retain your information for a longer period under applicable law, then we will do so.  When we no longer intend to retain your information, we will either delete or anonymize it, or, if this is not possible (for example, because your personal information has been stored in backup archives), then we will securely store your personal information and isolate it from any further processing until deletion is possible.</span></span></p>

<p><span style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:12pt"><strong>In short</strong>: We aim to protect your personal information through a system of organizational and technical security measures. </span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:12pt">We have implemented what we believe to be appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process, and we partner with security providers who alert us to any anomalies or security concerns related to our Services.  Our third-party service providers are required to implement security measures as well.  Your Employers are responsible for protecting the information we provide to them, so we suggest you contact them to understand how they seek to protect it.  </span></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family:Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:12pt">Please also remember that we cannot guarantee that the internet itself is 100% secure.
