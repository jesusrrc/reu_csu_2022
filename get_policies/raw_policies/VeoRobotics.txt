<p>Applications that require the speed and strength of standard industrial robots also increasingly require human dexterity and versatility. FreeMove<sup data-preserve-html-node="true">®</sup> is a comprehensive 3D safeguarding solution that reduces the cost and complexity of human-robot collaboration.</p>
</div></div></div></div><div class="row sqs-row"><div class="col sqs-col-12 span-12"><div class="sqs-block spacer-block sqs-block-spacer" data-aspect-ratio="4.075546719681909" data-block-type="21" id="block-yui_3_17_2_1_1609966330020_13125"><div class="sqs-block-content"> </div></div><div class="row sqs-row"><div class="col sqs-col-6 span-6"><div class="sqs-block markdown-block sqs-block-markdown" data-block-type="44" id="block-yui_3_17_2_1_1608643937643_16431"><div class="sqs-block-content"><h2 id="freemove-sup-data-preserve-html-node-true-sup-north-american-certification-march-2021-"><strong>FreeMove<sup data-preserve-html-node="true">®</sup> North American Certification March 2021 </strong></h2>
<p>Veo FreeMove<sup data-preserve-html-node="true">®</sup> is available to ship with ISO 13849 certification for PLd, Category 3. FreeMove<sup data-preserve-html-node="true">®</sup> monitors workcells in 3D and implements dynamic Speed and Separation Monitoring (SSM) as defined by ISO 10218-2:2011 and ISO/TS 15066:2016, enabling safe interaction between humans and robots.</p>
