<p><strong>General Privacy Policy of Aerodyne Group Ltd <b>and its subsidiaries </b>and Aerodyne Equipment applying also to websites belonging to any of those companies</strong></p>
<p><strong>For the purposes of this Notice, </strong></p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Aerodyne Group Ltd includes its subsidiaries Aerodyne Global Ltd and Nojerm Ltd, and the separate Partnership Aerodyne Equipment. When the text refers to ‘We’, ‘us’, ‘our’ , ‘ours’ and similar, the pronoun includes any and all of Aerodyne Group Ltd., Aerodyne Global Ltd., Nojerm Ltd and Aerodyne Equipment. This website is owned by Aerodyne Global Ltd.</strong></li>
<li><strong>References in this Notice to the Data Protection Act 2018 includes the General Data Protection Regulations (GDPR) as they apply in the UK.</strong></li>
</ul>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>WHAT IS THIS PRIVACY POLICY FOR?</strong></p>
<p>This privacy policy is for our websites and is issued by us and governs the privacy of its users who choose to use it. We take your privacy seriously and take steps to comply with the GDPR Regulations which came into force in May 2018.</p>
<p>The policy sets out the different areas where user privacy is concerned and outlines the obligations &amp; requirements of the users, the website and website owners. Furthermore the way this website processes, stores and protects user data and information will also be detailed within this policy.</p>
<p>This privacy policy will be updated by the data controller when necessary; always refer to this page for the updated version. <strong>Last updated on 17/11/2021.</strong></p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>If you have any questions please contact us:</strong></p>
<p>Aerodyne Group Ltd</p>
<p>Innovation House</p>
<p>Tunnel Bank</p>
<p>Bourne, Lincolnshire, PE10 0DJ</p>

<p> </p>
<p>You may also wish to contact the Information Commissioners Office for information about GDPR, see below.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>THE WEBSITE</strong></p>
<p>This website and its owners take a proactive approach to user privacy and ensure the necessary steps are taken to protect the privacy of its users throughout their visiting experience. This website complies with all UK national laws and requirements for user privacy, including GDPR Regulations.</p>
<p><strong> </strong></p>
<p><strong>INFORMATION WE MAY GATHER FROM YOU AND PROCESS</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Name and company name</li>
<li>Contact information including postal address/email address/ phone numbers (Landline and/or Mobile)</li>
<li>Job title /role within your Company</li>
<li>Demographic information, such as postcode, preferences and interests</li>
<li>Cookies</li>
<li>Website usage data</li>
<li>Other information relevant to client enquiries</li>
<li>The reason for your enquiry, e.g. details of your project or needs</li>
<li>Other information pertaining to special offers and surveys</li>
<li>Credit/debit card information</li>
</ul>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>HOW WE USE THIS INFORMATION AND PURPOSES FOR WHICH WE PROCESS IT</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>For our own internal records</li>
<li>To improve the products and services we provide</li>
<li>To contact you in response to a specific enquiry</li>
<li>To customise the website for you</li>
<li>To send you promotional emails about products, services, offers and other things we think may be relevant to you</li>
</ul>
<p>We use Google Analytics to collect user information about visitor behaviour on our website. Please see our Cookie Policy, as available on this website.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING THE DATA</strong></p>
<p>The basis for processing your data is Legitimate Interest, in that it is in the furtherance of our business and will enable us to market our products and services and inform you of new products, special offers, information relating to products you may have bought from us etc.</p>
<p>Another basis, where it applies is that of Consent, where you have given us permission to contact you.</p>
<p>In the case where we already have commercial dealings with you, we may process the data on the basis of Contract, and also of Legal Obligation.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>PROCESSORS OF THE DATA </strong></p>
<p>We are normally the only the processors of the data. Your details will not be normally passed on to any third parties. However, we store them in our ‘Goldvision’ CRM system, which is on the Cloud, and is backed up regularly.</p>
<p>We will only pass any of this data on (and only so much of it as is necessary) as needed for the normal purposes of operating a commercial enterprise, or are required to by law, for example to our Financial, Legal, or other Advisors and Consultants, and Government Departments. This would be on the basis of Legitimate Interest and/or Legal Requirement.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>CROSS BORDER TRANSFERS</strong></p>
<p><strong> </strong>We will not transfer this data outside of the EEA. Should the data commercially or legally need to be shared with another party, for example our Accountants, any further transfer would be subject to that party’s GDPR Policies.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>RETENTION PERIOD</strong></p>
<p>We will retain the data securely until such time as it is no longer needed, or is inapplicable, or until you request us to delete it under your ‘Right to be forgotten’</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>YOUR RIGHTS UNDER GDPR</strong></p>
<p>These are:-</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Access.</strong> You may require us to tell you what data about you we are processing, if your request is reasonable.</li>
<li><strong>Rectification. </strong>You may require us to correct or update data about you.</li>
<li><strong>Erasure. </strong>In certain circumstances you may ask us to delete data we hold about you.</li>
<li><strong>Restriction of Processing. </strong>In certain circumstances you may have a right to restrict what is done with the data.</li>
<li><strong>Data Portability. </strong>You have the right to obtain a copy of the data so as to transfer it to another controller in a commonly-used format.</li>
<li><strong>Object to Processing. </strong>You have the right to object to certain types of marketing for example, and we have to consider the request and justify our decision.</li>
<li><strong>Automated decision making including profiling. </strong>In certain circumstances, right to object to significant decisions made solely by automated means.</li>
<li><strong>Compensation. </strong>Right to claim compensation for damages caused by infringement of the Regulations by the Data Controller or Processor.</li>
</ol>
<p><strong> </strong></p>
<p><strong>WHETHER A STATUTORY OR A CONTRACTUAL REQUIREMENT. </strong></p>
<p>Our Privacy Notices and Policies are due to a statutory requirement.</p>
<p><strong>WHETHER THERE IS AUTOMATED DECISION MAKING AND UNDER WHAT LOGIC. </strong></p>
<p><strong> </strong>There is no automated decision making currently.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>USE OF COOKIES</strong></p>
<p>This website uses cookies to better the user’s experience while visiting the website. Where applicable, this website uses a cookie control system allowing the user on their first visit to the website to allow or disallow the use of cookies on their computer / device. This complies with recent legislation requirements for websites to obtain explicit consent from users before leaving behind or reading files such as cookies on a user’s computer / device.</p>
<p>Cookies are small files saved to the user’s computer’s hard drive that track, that save and store information about the user’s interactions and usage of the website. This allows the website, through its server to provide the users with a tailored experience within this website.</p>
<p>Users are advised that if they wish to deny the use and saving of cookies from this website onto their computer’s hard drive, they should take necessary steps within their web browser’s security settings to block all cookies from this website and its external serving vendors.</p>
<p>This website uses tracking software to monitor its visitors to better understand how they use it. This software is provided by Google Analytics, which uses cookies to track visitor usage. The software will save a cookie to your computer’s hard drive in order to track and monitor your engagement and usage of the website, but will not store, save or collect personal information. You can read Google’s privacy policy here for further information <a href="http://www.google.com/privacy.html"><strong>http://www.google.com/privacy.html</strong></a>.</p>
<p>Other cookies may be stored to your computer’s hard drive by external vendors when this website uses referral programs, sponsored links or adverts. Such cookies are used for conversion and referral tracking and typically expire after 30 days, though some may take longer. No personal information is stored, saved or collected.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>CONTACT &amp; COMMUNICATION</strong></p>
<p>Users contacting this website and/or its owners do so at their own discretion and provide any such personal details requested at their own risk. Your personal information is kept private and stored securely until a time when it is no longer required or has no use, as detailed in the GDPR and in the Data Protection Act 2018. Every effort has been made to ensure a safe and secure form to email submission process but we advise users using such form to email processes that they do so at their own risk.</p>
<p>This website and its owners use any information submitted to provide you with further information about the products / services they offer or to assist you in answering any questions or queries you may have submitted. This includes using your details to subscribe you to any email newsletter program the website operates but only if this was made clear to you and your express permission was granted when submitting any form to email process. If you the consumer have previously purchased from or enquired about purchasing from us a product or service that we offer, we will contact you at our discretion on the basis of legitimate interest unless you request us not to. This is by no means an entire list of your user rights in regards to receiving email marketing material.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>EMAIL NEWSLETTER</strong></p>
<p>This website operates an email newsletter program, used to inform subscribers about products and services supplied by this website. Users can subscribe through an online automated process should they wish to do so but do so at their own discretion. Some subscriptions may be manually processed through prior written agreement with the user.</p>
<p>Subscriptions are taken in compliance with UK Spam Laws detailed in the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations 2003. All personal details relating to subscriptions are held securely and in accordance with the Data Protection Act 2018. Apart from what is stated in ‘PROCESSORS OF THE DATA’, above, no personal details are passed on to third parties or shared with companies / people outside of Aerodyne Group Ltd. Under the Data Protection Act 2018, you may request a copy of personal information held about you by this website’s email newsletter program. If you would like a copy of the information held on you, please write to the business address in the footer of this website.</p>
<p>Email marketing campaigns published by this website or its owners may contain tracking facilities within the actual email. Subscriber activity is tracked and stored in a database for future analysis and evaluation. Such tracked activity may include; the opening of emails, forwarding of emails, the clicking of links within the email content, times, dates and frequency of activity [this is by no means a comprehensive list]. This information is used to refine future email campaigns and supply the user with more relevant content based around their activity.</p>
<p>In compliance with UK Spam Laws and the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations 2003, subscribers are given the opportunity to unsubscribe at any time through an automated system. This process is detailed at the footer of each email campaign. If an automated un-subscription system is unavailable, clear instructions on how to unsubscribe will be detailed instead.</p>
<p> </p>
<p><strong>CONTROLLING INFORMATION ABOUT YOU</strong></p>
<p>To opt out of receiving communication from us, by email, text, post or telephone, please email sales@aerodyneuk.com or write to us at Aerodyne Group Ltd, Innovation House, Tunnel Bank, Bourne, Lincs., PE10 0DJ, detailing the media of communication that you do not want us to use for you in future.</p>
<p>To opt out of this website collecting Cookies, please activate the relevant setting on your browser to refuse the setting of all or some Cookies. Please note this may affect access to parts of this website.</p>
