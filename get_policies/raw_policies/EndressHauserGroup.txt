The analysis involves linking the collected data, or elements thereof, and the web beacons to your email address and an individual ID.  We use the data obtained in this way to create a user profile to customize advertising email communications to better reflect your interests.  We record the time you read our emails, which links in the email you click, and then draw conclusions about your personal interests.  We may also link this data with your activities on our website.  You can object at any time to this tracking by unsubscribing from our advertising email communications.  
</p><p class="eh-generic--text">A list of the third parties we utilize for advertising email communications and a link to their privacy policies is below:
</p><ul class="eh-list-01 eh-generic--list"><li class="eh-list--item"><p class="eh-generic--text"><a class="eh-link eh-link--03" data-cs-tracking-ps-name="PS.ParagraphBlock" href="https://www.oracle.com/legal/privacy/marketing-cloud-data-cloud-privacy-policy.html">Oracle Eloqua</a></p></li><li class="eh-list--item"><p class="eh-generic--text"><a class="eh-link eh-link--03" data-cs-tracking-ps-name="PS.ParagraphBlock" href="https://www.inxmail.com/data-conditions">Inxmail</a></p></li></ul></div>
<h2 class="eh-headline-02 eh-dyn-ws eh-m-b-xs-2 eh-generic--headline">15. Do Not Track</h2> <div class="eh-rich-content-content"><p class="eh-generic--text">Endress+Hauser does not currently employ a process for automatically responding to “Do Not Track” (DNT) signals sent by web browsers, mobile devices or other mechanisms. Per industry standards, third parties may be able to collect information, including personal data about your online activities over time and across different websites or online services.</p></div>
<h2 class="eh-headline-02 eh-dyn-ws eh-m-b-xs-2 eh-generic--headline">16. Right of Access, Correction, Deletion, Objection, and to Opt-Out</h2> <div class="eh-rich-content-content"><p class="eh-generic--text">We take reasonable steps to ensure that your personal data on file with us is accurate and current. At your request, Endress+Hauser will inform you within a reasonable period of time, in accordance with applicable law, whether and what personal data about you is stored in our systems. If, despite our efforts to maintain data accuracy and timeliness, incorrect data should be stored in our systems, we will correct it at your request. You also have the right to restrict the processing of your information in certain circumstances, object to processing or ask that we transfer the information you gave us to another organization.
</p><p class="eh-generic--text"><strong>To exercise any of your rights or for questions concerning your personal data, information requests, or suggestions and complaints, please contact us at by email at</strong><a class="eh-link eh-link--03" data-cs-tracking-ps-name="PS.ParagraphBlock" href="mailto: legal.us.sc@endress.com"> legal.us.sc@endress.com</a>;<strong> by mail at Legal Department, 2350 Endress Place, Greenwood, Indiana 46143; or toll free at 1-844-0730.</strong></p><p class="eh-generic--text">Only you, or someone legally authorized to act on your behalf, may make a request related to your personal data. You may also make a request on behalf of your minor child. We cannot respond to your request or provide you with personal data if we cannot verify your identity or authority to make the request and confirm the personal data relates to you.
</p></div>
<h2 class="eh-headline-02 eh-dyn-ws eh-m-b-xs-2 eh-generic--headline">17. Rights of California Residents </h2> <div class="eh-rich-content-content"><p class="eh-generic--text">Effective on January 1, 2020, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides consumers (California residents) with specific rights regarding their personal data. If you reside in California, you may exercise the following rights:</p><ul class="eh-list-01 eh-generic--list"><li class="eh-list--item"><p class="eh-generic--text">The right to request that we disclose to you the personal data we have collected about you over the past 12 months from the day of your request;</p></li><li class="eh-list--item"><p class="eh-generic--text">The right to request that we delete any of the personal data that we collected from you and retained, subject to certain exceptions;</p></li><li class="eh-list--item"><p class="eh-generic--text">The right to direct us not to sell your personal data at any time (the “right to op-out”); and</p></li><li class="eh-list--item"><p class="eh-generic--text">The right to designate an authorized agent to make a request under the CCPA under your behalf.</p></li></ul><p class="eh-generic--text">We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.  We will confirm that we received your request within ten (10) days and respond within forty-five (45) days of its receipt.  If we require more time, we will inform you of the reason and extension period in writing.</p><p class="eh-generic--text"></p><p class="eh-generic--text">For more detailed information regarding your rights under the CCPA, <a class="eh-link eh-link--03" data-cs-tracking-file-name="CA Specific Notice - dec 11 draft (BT 12-16-2019) (003).pdf" data-cs-tracking-file-type="pdf" data-cs-tracking-page-name="data protection_5590036" data-cs-tracking-ps-name="PS.ParagraphBlock" href="/_storage/asset/8671414/storage/master/file/28401675/download/CA%20Specific%20Notice%20-%20dec%2011%20draft%20(BT%2012-16-2019)%20(003).pdf">please click here</a>.
Under California Civil Code Section 1798.83 (“Shine the Light Law”), users and customers who are residents of California may request certain information about our disclosures of personal data during the prior calendar year to third parties for their direct marketing purposes. We will comply with your request within thirty (30) days or as otherwise required by statute. Please be aware that not all information is covered by “Shine the Light” requirements and only information covered will be included in our response.
</p></div>
<h2 class="eh-headline-02 eh-dyn-ws eh-m-b-xs-2 eh-generic--headline">18. Rights of Nevada Residents</h2> <div class="eh-rich-content-content"><p class="eh-generic--text">Effective on October 1, 2019, the Nevada Privacy of Information Collected on the Internet from Consumers Act (“NPICICA”) provides consumers in Nevada with specific rights regarding their personal data, including the right to op-out of the sale of your personal data.</p></div>

<h2 class="eh-headline-02 eh-dyn-ws eh-m-b-xs-2 eh-generic--headline">20. If we learn that we mistakenly collected any information from anyone younger than 16 years of age, we will dispose of the information immediately after notification.</p></div>

Retention of Personal Data </h2> <div class="eh-rich-content-content"><p class="eh-generic--text">Endress+Hauser processes and stores your personal data based upon its legal and business needs and then securely destroys the information. It is possible that personal data may be stored for the period of time for which claims may be asserted against us (statutory limitation periods range from three to thirty years). Commercial and tax regulations and laws also impose documentation and retention obligations.</p></div>
