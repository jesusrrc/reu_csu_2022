<div class="content"><h1>About Us</h1>
<p> </p>
<h3>WHO WE ARE</h3>
<p style="text-align: justify;">BURNDY® was founded on July 19, 1924 as Burndy Engineering Company in Brooklyn, New York, by science historian and civic leader <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bern_Dibner" target="_blank">Bern Dibner</a>. The name BURNDY® is a contraction of Dr. Dibner’s first name and last initial, “BERN D”. Dr. Dibner designed and patented a universal connector, requiring neither soldering nor welding thereby enabling a unified grid.  Until its acquisition, in 1988, by the French corporation Framatome Connectors International (FCI), BURNDY® remained family-owned. Later, in November 2005, FCI was acquired by Bain Capital and it was in October 2009 that the BURNDY® segment became a part of the HUBBELL Electrical Systems family of companies (NYSE: HUB.B).</p>
<h3>WHAT WE ARE</h3>
<p>Headquartered in Manchester, New Hampshire, BURNDY® has 2 US manufacturing sites and also one in Mexico. Exceptional customer service, the industry’s highest quality products, and continuous innovation are things that BURNDY® prides itself on providing. We have a wide range of tooling for all your connector needs and an electrical connector to meet every one of your power needs. With superior manufacturing technology, BURNDY® can create and build any custom connector for our valued customer with our advanced engineering support teams. With over 95 years of dedicated customer support in both service and product, our customers need to look no further than BURNDY® for all their connector needs.</p>
<h3>WHAT WE DO</h3>
<p>BURNDY® today is a global organization known for their pioneering work in the manufacture of connectors, fittings, and tools for electrical utilities, commercial, industrial, and residential contractors, maintenance and repair companies, as well as the telecommunication and renewable energies market.</p>
<h3>MISSION STATEMENT <a href="https://hubbellcdn.com/ohwassets/HCE/burndy/about-us/mission-statement-2019.pdf" target="_blank">[PDF]</a></h3>
<p>We at BURNDY® are committed to TOTAL CUSTOMER SATISFACTION by developing, and continuously improving, QUALITY PROCESSES that are implemented by QUALITY PEOPLE. <br/>
TOTAL CUSTOMER SATISFACTION means meeting the requirements of our customers and co-workers by delivering error-free products and services on a timely basis.  Our goal is for BURNDY® to provide the highest value to our customers. We intend to be the most competitive company in the electrical connector industry and for each and every employee to strive for continuous quality improvement in his or her job.</p>
