what types of cookies do we use?
a: there are a number of different types of cookies. our website uses the following general categories of cookies: functionality/necessary cookies, analytical cookies, marketing/advertising cookies.
functionality/necessary cookies—these cookies are used to make a website function properly. our necessary cookies are not used for tracking. the most common necessary action performed using a cookie is user authentication. when you log in to a website, a cookie is stored in your browser including some piece of data used by the web to remember who is authenticated when you go to another internal page or refresh the site.
analytical cookies—these cookies are not necessary but are used to collect valuable information on how a website is being used, to attempt to identify issues and to determine what could be improved on the site and what content is most useful. the information collected is, in most cases, anonymous, but some analytics services collect information that can be used to identify the user.
marketing/advertising cookies—these cookies are used to track visitors across websites to profile user behavior and allow publishers to display relevant advertisements. we use these cookies so that we recognize you on our website and remember your previously selected preferences. examples of these could include what language you prefer and what geographical location from which you are viewing our sites. a mix of first-party and third-party cookies may be used.
additional information on cookies used for marketing purposes—we would like to send you information about products and services of ours that we think you might benefit from. if you have agreed to receive marketing, you may always opt out at any time you choose. you have the right at any time to stop us from contacting you for marketing purposes.
how to manage cookies—us users may choose to reject cookies either outright, or on a case-by-case basis by using the settings in your internet browser. you can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. however, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result. eea users will be provided the option of managing cookies via the pop-up message displayed at the bottom of a user’s screen upon visiting our sites.
privacy policies of other websites—our sites contains links to other websites. our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.
