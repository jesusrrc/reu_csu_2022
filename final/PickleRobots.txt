introduction
pickle robot company (“we,” or the “company”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. “personal information” includes any information that can or does identify an individual or can be directly linked to an individual. this privacy policy, effective as of the date above, puts forth our current privacy practices regarding how we collect, use, process, maintain, and share your personal information when you visit and interact with www.picklerobot.com (“website”). it also provides you with how to request to have your personal information deleted.
by accessing our website, you acknowledge this privacy policy and freely consent to the information collection and use practices as described. if you do not agree with our policies and practices, your choice is to not use the website. our privacy policy may change from time to time, any previous versions, will be made available to you on our website. your continued use of this website after we make changes to the privacy policy constitutes your acceptance of those changes. it is your responsibility to check the privacy policy periodically for updates.
information we collect
when you visit our website, some information is collected automatically. information that we collect automatically includes your browsing and click actions, ip address, device information, operating system, screen resolution, and information about your internet connection.
when you use the website to contact us, or to request information about our offerings, you may share your name, job title, phone number, and email address, as well as your employer’s or company’s name, a description of your business, and/or a message. we keep records of these messages. please do not include sensitive data in any message submitted on our website contact form (whether sensitive personal data like social security, bank account, or credit card numbers, or confidential or proprietary information).
if you decide to purchase our products or services, or to pay us a deposit on a future purchase, we may ask that you provide us with payment information, including a credit or debit card number, expiration date, cvv code, bank account information, and/or billing address.
you aren’t required to share any information to access or use our website, but if you do, we use that information to contact you and, if appropriate, to process payment for products, services, or deposits.
how we use this information
we use the information we collect about you when you visit our website to optimize and improve your experience and the website’s functionality. we also use that information for remarketing purposes, which allows third party websites to host and display advertisements for our products and services when you visit those websites.
we provide web-based submission forms so you can contact us. if you voluntarily disclose your email address or other contact information, we may contact you via email or by other methods in the future to inform you about our products or services.
we may also ask that you provide payment information so that we can process payments you send to us. when our customers or potential customers pay for, or place deposits for, our products or services, we use that information, including quantities ordered and geographical information, to gauge interest in our offerings and plan our current and future operations.
how we collect this information
as you navigate and interact with our website, we use third-party analytics platforms including google analytics (see below for more information on google analytics), clicky analytics, and hubspot to collect information about your activity on our website.
we also collect personal information about you through our web-based submission form that you provide voluntarily, and where applicable, through payment details that you may provide voluntarily.
google analytics
we use a tool called “google analytics” to collect information about the use of our site. google analytics collects information such as how often users visit this website, what pages they visit, and what other websites they used prior to coming to our website. google analytics collects the ip address assigned to you on the date you visit this site, rather than your name or other identifying information. we do not combine the information collected through the use of google analytics with other personal information. although google analytics plants a permanent cookie on your web browser to identify you as a unique user the next time you visit the website, the cookie cannot be used by anyone but google. google’s ability to use and share information collected by google analytics about your visits to this site is restricted by the google analytics terms of use and the google privacy policy. you can prevent google analytics from recognizing you on return visits to this site by disabling cookies on your browser.
cookies, remarketing, and targeted advertising
we and third parties who provide services to us use cookies to recognize you as you use or return to the website. we do this to enhance your experience of the website and to facilitate our remarketing activities. we use remarketing to advertise online using a range of technologies, which may include web beacons, pixels, ad tags, cookies, and mobile identifiers. our use of remarketing allows us, through our third party providers, to display our advertisements when you visit certain third party websites.
we may also use cookies and partner with third parties who may serve customized ads on third party websites based on your behavior on the website, using information such as ip addresses, unique device identifiers, browser type, operating system, time zone, country, referring pages, information gathered through the use of cookies, web server logs, and pixel tags.
