CATEGORIES OF PERSONAL DATA PROCESSED BY TRACKI.


Personal data that is processed when you create a Tracki account:


When you create a Tracki account, we ask you to provide your email address and name. You can choose to provide only your first name or a nickname instead of your full name if you wish.


PURPOSES AND LEGAL GROUND 1:


(a) Tracki processes your email address because you use your email address and your password to sign in to your account. The legal ground for processing your email address for this purpose is based on Trackis legitimate interest in protecting the security of your account.


(b) Tracki also processes your email address for the purpose of sending you important information about your Tracki products, apps or account, such as important safety information or material changes to this Privacy Policy. The name you provide is associated with your account profile, and is displayed when you submit comments or other material on a Tracki website or app. The legal ground for processing your email address and name for these purposes is Trackis legitimate interest in providing you important safety or other information about your Tracki products, apps or account or material changes to this Privacy Policy, and in providing you an opportunity to engage with other Tracki users.


(c) If you provide your opt-in consent to receiving marketing information from Tracki, Tracki will also process your email address for the purpose of sending you marketing information about Tracki products and apps. The legal ground for processing your email address for this purpose is your consent. You may withdraw your consent at any time by changing your preferences in your Tracki account or through the unsubscribe link at the bottom of our marketing emails. The marketing emails you receive from Tracki are based on the preferences you provide in your Tracki account, the locale indicated by your Internet Protocol (IP) address, the types of Tracki devices you have added to your Tracki account, and any subscriptions included in your Tracki account. The legal ground for processing this data for this purpose is Trackis legitimate interest in reducing the number of marketing emails sent to each particular customer by selecting which customers receive a particular marketing email rather than sending every marketing email to every customer who has consented to receiving marketing emails.


(d) Tracki also processes your email address to associate it with your Tracki account when you interact with our customer support representatives. The legal ground for this processing is our legitimate interest in providing quality customer support.

CATEGORIES OF RECIPIENTS OF PERSONAL DATA.


Your Tracki-friends invitees:


Tracki-friends is a feature available on some Tracki devices that enables you to send a link to people of your choice that allows them to see the real-time location of your device. Because anyone with access to the link will be able to see the real-time location of your Tracki device, you should use caution in determining to whom you want to send the link and be sure that you trust them to not send the link to others whom you do not want to be able to view the location of your Tracki device.


OTHER SERVICE PROVIDERS:


Tracki uses cloud services from third parties to assist in sending emails. Those services track the activities associated with these emails, such as whether they were opened, whether links in the emails were clicked on, and whether purchases were made following clicks on those links. Tracki uses this data to analyze the level of engagement with its emails. Tracki uses third-party service providers to ship purchased products to customers and to help us better understand our customers needs.
